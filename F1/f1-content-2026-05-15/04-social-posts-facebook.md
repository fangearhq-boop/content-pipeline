#### Long-Form Post — 12:00 PM ET

Mercedes is bringing a significant upgrade package. McLaren is bringing the second part of their Miami updates. His teammate is on a three-race win streak. The moment Russell falls further behind at a circuit he traditionally dominates, the title conversation becomes very uncomfortable for him.

For Antonelli, a fourth consecutive win would be utterly historic. For Norris and McLaren, a strong performance could insert them directly into the title picture.
