# F1 Fanrecap — Research Playbook

How to research, tier, and fact-check Formula 1 stories for daily content.

## Research Categories

Run **8-10 web searches** across these categories before writing content.

| # | Category | Example Search Queries |
|---|----------|----------------------|
| 1 | Race Results & Qualifying | "F1 race results [date]", "F1 qualifying results [GP name]", "F1 sprint results", "F1 official classification" |
| 2 | Championship Standings | "F1 drivers standings 2026", "F1 constructors standings", "F1 championship points", "F1 points table" |
| 3 | Technical & Strategy | "F1 [GP name] strategy analysis", "F1 tire strategy", "F1 car upgrades [team]", "F1 penalty stewards decision", "F1 technical directive" |
| 4 | Driver & Team News | "F1 driver contract", "F1 team principal news", "F1 driver transfer", "F1 team orders controversy", "F1 driver retirement" |
| 5 | Regulations & FIA | "F1 2026 regulations", "F1 rule change", "F1 budget cap", "FIA technical directive", "F1 cost cap penalty" |
| 6 | Injury & Substitutions | "F1 driver injury", "F1 reserve driver", "F1 driver replacement", "[driver] medical update", "F1 race ban" |
| 7 | Practice & Testing | "F1 FP1 results [GP name]", "F1 practice times", "F1 pre-season testing", "F1 car reliability", "F1 new parts upgrades" |
| 8 | Preview & Lookahead | "F1 next race 2026", "F1 [GP name] circuit guide", "F1 weather forecast [city]", "F1 race start time ET" |

## Story Tiering

### Tier 1 — Lead Stories (top time slots)

Must-cover content that anchors the day. Gets the 8:00 AM or 10:00 AM slot.

| Story Type | Why Tier 1 |
|-----------|-----------|
| Race results (any Grand Prix) | Every race is a marquee event — only 24 per season |
| Championship-deciding moments | Title clinch, mathematical elimination, or massive points swing |
| Major crashes and red flags | Safety incidents are always the top story — report facts, not speculation |
| Driver transfers between teams | Reshapes the competitive order, dominates the news cycle for days |
| Major regulation announcements | 2026 regs especially — biggest technical shakeup in years |
| Qualifying (pole position) | Sets the grid, especially dramatic in rain or at street circuits |
| Last-lap overtakes and photo finishes | The most shareable moments in motorsport |

### Tier 2 — Developing Stories (mid-day slots)

Strong content that fills the 12:00 PM or 2:00 PM slots.

| Story Type | Why Tier 2 |
|-----------|-----------|
| Qualifying results (full grid) | Grid order with analysis, especially if surprise results |
| Strategy analysis | Tire calls, pit stop windows, undercuts that decided the race |
| Team updates and upgrades | New parts, aero packages, power unit penalties |
| Penalties and stewards' decisions | 5-second penalties, grid drops, post-race investigations |
| Sprint race results | Shorter races but still award championship points |
| Contract and silly season news | Credible reports from established journalists, not rumor mills |
| Championship standings movement | Points gap changes, constructors' battle shifts |

### Tier 3 — Supporting Content (later slots)

Fills out the schedule. Gets the 5:00 PM or 8:00 PM slot.

| Story Type | Why Tier 3 |
|-----------|-----------|
| Next race preview and circuit guide | Lookahead at the upcoming Grand Prix weekend |
| Practice session times (FP1/FP2/FP3) | Practice pace is interesting but unreliable for predictions |
| Evergreen analysis | "Best overtakes of the season so far" type features |
| Historical comparisons | "How 2026 compares to the last major reg change" type content |
| Power rankings and form guides | Weekly or mid-season team/driver rankings |
| Pre-season testing analysis | Pace data from testing — interesting but misleading (sandbagging) |

## Posting Priority

This order is non-negotiable:

1. **Race/qualifying results** — always first. A race recap hours after the next session starts is useless. Many races finish early morning ET — the 8:00 AM slot is critical.
2. **Today's session previews** — if practice, qualifying, or a race is happening today, preview it before it starts.
3. **Breaking news** — driver transfers, major crashes, penalty decisions, regulation changes.
4. **Everything else** — analysis, evergreen, previews for future weekends.

If multiple race results exist (sprint + qualifying on the same day), order by importance: race > qualifying > sprint > practice.

## Fact-Check Requirements

Every post must be verified before publishing. Priority order:

### Priority 1 — Race Times, Dates, Circuits

| Check | How to Verify |
|-------|--------------|
| Race start time | Verify against formula1.com schedule — always state ET with local time |
| Day of week | Cross-reference the calendar — "Sunday's race" must actually be Sunday |
| Circuit name | Use official FIA name (e.g., "Circuit de Monaco" not "Monaco street circuit") |
| Race weekend format | Confirm if it's a sprint weekend or standard weekend |
| Session times | Verify FP1/FP2/FP3/Qualifying/Race times — they vary by circuit and timezone |

### Priority 2 — Race Results, Standings, Championship Points

| Check | How to Verify |
|-------|--------------|
| Finishing order | Cross-reference FIA official classification AND formula1.com results |
| Fastest lap | Verify who set it and on which lap — affects bonus point |
| Championship standings | Verify against formula1.com/standings — standings change after every race |
| Points scored | Verify points allocation (25-18-15-12-10-8-6-4-2-1 for race, 8-7-6-5-4-3-2-1 for sprint) |
| DNF/DNS reasons | Verify retirement cause from official team statements, not live commentary speculation |
| Penalties applied | Check FIA stewards' documents for exact penalty and reasoning |

### Priority 3 — Lap Times, Driver Stats, Contract Values

| Check | How to Verify |
|-------|--------------|
| Qualifying lap times | formula1.com official timing — times to three decimal places (e.g., 1:28.432) |
| Gap to pole | Calculate from official times, don't trust secondary sources |
| Career stats | StatsF1.com for wins, poles, podiums, points — cross-reference formula1.com |
| Contract values | Credible journalism sources only (Autosport, The Athletic) — F1 contracts are not publicly filed |
| Speed trap / top speeds | formula1.com timing data — available during sessions |
| Pit stop times | FIA official timing — verify to hundredths of a second |

### Priority 4 — Schedule and Logistics

| Check | How to Verify |
|-------|--------------|
| Race start times | formula1.com schedule — convert to ET, note local time |
| Day of week | Cross-reference the calendar — especially tricky for overseas races |
| Weather forecast | Multiple weather sources for race-day conditions — never rely on one source |
| TV/streaming info | F1TV, ESPN (US), Sky Sports (UK) — broadcast details vary by market |
| All times in ET | Convert from local race time — many European races are morning ET, Asian races are overnight ET |

## Market Bias Check

Before finalizing any day's content, verify coverage breadth:

- Do not default to Red Bull/Ferrari/Mercedes/McLaren every day just because they lead the championship
- Midfield battles (Aston Martin, Alpine, Williams, Haas, RB, Sauber/Audi) produce compelling stories too
- If 4+ posts feature the same team or driver, look harder at the rest of the grid
- Midfield overtakes and points finishes are always worth covering
- Balance coverage of the drivers' AND constructors' championships
- Rookie drivers and new team pairings deserve spotlight content

## Key Dates Reminder

- **Car Launches**: January – February (teams reveal new cars individually)
- **Pre-Season Testing**: Late February (single test, ~3 days)
- **Season Opener**: Mid-March
- **Monaco Grand Prix**: Late May (always Tier 1)
- **British Grand Prix (Silverstone)**: Early July (always Tier 1)
- **Summer Break**: August (~2 weeks mandatory factory shutdown)
- **Italian Grand Prix (Monza)**: Early September (always Tier 1)
- **Abu Dhabi Finale**: Late November / December (always Tier 1)
- **Sprint Weekends**: ~6 per season (exact rounds announced by FIA)
- **FIA Prize Giving**: December
