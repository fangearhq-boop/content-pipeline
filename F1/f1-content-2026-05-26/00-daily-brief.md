### STORY 1: Antonelli Wins Canadian Grand Prix — Russell Retires from Lead on Lap 30
- **Tier:** 1
- **Category:** Race Result / Canadian Grand Prix
- **Why it matters:** Kimi Antonelli converted pole position into his fourth consecutive grand prix victory at the 2026 Canadian Grand Prix (May 24, Circuit Gilles-Villeneuve, Montreal).
