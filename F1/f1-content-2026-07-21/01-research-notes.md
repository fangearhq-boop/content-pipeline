- Piastri suspects any contact between Verstappen's team and McLaren has been Verstappen "exploring options" (Source: ESPN)

- A seat-swap scenario (Verstappen to McLaren, Piastri to Red Bull) was widely circulated in paddock rumours weeks before denials
- Verstappen's exit clause allows him to leave Red Bull between August and October if outside top 2 at specific performance milestones
