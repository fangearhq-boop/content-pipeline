### STORY 1: Antonelli Wins Monaco — Fifth Win of 2026
- **Tier:** 1
- **Tag:** FOLLOW UP (ongoing championship; race-day coverage)
- **Why this matters:** Monaco pole-to-win is the cleanest narrative in F1. Antonelli converting another pole to a win — his 5th of the season — puts him in a completely different conversation. He started the season as the youngest Mercedes race driver, and he is now firmly in the Lewis Hamilton-era discussions about early-career dominance. The championship gap extending further makes this a season-defining weekend.
- **Best angle:** "Can't be overtaken at Monaco" + Antonelli's maturity + championship impact.
- **Social hook:** "Antonelli. Pole. Win. Five victories in 2026. Monaco is officially his."
- **Key facts to use:** P1 from pole; 5th win; championship lead extension; 9am ET Apple TV+.

### STORY 2: Verstappen P2 / Exit Clause
- **Tier:** 1
- **Tag:** FOLLOW UP
- **Why this matters:** Verstappen P2 at Monaco is excellent by Red Bull's 2026 standards, but the clock ticking on his July 27 exit clause means every race is existential. He's had back-to-back podiums (Canada P3, Monaco P2) but is still outside the top-2 in the championship. He needs Russell to fall and Antonelli to fail multiple times to get there in time.
- **Best angle:** The exit clause math — two great races, still not enough; what needs to happen for him to trigger (or not trigger) the clause.
- **Social hook:** "Verstappen just had back-to-back podiums. He's still not safe. The July 27 deadline is real."
- **Key facts to use:** Exit clause (top-2 by July 27); Canada P3; Monaco P2; still outside top-2 in standings; Red Bull has been 4th-fastest team.

### STORY 3: Hamilton P3 Monaco
- **Tier:** 2
- **Tag:** FOLLOW UP
- **Why this matters:** Hamilton is the most famous Formula 1 driver alive, and Monaco is his most celebrated venue (three wins). His P3 with Ferrari — first season with the team — is an emotional milestone that bridges the Mercedes era and this new chapter. He wasn't on pole, he wasn't first. But he was on the podium at the most glamorous race on the calendar.
- **Best angle:** Legacy and new beginnings — his Monaco history with Mercedes vs. this first Ferrari Monaco podium.
- **Social hook:** "Hamilton has won Monaco three times. Today he stood on the podium for the first time doing it in red."
- **Key facts to use:** 3 Monaco wins (2008, 2016, 2019); first Ferrari Monaco podium; P2 Canada; FP2 fastest in practice.

### STORY 4: Leclerc Home Race Heartbreak
- **Tier:** 2
- **Tag:** FOLLOW UP
- **Why this matters:** Leclerc lives in Monaco. He won here in 2024. He was the pre-race favorite (+185). He was fastest in practice. And he finished P4. The Monegasque driver failing to win his home race — especially in a year when Ferrari expected to contend — is a compelling heartbreak story.
- **Best angle:** The brutal reality of Monaco: at this circuit, the race is often decided in qualifying. Leclerc was P4 on the grid; he was going to be P4 at the flag.
- **Social hook:** "Leclerc lives in Monaco. He was the favorite. He was P4 in qualifying. He was P4 at the flag. That's the brutal truth of this circuit."
- **Key facts to use:** Lives in Monaco; 2024 winner; pre-race +185 favorite; FP1 fastest; P4 qualifying; P4 race.

### STORY 5: Championship Standings After Monaco
- **Tier:** 2
- **Tag:** FOLLOW UP
- **Why this matters:** Monaco reshapes the standings significantly. Antonelli extends his lead; Russell loses ground further after another below-expectation result; Verstappen climbs. The math toward the summer break becomes clearer with each race.
- **Best angle:** The gap between Antonelli and the rest is growing to a point where it becomes difficult to reverse. The storyline is no longer "who will win it" but "can anyone stop Antonelli."
- **Social hook:** "After Monaco: Antonelli has won 5 of 8 races. Can anyone stop him?"
- **Key facts to use:** Championship standings; Antonelli's lead extension; Russell P6 at Monaco; Verstappen climbing.
