**Long-Form Post**

Now his rivals are responding.

Lando Norris was asked about a potential Verstappen exit from Formula 1 ahead of Miami. His answer was both generous and candid: "It would be a shame and a miss for the sport," Norris said. "He probably is one of the best drivers you'll ever see in Formula 1."

He reportedly has an exit clause that activates if he's not in the top two of the championship by summer — and at ninth, he is nowhere near that threshold.
