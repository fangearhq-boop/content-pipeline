# F1 Fanrecap — Image Concepts
**Date:** June 28, 2026
**Photo Source:** imagn
**Search Rule:** Driver/person name + team ONLY — no years, no events, no track names

---

### STORY 1: Austrian GP Race Day — Russell on Pole as Verstappen Fights Back from Grid Drop

**Primary Search:** George Russell Mercedes
**Secondary Search:** Kimi Antonelli Mercedes
**Backup Search:** Lewis Hamilton Ferrari

**Preferred shot type:** Russell in cockpit or on the pit wall — a shot that conveys focus and readiness before a race start. Clean, bright lighting preferred. Helmet on.

**Avoid:** Any shot that shows a crash, car damage, or pit lane chaos. Avoid group celebration shots that include post-race elements.

---

### STORY 2: Verstappen's Austrian GP Nightmare — Crash in Qualifying, Exit Clock Ticking

**Primary Search:** Max Verstappen Red Bull
**Secondary Search:** Max Verstappen helmet
**Backup Search:** Max Verstappen cockpit

**Preferred shot type:** Close-up of Verstappen in the cockpit or helmet-on standing beside the car — conveys intensity and isolation. Slightly tighter crop works well for the gravity of this story.

**Avoid:** Images showing Verstappen celebrating, trophy shots, or champagne. Avoid any Red Bull team celebration imagery.

---

### STORY 3: Antonelli vs Hamilton — Championship Math at the Austrian GP

**Primary Search:** Kimi Antonelli Mercedes
**Secondary Search:** Lewis Hamilton Ferrari
**Backup Search:** Lewis Hamilton helmet

**Preferred shot type:** Side-by-side if available; otherwise Antonelli on his own in Mercedes livery — championship-leader framing. Alternatively, Hamilton in Ferrari red for the "challenger" framing. Both work well for this rivalry piece.

**Avoid:** Any imagery showing the two drivers in contact or racing side-by-side (could imply an incident). Avoid McLaren or Red Bull liveries in the background.

---

### STORY 4: Cadillac's Austrian GP Upgrade — Zero Points, Final Gamble

**Primary Search:** Sergio Perez Cadillac
**Secondary Search:** Valtteri Bottas Cadillac
**Backup Search:** Gene Haas Cadillac

**Preferred shot type:** Car on track — the Cadillac livery is distinctive and a clean on-track shot communicates the "upgrade" angle well. Alternatively, a pit lane or garage shot that suggests mechanical work and preparation.

**Avoid:** Any crash imagery or shots showing smoke, fire, or car damage (given the FP2 Bottas cockpit fire). Avoid images with other team liveries prominently featured.

---

### STORY 5: F1 Silly Season 2026 — 15 Drivers Without 2027 Seats

**Primary Search:** Max Verstappen Red Bull
**Secondary Search:** Fernando Alonso Aston Martin
**Backup Search:** Carlos Sainz Williams

**Preferred shot type:** Verstappen out of the car or in the paddock — a pensive or walking-away shot works better than an in-cockpit shot for this story's "what next?" framing. The story is about the future, not the present race.

**Avoid:** Any shot of signed contracts, trophies, or celebration imagery. Avoid images that show drivers clearly alongside their current cars in a promotional/committed way — this story is about uncertainty.
