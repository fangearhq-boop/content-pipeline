### STORY 2: Monaco Strategy Shake-Up: FIA Scraps Mandatory 2-Stop Rule for 2026
- **Tier:** 1
- **Category:** Race Strategy / Regulation Change
- **Why it matters:** The FIA has dropped the mandatory minimum two-stop rule that was controversially introduced at the 2025 Monaco Grand Prix. For 2026, Monaco reverts to free-strategy racing. The 2025 rule was criticized for allowing teams to exploit "backing up" tactics (one car slowing traffic to create a pit window for the other). The 2026 Monaco GP is June 5-7, just 9 days away. This strategic reset changes the race calculus for all teams and could produce an entirely different race dynamic.
- **Angle:** Monaco in 2026 will look very different tactically from 2025 — teams are now planning around open strategy rather than mandatory stops.
