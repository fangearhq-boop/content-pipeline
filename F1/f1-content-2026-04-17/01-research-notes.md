### STORY 1: FIA April 20 Energy Management Vote

#### Key Facts
- **Meeting date:** April 20, 2026 (three days from now)
- **Format:** Full F1 Commission style meeting — team principals, FIA, F1 management, plus additional reps from all six 2026 power unit manufacturers
- **E-vote:** Expected a few days after the meeting; changes ratified by FIA World Motor Sport Council
- **Target:** Changes implemented by Miami GP (May 1-3)
- **Key issues being addressed:**
  1. Super clipping: Raising limit from 250kW to 350kW
  2. Qualifying energy recovery: Current limit is 8MJ (Suzuka reduced to this from prior standard); proposals target 6MJ or lower
  3. ADUO (Automatic Drag Reduction Override Unit) refinements
  4. Safety: Oliver Bearman's crash in Japan due to speed differentials when cars ran out of energy
- **Prior meetings:** April 15 (sporting regs), April 16 (technical follow-up), April 20 (vote)
- **FIA statement:** "Constructive dialogue" confirmed; "commitment to making tweaks to some aspects of regulations"
