### STORY 1: FIA April 20 Vote — Critical Energy Management Decision for Miami (Tier 1)
The FIA is holding a high-level F1 Commission meeting on April 20 — three days from now — where team principals, power unit manufacturers, FIA and F1 management will vote on energy management tweaks for the 2026 regulations. Topics include super clipping reform (increasing from 250kW to 350kW), reducing qualifying energy recovery (current 8MJ target, possibly cut to 6MJ), ADUO refinements, and safety measures after Oliver Bearman's crash in Japan due to speed differentials. An e-vote result is expected days after the meeting, with changes implemented for Miami (May 1-3).
