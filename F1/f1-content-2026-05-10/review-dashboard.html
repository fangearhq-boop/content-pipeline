### Long-Form Post Kimi Antonelli just keeps winning — and the drama around him keeps getting wilder. At the 2026 Miami Grand Prix on May 3, the 19-year-old Mercedes driver converted his third consecutive pole position into his third consecutive race victory, crossing the line 3.264 seconds ahead of Lando Norris in a McLaren that had genuinely closed the gap. Oscar Piastri completed the podium in third for a McLaren 2-3. George Russell was fourth for Mercedes, Max Verstappen fifth for Red Bull, and Lewis Hamilton sixth for Ferrari. But the story everyone was talking about was Charles Leclerc.
