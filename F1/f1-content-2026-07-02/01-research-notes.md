### STORY 5: Silverstone Battery Crisis

**Key Facts:**
- 2026 regs removed MGU-H; cars rely solely on MGU-K (kinetic braking recovery)
- Silverstone layout: between Turn 7 (Luffield) and Turn 15 (Stowe) = ~35-40 seconds of sweeping high-speed corners with NO heavy braking zones
- By Hangar Straight, the 350kW electrical system drained; cars suffer severe power derate before Stowe braking
- Both Hamilton AND Verstappen independently flagged this issue
- Hamilton quote: "There's lots of straights in Silverstone, lots of straights and lots of deployment, and not many places to recover the power"
- Verstappen quote: "I did a few laps on the simulator, I just started laughing. It felt like a different track"
- Teams with better energy management software (Mercedes likely favored) may gain edge
- Expected to produce visible speed differentials and potentially chaotic racing
- Sources: Sportsnaut, PlanetF1, MotorBiscuit

- 2026 MGU-H removal (HIGH — F1 regulations fact)
