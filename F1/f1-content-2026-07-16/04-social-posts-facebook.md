Under the new regulations, Spa is a uniquely energy-intensive circuit. The long flat-out sections through Eau Rouge, Raidillon, and the Kemmel Straight drain battery systems faster than almost anywhere else on the calendar — and energy management has been one of Red Bull's persistent weaknesses this season.

Red Bull's engineers have been working on fixes, but the underlying challenge at Spa — where the circuit demands both raw power and flawless energy deployment — remains.
