### STORY 1:
**Kendall Wells HR No. 36 / No. 1 Oklahoma Sweeps Georgia**
Wells hit her 36th home run of the season Sunday in OU's 3-1 win over No. 11/12 Georgia at Love's Field, clinching the series sweep. She now sits one home run away from tying Lauren Espinoza's all-time NCAA single-season record of 37 (Arizona, 1995). OU is now 45-6, 17-3 SEC. Miali Guachino earned the win (13-1).
