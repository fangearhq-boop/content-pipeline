### STORY 1: Kendall Wells Breaks NCAA Freshman HR Record With No. 31

**Freshness Tags:** BREAKING, RECORD, HISTORIC

**Summary:**
Kendall Wells hit HR No. 31 Saturday night, becoming the NCAA all-time leader in freshman single-season home runs. The three-run shot came in the fifth inning of Oklahoma's 4-3 win over Texas in Game 2 of the Red River Showdown. Wells surpassed the previous record of 30, shared by Lauren Chamberlain, Janie Reed Espinoza, and Dejah Mulipola. She is still chasing the all-time NCAA single-season record of 37 (Espinoza, 1995) — 6 more to go.
