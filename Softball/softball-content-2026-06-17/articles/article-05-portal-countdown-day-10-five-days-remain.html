Transfer Portal Countdown — Day 10 of 15, Five Days Remain

By Emily Rawlings | I Love Softball | June 17, 2026

Tuesday evening changed the shape of the 2026 transfer portal.

Isa Torres committed to Texas. The D1 Softball Player of the Year, the most watched uncommitted transfer in the portal, ended a 10-day wait with a burnt orange Instagram post and two words: "I'm coming home." The biggest domino has fallen.

Now we are at Day 10 of the 15-day window. The portal closes June 22. Five days remain.

The Committed Column

Here is where the major moves have landed as of June 17:

Texas Longhorns: Isa Torres (SS/FSU — D1 POY, .530 BA), Samantha Swan (C/Arizona State — No. 1 portal catcher, .383 BA / 12 HR), Samantha Lincoln (P/Texas Tech), Maya Altmeyer.

Texas Tech Red Raiders: Moriah Polar (OF/Purdue — .555 BA, 101 hits), Jayden Heavener (P/LSU — No. 1 portal pitcher, 2.81 ERA / 128 K).

Other Power programs: Madison Hartley to Oklahoma State, Katie Wetteland to Arkansas, Maddy Azua to Texas A&M (Sun Belt POY, 1.95 ERA), Samantha Plumlee to Alabama.

Still to Be Determined

Expected/DNT: Kasidi Pickering (.370 BA / 20 HR / 61 RBI from OU) entered the portal with a "Do Not Contact" tag. All reporting points to Texas Tech. No official announcement yet.

Still uncommitted: Jaysoni Beachum (3B/FSU — .417 BA / 16 HR / 66 RBI / .549 OBP / career 37 HR, 180 RBI). Ranked No. 1 overall transfer by Softball America. The last elite name in the pool. Her announcement will be the defining final chapter of this portal cycle.

The Big Picture

The 2026 portal cycle has been dominated by two programs. Texas absorbed the D1 POY, the No. 1 catcher, and a pitcher from its WCWS championship opponent. Texas Tech absorbed the nation's top hitter and No. 1 pitcher, and is expected to add the No. 2 overall transfer.

Every other program — A&M, Alabama, OSU, Arkansas — has added meaningful pieces in the middle tier. But the top of the market has been claimed by two programs that will likely meet again in Oklahoma City next May.

The final five days belong to Beachum — and whatever program earns her signature.

What's Next

The portal window closes June 22 at 11:59 PM. Kasidi Pickering's official announcement for Texas Tech is expected at any time. Jaysoni Beachum's commitment is the last major piece of news the portal can produce.

After June 22, the window closes and programs shift to fall practice preparation. The rosters are being built right now, in the final five days, for a 2027 season that is already shaping up to be one of the most competitive in recent memory.

Day 10. Five days left. The final chapter is being written.
