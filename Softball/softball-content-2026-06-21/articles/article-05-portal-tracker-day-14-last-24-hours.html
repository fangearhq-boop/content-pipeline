Transfer Portal Tracker — Day 14 of 15: The Final 24 Hours

By Emily Rawlings | I Love Softball | June 21, 2026

One day left.

The 2026 NCAA softball transfer portal opened June 8. It closes Monday, June 22. Today is Day 14 of 15, and the final 24 hours have arrived. Here's the complete state of the portal heading into the last stretch.

By the Numbers

Roughly 120 players entered the 2026 transfer portal. As of June 21, more than 84 have committed to new programs — a commitment rate of approximately 70 percent. Programs have 24 hours to finalize any remaining moves before the window closes permanently.

Final-stretch commitments in the last 48 hours include: Gold Glove outfielder Bella Foran to South Carolina (June 20), pitcher KG Favors (June 20), pitcher Delainey Everett (June 19), and Creighton left fielder Zoey Greenwood to Texas State (.328/.384/.460 / 37 RBI / 24 stolen bases in 2026).

The Last Name on the Board

One significant name remains uncommitted: Jaysoni Beachum.

The Softball America No. 1 transfer and Florida State's former third baseman is carrying a .417 batting average, 16 home runs, 66 RBI, and a .549 on-base percentage from her 2026 season into a single-day decision window. She has visited the Florida Gators. Programs still pursuing her have hours, not days, to make their final case.

Whatever Beachum decides will be the final headline of the 2026 portal. The rest of the class is set. One name remains.

What's Next

The portal closes Monday, June 22. After the window shuts, attention shifts to the fall — practice reports, depth charts, and the first 2027 preseason rankings. The portal reshapes every offseason.
