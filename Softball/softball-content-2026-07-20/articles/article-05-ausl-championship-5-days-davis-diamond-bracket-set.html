Five Days to Davis Diamond. The Bracket Is Set. Here Is What You Need to Know.

By Sarah Mitchell | I Love Softball | July 20, 2026

The 2026 AUSL regular season is over. The postseason bracket is official. Five days from now, the first-ever AUSL Play-in Game will be played at Davis Diamond in College Station, Texas. Five days after that, a professional softball game will air on ABC for the first time in U.S. history.

Here is everything you need to know.

The Bracket

Three teams made the postseason. The Utah Talons earned the No. 1 seed and a direct bye to the AUSL Championship Series. They will rest and wait while the other two teams fight for the right to face them.

The No. 2 Chicago Bandits and the No. 3 Oklahoma City Spark meet in the Play-in Game on Thursday, July 23 at 9 PM ET on ESPN at Davis Diamond. Single elimination. One team advances. The winner plays the Talons in the best-of-three Championship Series beginning July 25.

The Portland Cascade, who appeared to hold the third playoff spot entering Monday, were eliminated when the Spark defeated the Carolina Blaze in the final game of the regular season.

The Venue

Davis Diamond sits on the Texas A&M campus in College Station, Texas. It is one of the finest softball venues in the country — a purpose-built, premier facility at one of the sport's flagship university programs. The Play-in Game on July 23 and all three potential Championship Series games (July 25, 26, and 27) will be played there.

College Station becomes the center of professional softball for the next week.

The Broadcast

Game 1 of the AUSL Championship Series on July 25 airs on ABC.

This has not happened before. A professional softball game has never been broadcast on U.S. network television. The ABC broadcast means the largest audience professional softball has ever reached will be watching on a Saturday afternoon in late July.

The AUSL's 2026 broadcast schedule has included more than 90 games across ESPN, ESPN2, ESPNU, ESPN Deportes, CBS Sports Network, and MLB Network. The ABC broadcast is the capstone: the moment when the audience for professional softball expands beyond the dedicated fans who have followed the league all season to include the casual viewer who simply turns on ABC on a Saturday and finds softball waiting for them.

The Utah Talons

The Talons earned the No. 1 seed by being the league's best team all season. They hold the head-to-head tiebreaker over the Bandits by virtue of sweeping their earlier regular-season series. Montana Fouts leads the pitching staff. The Talons did not just back into this bracket position — they have been the standard-setter for the entire AUSL regular season.

They will watch Thursday's play-in game, warm up, and be ready to play July 25 on ABC against whoever survives.

What's Next

AUSL Play-in Game: Thursday, July 23, 9 PM ET on ESPN at Davis Diamond, College Station, Texas. Chicago Bandits vs. Oklahoma City Spark, single elimination. AUSL Championship Series: July 25-27, Davis Diamond. Game 1 on ABC, Saturday July 25. The first professional softball game ever on U.S. network television. Five days away.
