# I Love Softball — Research Playbook

How to research, tier, and fact-check NCAA softball stories for daily content.

## Research Categories

Run **8-10 web searches** across these categories before writing content.

| # | Category | Example Search Queries |
|---|----------|----------------------|
| 1 | Scores & Results | "NCAA softball scores today", "college softball results [date]", "[team] softball score" |
| 2 | Rankings & Polls | "NFCA softball poll", "USA Softball top 25", "D1Softball power rankings" |
| 3 | Upsets & Surprises | "college softball upset", "unranked beats ranked softball", "[conference] softball surprises" |
| 4 | Individual Performances | "NCAA softball home run leaders", "college softball no-hitter", "softball player of the week [conference]" |
| 5 | Conference Play | "[conference] softball standings", "SEC softball results this week", "Big 12 softball series" |
| 6 | Coaching & Transfers | "NCAA softball transfer portal", "softball coaching change", "softball portal commitments [year]" |
| 7 | Injury & Roster | "[team] softball injury", "softball starter out", "[pitcher] injury update" |
| 8 | Preview & Lookahead | "college softball schedule this week", "softball ranked matchups this weekend", "[team] softball preview" |

## Story Tiering

### Tier 1 — Lead Stories (top time slots)

Must-cover content that anchors the day. Gets the 9:00 AM or 11:00 AM slot.

| Story Type | Why Tier 1 |
|-----------|-----------|
| Upsets over top-15 teams | Unranked or low-ranked team beating a top-15 opponent is the biggest story in softball on any given day |
| Program records broken | Career HR records, win records, strikeout milestones — these are historic |
| WCWS/Regionals/Supers results | Postseason results are always the top story during May-June |
| No-hitters and perfect games | Rare achievements that define a season |
| Selection Show bracket reveal | One day a year, always the lead |
| Major coaching hires/firings | Head coach changes at Power 4 programs |

### Tier 2 — Developing Stories (mid-day slots)

Strong content that fills 1:00 PM or 3:00 PM slots.

| Story Type | Why Tier 2 |
|-----------|-----------|
| Ranked vs ranked matchup results | Top-25 games always matter, even if no upset |
| Notable individual performances | 3+ HR games, 15+ K starts, walk-off homers |
| Developing win/loss streaks | Team on 10-game win streak or 5-game skid |
| Weekly/monthly awards | Conference POW, NFCA National Player of the Week |
| Conference standings shifts | Team takes over first place, clinches title |
| Transfer portal headline moves | Star player entering or committing |

### Tier 3 — Supporting Content (later slots)

Fills out the schedule. Gets the 5:30 PM or 8:00 PM slot.

| Story Type | Why Tier 3 |
|-----------|-----------|
| Weekend series previews | Lookahead at upcoming ranked matchups |
| Evergreen analysis | "Best pitching staffs in the country" type features |
| Milestone watch | Player approaching but not yet reaching a record |
| Midweek game previews | Tuesday/Wednesday non-conference games |
| Historical comparisons | "Best freshman classes ever" type content |

## Posting Priority

This order is non-negotiable:

1. **Game results and scores** — always first. A game-day recap after the next game starts is useless.
2. **Today's game previews** — ranked matchups, rivalry games, or elimination games happening today.
3. **Breaking news** — coaching changes, major transfers, injury announcements.
4. **Everything else** — analysis, evergreen, previews for future days.

If multiple game results exist, order by tier: upsets first, then ranked matchups, then notable performances.

## Fact-Check Requirements

Every post must be verified before publishing. Priority order:

### Priority 1 — Scores and Box Scores

| Check | How to Verify |
|-------|--------------|
| Final score | Cross-reference ESPN box score AND the team's official Twitter/athletics page |
| Inning details | Verify if game went extra innings — never assume 7 innings |
| Run-rule status | Confirm if game ended via run rule (8-0 after 5, or 10+ after 5 in some conferences) |
| Winning/losing pitcher | Verify from box score — do not guess based on starter |

### Priority 2 — Records and Streaks

| Check | How to Verify |
|-------|--------------|
| Career records | Check NCAA record book or team's official stats page |
| Season records | Verify against current NCAA leaderboards on ncaa.com |
| Win streaks | Count manually from schedule/results — don't trust secondary sources |
| Program records | Check the school's all-time records page or media guide |

### Priority 3 — Rankings and Stats

| Check | How to Verify |
|-------|--------------|
| Rankings | NFCA poll releases Tuesday, USA Softball poll releases Tuesday — verify poll source AND date |
| RPI/rankings movement | Confirm which poll (NFCA vs USA Softball vs RPI) — they differ |
| Player stats (BA, ERA, HR) | Verify against NCAA stats page or team's official stats — stats change daily |
| Player ages | Look up birth date, then calculate — never trust a secondary source's stated age |

### Priority 4 — Schedule and Logistics

| Check | How to Verify |
|-------|--------------|
| Game times | Verify against the official team schedule page — times change due to weather/TV |
| Day of week | Cross-reference the calendar — "Saturday's game" must actually be Saturday |
| Venue/location | Confirm from schedule — neutral-site tournaments move games between fields |
| TV/streaming info | Check ESPN schedule page — broadcasts get added/changed late |

## Conference Bias Check

Before finalizing any day's content, verify coverage breadth:

- Do not default to SEC/Big 12 every day just because they dominate rankings
- ACC, Big Ten, Pac-12 (or successor), AAC, and mid-majors produce Tier 1 stories too
- If 4+ posts are from the same conference, look harder at other conferences
- Mid-major upsets over Power 4 teams are always worth covering

## Poll Day Reminder

- **NFCA Coaches Poll**: Releases Tuesday
- **USA Softball Top 25**: Releases Tuesday
- **D1Softball Power Rankings**: Typically Wednesday
- On Tuesdays, rankings analysis is automatic Tier 2 content
- Never cite "this week's poll" on a Monday — the new poll hasn't dropped yet
