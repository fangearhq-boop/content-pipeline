### STORY 2: MLBx All-Star 3-On-3 — 3 Days Away

#### Long-Form Post — 11:00 AM ET

Sunday is three days away — and softball is about to take over Citizens Bank Park.

Four AUSL stars will anchor teams in the inaugural MLBx: All-Star 3-on-3 on July 12 in Philadelphia. Before you tune in, here's exactly how the event works:

The format is a 3-inning structure. Each player on both teams gets a 2-minute at-bat each inning. Offensively, home runs count as 1 point. Defensively, catches count as 1 point — so the teams that play strong defense can actually score. There's also a center-field target that awards bonus points on a direct hit.

The wildcard: the "hot streak" bonus. A team can call a hot streak, and on the next 5 consecutive swings, all offensive and defensive points are doubled. High risk, high reward — and it creates the kind of momentum swings that make for great TV.

🥎 Rachel Garcia (Texas Volts) leads Team Victorino — Shane Victorino, "The Flyin' Hawaiian"

Two semi-finals, then a final. 30 minutes each. At Citizens Bank Park, with 40,000 seats and MLB All-Star Week energy.
