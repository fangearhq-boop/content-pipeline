PGF Nationals Is 10 Days Away — And Thursday's Roster Freeze Is Your Last Chance

By Emily Rawlings | July 9, 2026

Ten days. One roster freeze deadline. If you're a coach or a family preparing for PGF Nationals, this is the week that counts.

PGF Nationals begins July 19 at Great Park in Irvine, California — the largest and most prestigious travel softball showcase of the summer. But before any 18U or 16U team steps onto a field in Orange County, rosters must be locked. The deadline: Thursday, July 13, at 11:59 PM PST. That's four days from now.

What the Freeze Means

After Thursday, rosters are set. No additions, no swaps, no last-minute portal pickups. The NCAA transfer portal remains open through July 31, but PGF's roster freeze operates independently of the college calendar — once the July 13 deadline passes, the players on your list are the players you bring to Irvine.

That makes this week the final window for any last-minute decisions. If there's a player your program has been evaluating, a late commitment you've been working toward, or an open spot you've been trying to fill — Thursday is the last call.

What Coaches Are Watching For

PGF Nationals is not just a competition — it's the summer's biggest evaluation event. College coaches from programs across the country will be in the stands at Great Park with clipboards, apps, and recruiting boards already organized for the Class of 2027 and the Class of 2028.

The September 1 contact window — when coaches can first directly contact recruits in the Class of 2028 — opens in 54 days. The coaches watching at PGF in July are building their lists for those September calls. A strong showing in Irvine can put a player on a program's radar in a way that changes the trajectory of their recruiting entirely.

For families with daughters in the Class of 2028, PGF Nationals is one of the most important stages before September 1. Show up, compete well, and coaches will know your name by the time contact rules allow them to reach out.

The Calendar in Full

Here's what the next several weeks look like for the softball recruiting and travel ball calendar:

Thursday, July 13: PGF Nationals roster freeze (11:59 PM PST). Sunday, July 19: PGF Nationals begins, Great Park Irvine — Week One (18U and 16U). July 31: NCAA transfer portal closes for the 2026-27 cycle. September 1: First contact day for the Class of 2028, 54 days from today.

The summer is short. The window to shape your program before the evaluation season begins is shorter. Get your roster set before Thursday.
