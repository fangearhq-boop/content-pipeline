**Tier:** 1
**Type:** FOLLOW UP

No. 2 Nebraska (42-6, 22-1 Big Ten) swept Penn State in Saturday's doubleheader — 5-1 and 10-4 — to clinch the outright Big Ten regular season title, the first in program history. In Game 1, Jordy Frahm went seven innings with 7 strikeouts. Jesse Farrell went 2-for-3 with a double and a solo HR. In Game 2, Nebraska trailed 4-1 entering the sixth before rallying: Kacie Hoffman drove in 2 runs, Frahm tied it with an RBI single, and Hannah Coor provided the lead with a 2-RBI double. Nebraska is the No. 1 seed in the Big Ten Tournament (May 6-9, College Park, MD). It is the Huskers' first regular season title since 2014.
