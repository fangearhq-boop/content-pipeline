Portal Countdown: 8 Days Left, Pickering's DNT Tag, and Three Stars Still Available

By Sarah Mitchell

June 14, 2026

The 15-day transfer portal window is more than halfway done. Day 7 is here, with eight days remaining before the June 22 deadline. Here is where the class stands.

Already Committed

Three notable players have found homes since the window opened June 8.

Samantha Swan, the Arizona State catcher who hit .383 with 12 home runs and 55 RBIs and earned All-Big 12 honors, committed to Texas. Swan is from Georgetown, Texas — the same hometown as Isa Torres — and her signing completed a storyline that has been building all summer.

Kailey Plumlee, a four-year eligible left-handed pitcher from Tennessee, committed to Alabama. The Crimson Tide added a proven arm to a pitching staff that is already among the best in the country.

Kaiah Altmeyer, a utility player from Arizona, also committed to Texas, adding another piece to head coach Mike White's 2027 roster.

And on June 13, Sun Belt Pitcher of the Year Maddy Azua committed to Texas A&M, giving the Aggies a left-handed option with a 1.95 ERA and 244.2 innings of experience.

Still Officially Available

The three biggest uncommitted names remain in the portal. Isa Torres (On3 No. 1, .530 BA, D1 Player of the Year) is still choosing between Texas and Texas A&M. Jaysoni Beachum (Softball America No. 1, .417 BA / 16 HR / 66 RBI) has not committed. Moriah Polar (On3 No. 5, .555 BA / 101 hits) remains available at Purdue.

The Pickering Wildcard

One player has moved into a different category: Kasidi Pickering (On3 No. 2), the Oklahoma outfielder who entered the portal with a "Do Not Contact" tag. The DNT designation is widely understood as a signal that she has privately selected a destination and is moving through the portal as a procedural step. Social media activity and recruitment network ties have pointed toward Texas Tech as her destination — though no official announcement has been made.

What's Next

Eight days remain. With Torres, Beachum, and Polar all still available, the biggest decisions of this portal cycle are still ahead. Programs still in pursuit of those three names are watching every day for news. The programs that land even one of the remaining stars will reshape their 2027 outlook immediately. June 22 is coming fast.
