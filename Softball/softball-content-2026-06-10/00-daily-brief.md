### STORY 1: Earleywine Leaves Texas for Tennessee — Longhorns Lose Second Key Coach
- **Tier:** 1
- **Classification:** NEW STORY
- **Angle:** Ehren Earleywine, the offensive coordinator who engineered Texas's record-setting 99-HR season and helped Katie Stewart hit a program-record 31 HRs in 2026, is departing for Tennessee as associate head coach. Tennessee head coach Kim Weekly adds Earleywine as a major rebuild piece after the Lady Vols lost Morgan and Plumlee to the transfer portal.
- **Why It Matters:** Texas's dynasty machine is facing its first real coaching turnover. Losing the architect of the nation's most explosive offense is a significant blow to the Longhorns' three-peat bid. Tennessee gets an elite offensive mind who knows how to develop hitters.
- **Sources:** Rocky Top Insider (June 9), WATE/WIVK (June 9), SI/Texas (June 9)

### STORY 3: Samantha Swan Visits Texas — Catcher Would Fill Key Need
- **Tier:** 2
- **Classification:** NEW STORY

- **Sources:** Roundtable.io (June 2026), Yahoo Sports/Arizona State portal tracker, SI Transfer Portal rankings
