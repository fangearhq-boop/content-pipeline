If LSU expected a routine home regional, they might want to check the VT roster first.

### STORY 2: Texas Title Defense, Kavan, Austin Regional

#### Long-Form Post — 11:00 AM ET

Texas won the 2025 Women's College World Series. They beat Texas Tech in the WCWS finals to claim the program's first national championship, one year after leaving the Big 12 for the SEC. This year, they went right back to Devon Park and proved it wasn't a fluke by winning the SEC Tournament title in dominating fashion.

On Friday, their title defense begins.

The Longhorns open the 2026 NCAA Tournament at home in Austin as the No. 2 overall seed. Their first regional opponent is Wagner, with Wisconsin taking on Baylor in the other first-round game. Texas is expected to advance out of the Austin Regional without much difficulty, which sets up a potential super regional at home against an opponent that would need to win an away regional first.

The key to everything, as it has been all season, is Teagan Kavan.

Kavan was the 2025 WCWS Most Outstanding Player. She threw a complete-game, 12-strikeout masterpiece in the SEC championship win over Alabama nine days ago. Her numbers this season are not just good. They are historically good. And she pitches with the kind of calm confidence that makes her difficult to rattle in high-pressure situations.

Back-to-back national titles in college softball is extremely rare. Oklahoma won four straight from 2021-2024, which stands as the sport's gold standard for sustained excellence. Before that, Florida won back-to-back in 2013 and 2014. Texas is trying to join a short list.

Friday is just the start. But the defending champions are ready.

### STORY 4: Megan Grant, UCLA L.A. Regional

#### Long-Form Post — 3:00 PM ET

Megan Grant hit 38 home runs this season. That number matters because the NCAA record before this year was 37. Laura Espinoza set it at Arizona in 1995, when she hit 37 home runs over 72 games. Grant broke it in 55 games. She didn't just tie the record in the Big Ten championship and let it sit — she walked up to the plate the next day and made history with her 38th.

Now she's taking that swing into the 2026 NCAA Tournament.

UCLA earned the No. 8 overall seed and will host the L.A. Regional at Easton Stadium, which will be hosting its 12th consecutive NCAA Softball Regional. The Bruins open Friday against Cal State Fullerton, with South Carolina taking on California Baptist in the other first-round game.

Easton Stadium is one of the best regional atmospheres in the sport. UCLA fans pack it out for postseason play, and this year they have a reason to be especially loud.

Grant's 38 home runs represent more than a number. They represent what she can do to a pitcher in a single at-bat. Any team in the L.A. Regional that lets one mistake pitch get to her could see a game change in an instant. No lead is safe until she's been retired three times.

This is her final run at a national title in college. Easton Stadium has seen a lot of great players over the years. It's seeing one of the best right now.
