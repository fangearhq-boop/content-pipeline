PGF Nationals Championship Day Is Tomorrow. September 1 Is 38 Days Away.

By Jordan Reeves | I Love Softball | July 24, 2026

The semifinals are today. The championships are tomorrow.

PGF Nationals championship games are Saturday, July 25, at Deanna Manning Stadium in Bill Barber Park, Irvine, California. The 16U Premier Championship is on ESPN+ at 8:30 AM PT (11:30 AM ET). The 18U Premier Championship is on ESPNU at 12:00 PM PT (3:00 PM ET). Two games. Two championships. The biggest day in club softball every year.

What PGF Nationals Is

More than 300 programs from 34 states came to Southern California last weekend. More than 70 teams competed in the 18U Premier bracket alone, each one representing a different corner of the country. The tournament runs five venues across the Irvine area, spreads across two full weeks, and ends with championship games on national television.

The teams playing for a title on Saturday made it through four days of bracket play. Every single one of them beat programs that were also trying to do the same thing. At this level, at this time of year, the talent pool is deep. Getting to Saturday is not a given for anyone.

The Number That Matters

September 1 is 38 days away.

That is the date when Division I coaches can, for the first time, directly contact the Class of 2028 — the current high school sophomore class — via phone, text, or email. Before September 1, coaches can attend tournaments. They can watch players from the stands and evaluate talent. They can put names on lists. But they cannot reach out.

September 1 is when the lists get dialed.

The players competing at PGF Nationals this week are exactly the players on those lists. A championship run in the 18U Premier or 16U Premier bracket at PGF does not guarantee anything. But it does mean that a coach who already has a player's name writes something next to it — a tournament result that confirms what they saw on film, a performance that moved a prospect from "interested" to "call her first."

That is what is at stake tomorrow beyond the hardware.

Championship Day Schedule

The 16U Premier Championship tips off at 8:30 AM PT (11:30 AM ET) on ESPN+. The 18U Premier Championship follows at 12:00 PM PT (3:00 PM ET) on ESPNU. In the evening, the PGF High School All-American Games run on both networks — the Futures game on ESPN+ at 3:00 PM PT and the Senior All-American Game on ESPNU at 7:00 PM PT.

For programs that have spent the last two weeks competing at five different venues in 90-degree California heat, Saturday is the finish line and the starting line at the same time. Win the championship and you carry that result into every recruiting conversation for the next twelve months. The coaches watching tomorrow know it. The players competing tomorrow know it.
