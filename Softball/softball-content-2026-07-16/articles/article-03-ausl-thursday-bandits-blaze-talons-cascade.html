AUSL Thursday: Bandits Face Blaze Rematch, Talons Host Cascade in Final Stretch

By Jordan Reeves | I Love Softball | July 16, 2026

Four games remain in the 2026 AUSL regular season. Two of them are tonight.

Game 1: Bandits vs. Blaze — 7 PM ET, Smith Family Stadium, Durham, NC

The Chicago Bandits are on the road for a rematch after the Carolina Blaze beat them 12-6 in Chicago on Wednesday. The Bandits need this one. They sit at approximately 13-8, trailing the Utah Talons — who have already clinched the No. 1 seed and a direct bye to the Championship Series — by more than a game in the standings.

Carolina infielder Ana Gold hit two home runs and drove in five runs in Wednesday's win. The Blaze have shown they can put up runs in bunches. Chicago will need a cleaner defensive game and better pitching to take the series back on the road.

The Bandits' path through the postseason runs through the play-in game on July 23 if they finish second. If they somehow close the gap on Utah before July 20, they earn the bye. Either way, they need to hold second place — and that means tonight matters.

Game 2: Talons vs. Cascade — 10 PM ET, Hillsboro Ballpark, Hillsboro, OR

The Utah Talons have nothing at stake in the standings — they've already clinched. But they're playing the Portland Cascade, who hold the third and final playoff spot and need wins to protect it.

Cascade sits at approximately 11-9. The regular season ends July 20. The team that finishes third plays the second seed in the play-in game on July 23. Portland needs to finish there — and finishing there requires wins against Utah, who will still be playing to sharpen the lineup for the Championship Series.

Megan Grant leads the AUSL in home runs with 8 through 20 games. She'll be in the Cascade lineup tonight in front of a home crowd at Hillsboro Ballpark.

The Road to Davis Diamond

The 2026 AUSL Championship begins July 25 at Davis Diamond on the Texas A&M campus in College Station, Texas. Game 1 airs on ABC — the first professional softball game ever on network television. The play-in between the second and third seeds is July 23. The No. 1 seed — the Utah Talons — gets a direct pass to the Championship Series.

Tonight starts the final push. Four games left for most teams. Every at-bat counts.
