### STORY 1: Arkansas — The Only Host Without a WCWS Berth

**Tier:** 1
**Angle:** Out of 16 regional hosts in the 2026 NCAA tournament, only one has never appeared at the Women's College World Series: Arkansas. Despite nine consecutive NCAA Tournament appearances under Courtney Deifel and a near-miss résumé that includes three home super-regional eliminations (2021, 2022, 2025), the Razorbacks have yet to reach Oklahoma City. Friday's Fayetteville Regional opens their latest chapter — and the weight of being the field's only host without a WCWS credit hangs over every pitch. A hitting slump (2 runs per game over their last six outings) adds genuine tension to a program that returned nearly all of its pitching from 2025.
