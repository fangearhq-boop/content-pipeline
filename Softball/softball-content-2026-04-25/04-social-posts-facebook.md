## STORY 1: Wells HR No. 35 / OU NCAA Team Record

#### Long-Form Post — 9:00 AM ET
Kendall Wells hit her 35th home run of the season Friday, and with it she broke two records at once.

The solo blast in the first inning against Georgia wasn't just the go-ahead run in a 10-2 Oklahoma win — it was the home run that broke the Sooners' single-season program record held by Jocelyn Alo, who slugged 34 in both 2021 and 2022. And it was the home run that pushed Oklahoma's team total to 165 on the season, shattering the NCAA single-season team home run record.

Gabbie Garcia added a six-RBI night as OU cruised to a six-inning, 10-2 win over No. 11 Georgia at Love's Field.

Two more. That's all Wells needs to tie Lauren Espinoza's all-time NCAA single-season record of 37, set at Arizona in 1995 — a mark that has stood for over 30 years.

Game 2 is today at 1:30 PM on ESPN+. Oklahoma is 43-6, tied with Alabama at the top of the SEC. This is appointment softball.
