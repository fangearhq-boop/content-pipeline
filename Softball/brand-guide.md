# I Love Softball — Brand Guide

## Brand Identity

- **Brand Name:** I Love Softball
- **Parent Company:** FanGear HQ
- **Logo:** Softball with colorful fireworks (file: `i-love-softball-logo.jpg` in project root)
- **Canva Brand Kit ID:** kAGENnl7bLQ

## Colors

| Name | Hex Code | Usage |
|------|----------|-------|
| Primary Orange | #FF8A00 | Headlines, player names, key stats |
| Primary Gold | #FFD700 | Rankings, awards, accent highlights |
| Accent Red | #E53935 | Upsets, breaking news, dramatic moments |
| Dark Navy | #1A1A2E | Text overlay backgrounds (semi-transparent 40-50% opacity) |
| White | #FFFFFF | Body text on dark overlays, secondary text |

## Typography

| Role | Font | Usage |
|------|------|-------|
| Title/Headlines | Bebas Neue | All caps, bold. Used for main text overlays on images |
| Subtitle | Montserrat | Clean, professional. Used for stat lines and sub-text |
| Body | Open Sans | Readable. Used for article body text |

## Image Dimensions

| Platform | Width | Height |
|----------|-------|--------|
| X/Twitter | 1200 | 675 |
| Facebook | 1200 | 630 |

## Image Composition Rules

1. Semi-transparent dark navy bar (#1A1A2E at 40-50% opacity) behind all text overlays
2. Text placement: top-left or bottom-center preferred
3. Keep 50-65% of image clean for action photography
4. Logo placement: bottom-right corner when space allows
5. Action shots preferred over posed shots
6. Horizontal/landscape orientation only

## Social Media Voice

### X/Twitter Voice
- Enthusiastic, passionate, like a knowledgeable friend at a game
- ALL CAPS for emphasis on key stats or moments
- Dashes and periods for dramatic pacing
- Strong opinions backed by stats
- Max 3 hashtags per tweet
- Primary hashtags: #Softball #NCAASoftball #CollegeSoftball #WPF

### Facebook Voice
- More explanatory — assume reader needs context
- Narrative storytelling across 3-4 paragraphs
- Opinionated but less aggressive than X
- One emoji per caption OK, no emojis in long-form
- No hashtags in Facebook posts

### Article Voice
- Professional sports journalism
- Accessible to casual fans
- Stats woven into narrative, not listed
- Always end with "What's Next" forward-looking section

## Photo Sourcing

- **Primary source:** Imagn (USA TODAY Sports Images)
- **URL format:** `https://www.imagn.com/search?q=SEARCH+TERMS`
- **Credit required:** "(USA TODAY Sports Images)" or "(Imagn Images)"
- **Licensed through:** Reuters

## Canva Workflow

1. Download selected photo from Imagn
2. Create new design in Canva (use dimensions above)
3. Upload Imagn photo as background
4. Apply Brand Kit (ID: kAGENnl7bLQ) for fonts and colors
5. Add text overlay per image concept specs
6. Add I Love Softball logo
7. Export as PNG
