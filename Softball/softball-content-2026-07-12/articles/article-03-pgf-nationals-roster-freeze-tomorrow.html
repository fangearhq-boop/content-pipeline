PGF Nationals Roster Freeze Is Tomorrow — What Families Need to Know Before 11:59 PM PST

By Jordan Reeves | I Love Softball | July 12, 2026

Tomorrow at 11:59 PM Pacific Standard Time, the PGF Nationals roster freeze takes effect — and every team heading to Great Park in Irvine, California is locked in.

That moment is less than 36 hours away. If your family has any remaining roster decisions to make — a potential addition, a late move from an eligible age group — today is the day to act. Not tomorrow morning. Today.

What the Freeze Actually Means

Once the July 13 deadline passes, team rosters are frozen through August 2, 2026. That covers the entire PGF Nationals tournament week (which begins July 19) and then some. No player may be added after the freeze deadline, regardless of circumstance.

The rule applies uniformly: once a team has qualified for Nationals, all players on that roster are frozen from the moment their qualification is recorded through the August 2 release date.

Before the freeze, qualifying teams do have limited flexibility. They may add players from like or younger age groups up until the deadline — but only if the player being added was not on a team that already qualified in any age group, and only if a formal release has been obtained. If there is any ambiguity about a player's eligibility for addition, check the PGF rules directly at pgfnationals.com before proceeding.

System Maintenance Warning

PGF has also announced scheduled system maintenance beginning July 13 at 10:00 PM PST, running through July 14 at 10:00 AM PST. This is on the same day as the roster freeze deadline.

If you are planning to make a roster change, do not wait until the final hour. Completing roster updates before the system goes offline at 10:00 PM PST gives you a buffer — and avoids the risk of a technical issue preventing a move you intended to make. The deadline is 11:59 PM PST, but the system maintenance window starts at 10:00 PM PST. Those are two different times, and the one that matters for your access is 10:00 PM.

What Comes Next

PGF Nationals begin July 19, 2026 at Great Park in Irvine, California (8000 Great Park Blvd, Irvine CA 92618). Week One covers the 18U and 16U age groups. The transfer portal remains open through July 31, and the Class of 2028 contact window opens September 1 — now 50 days away.

For teams that are ready and locked, the next seven days are about preparation, travel, and arriving with everything in order. PGF Nationals is the biggest travel softball tournament in the country. The freeze is the last logistical gate before it begins.
