PGF Nationals Is 14 Days Away: The Roster Freeze Clock Is Ticking

By Emily Rawlings

July 5, 2026

Tia Milloy's commitment to Arizona State on July 5 was not just the close of a 41-day portal saga — it was a reminder of where the college softball calendar now stands.

The roster freeze is eight days away. PGF Nationals is 14 days away. And the September 1 contact window for the Class of 2028 is 58 days out. The summer is moving fast, and programs that haven't finalized their portal additions need to do it now.

What the Roster Freeze Means

The July 13 deadline — 11:59 PM — is when NAIA and NCAA portal rosters lock. Any transfer who hasn't committed and completed paperwork before that date cannot be added to a program's 2027 roster. For teams that still have open spots to fill, the next eight days are critical.

Milloy's commitment today lands inside that window — barely. ASU made its move with eight days to spare. Programs that waited longer than that now face a harder calculus.

PGF Nationals and the 2028 Pivot

PGF Nationals opens July 19 at Great Park Irvine in Irvine, California, running through August 3. It's the sport's largest summer recruiting showcase, drawing hundreds of high school prospects and college coaches from every Power Conference program.

The 18U and 16U divisions run during Week 1 (July 19-26). The 14U division follows in Week 2. College coaches at PGF will be operating in evaluation mode — the July 31 dead period ends contact with recruits, so programs attending are watching and building their September call list.

September 1 opens the Class of 2028 contact window. Under the House Settlement's 25-scholarship cap, every incoming freshman spot carries more deliberate consideration than the previous generation of recruiting. Coaches attending PGF aren't just evaluating prospects informally — they're making decisions that affect scholarship budgets for two or three years.

What the Numbers Say

The transfer portal closed June 22 after a 15-day window. Most of the significant moves — Torres to Texas, Beachum to Florida, Pickering to Texas Tech, Milloy to Arizona State — happened within that window or in the weeks immediately following. The portal's influence on 2027 rosters is largely set. What remains is the smaller set of uncommitted players who, like Milloy, waited through July.

That group is now dramatically smaller. Eight days from now, it disappears entirely.

What's Next

Programs finalizing portal rosters have until July 13 at 11:59 PM. After that, the focus shifts entirely to the PGF evaluation period, fall practice planning, and the September 1 contact push for the 2028 class. For teams that head to PGF with clean, complete rosters — including Arizona State, which just added Milloy — the summer runway opens up. For programs that still have open spots, the next eight days are among the most consequential of the entire offseason.
