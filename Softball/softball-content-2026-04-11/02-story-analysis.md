### STORY 4: Kendall Wells at 30 HRs — All-Time Record Chase

**Freshness Tags:** MILESTONE, RECORD CHASE, HISTORIC

**Summary:**
Kendall Wells is 30 home runs into the greatest power-hitting freshman season in college softball history. She's already broken the SEC single-season record (HR No. 27 vs. Wichita State) and tied the NCAA freshman record (HR No. 30 vs. Kentucky). Now she needs seven more to break the all-time NCAA single-season record of 37, set by Janie Reed Espinoza of Arizona in 1995. The Texas series is the biggest stage she's played on yet — and it arrives right as the record is within reach.
