## STORY 3: Oklahoma Wins Series Over Arkansas — Wells at 32

#### Long-Form Post — 12:30 PM ET

Oklahoma's weekend against Arkansas had everything: a historic home win streak snapped, a dominant run-rule bounceback, and one very significant number.

32.

Kendall Wells hit her 32nd home run of the season during the OU-Arkansas series, and the math now reads like this: Lauren Espinoza hit 37 home runs at Arizona in 1995. That record has stood for 31 years. Wells needs 5 more to tie it.

The series itself went 2-1 in OU's favor. Arkansas was scrappy — in Game 2, the Razorbacks won 3-2 and snapped the Sooners' remarkable 31-game home winning streak. That sting lasted about 24 hours.

Because in Game 3, Oklahoma was a different team. Freshman Allyssa Parker threw a complete game. The Sooners bashed four home runs. The final was 11-1 in five innings — a run-rule statement.

Oklahoma heads into the final stretch of the regular season with a 13-2+ SEC record and one of the most captivating individual storylines in college sports history watching over them.

Every Kendall Wells at-bat now carries the weight of three decades of softball history. 🥎

## STORY 6: WCWS Bracketology Update

#### Long-Form Post — 5:30 PM ET

The 2026 Women's College World Series tournament bracket is starting to take shape — and there are some new names at the top of the hosting picture.

The latest Softball America bracketology update names Stanford and Texas A&M as new regional host favorites, replacing Florida State and Arizona, which dropped out of the hosting conversation following recent results.

A few things you need to know about the 2026 tournament:

The selection show is May 10. Regionals begin May 15. And for the first time, 32 teams will receive official seeds — a format change that doesn't alter the 16 national seeds but gives top programs more favorable regional matchup brackets.

The SEC leads the way with roughly half of the regional hosting spots. The ACC, Big 12, and Big Ten all have multiple host candidates as well.

With results like tonight's FSU-Florida showdown directly impacting RPI and seeding, every game over the next three weeks matters for the bracket.

Your team's postseason path is being written right now. 🥎
