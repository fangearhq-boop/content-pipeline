PGF Nationals Opens in 48 Hours — Here's What to Know Before Saturday

By Sarah Mitchell | I Love Softball | July 17, 2026

The biggest girls' fastpitch tournament in the United States begins Saturday, July 19 at Great Park in Irvine, California. PGF Nationals is two days away, rosters are locked, and the competition field is set.

Here is what you need to know before the first pitch Saturday.

The Format

PGF Nationals runs July 19 through August 4, split across two weeks. Week 1, beginning Saturday, features the 18U and 16U divisions and runs through July 25. Week 2, starting July 26, brings out the 14U, 12U, and 10U programs and runs through August 2, with a national championship final on August 4.

All competition takes place at facilities within the Great Park complex in Irvine — a venue built for exactly this kind of scale. Multiple diamonds run simultaneously through the bracket phase before the field narrows toward championship weekend.

18U — Where the Recruiting Stakes Are Highest

The 18U division at PGF Nationals is where the highest-profile programs compete for the national title, and where Division I college coaches do much of their evaluation. Arizona Storm Appel comes in as one of the top contenders for the 18U crown.

Every team in the field at PGF earned their way in through qualifying. The bracket includes some of the best travel programs in the country — teams that have spent the entire summer refining rosters, developing pitchers, and preparing for this week.

The Recruiting Clock

For players in the 14U through 18U age groups, what happens at PGF Nationals over the next two weeks will shape conversations that begin in 45 days.

September 1 is the date when college coaches can officially begin making direct contact with Class of 2028 recruits. Coaches spend the weeks before September 1 evaluating players and building their offer lists. A player who performs at PGF Nationals goes into September with momentum and visibility. A player who struggles does not disappear from the picture, but has work to do.

The recruits who stand out at PGF over the next 18 days are the ones whose phones will be busy on September 2.

Rosters Are Locked

The PGF roster freeze passed on July 13 at 11:59 PM PST. The rosters entered in the bracket are the rosters that will compete. There are no last-minute moves now — the teams are set, the schedules are posted, and Great Park will decide the rest.

What's Next

PGF Nationals 18U and 16U competition begins Saturday, July 19 at Great Park, Irvine, California. Live scoring is available through FloSoftball and the PGF official website. Watch for Arizona Storm Appel in the 18U bracket. Class of 2028 recruits: September 1 is in 45 days. Make the most of this week.
