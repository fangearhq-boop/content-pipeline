## Today's News Landscape

Monday March 30 brings a full set of Sunday rubber match results — and the biggest story is a stunning upset. No. 15 Texas A&M traveled to McCombs Field and stunned No. 1 Texas 9-7, snapping the Longhorns' 29-game win streak (and their 19-game home win streak). It marks the first time A&M has beaten Texas in a regular-season game since 2009. Meanwhile, Arkansas delivered its own dramatic finish — trailing 4-0 through six innings, the Razorbacks rallied for a walk-off 6-4 win over No. 5 Florida, capped by Dakota Kennedy's walk-off three-run homer. It's Arkansas's fourth straight series win over Florida. Oklahoma bounced back from Saturday's loss to win Game 3 over LSU 8-4, with Kendall Wells hitting her 26th home run and pulling to within four of the NCAA freshman record. Nebraska upset No. 7 UCLA 8-4 in the rubber match. And No. 16 Washington threw a combined no-hitter over Iowa on Saturday. The NFCA Week 8 poll drops Tuesday — expect a major reshuffling.
