No. 12 Texas A&M pulled off one of the biggest rallies of the 2026 season Friday, erasing a 5-3 Oklahoma deficit with five runs in the sixth inning to stun No. 1 OU, 8-5, in the weather-delayed Game 1 of their series.

Can OU bounce back? Will Wells tie — or break — the record today? Tune in. 📺
