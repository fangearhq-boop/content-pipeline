PGF Nationals Roster Freeze Is Tonight — Read This Before 10 PM PST

By Emily Rawlings | I Love Softball | July 13, 2026

Today is the day that PGF families have been tracking for weeks. The 2026 PGF National Championship roster freeze closes tonight at 11:59 PM Pacific Standard Time.

After that moment, your team's roster is locked. No additions. No substitutions. No exceptions. Whatever lineup you have entered into the PGF system at midnight Pacific will be the roster your team carries to Great Park in Irvine, California, when Nationals begin July 19.

If you are a family still waiting on a last-minute roster change, tonight is your window — and it is narrower than you think.

The Practical Deadline Is 10 PM PST, Not Midnight

Here is the detail that changes everything: PGF has scheduled system maintenance beginning tonight at 10:00 PM PST, running through July 14 at 10:00 AM PST. The system will be offline during that window.

That means the effective deadline for making roster changes is 10:00 PM PST — not 11:59 PM. If you try to log in at 10:30 PM to add a player, the system will not be available. The maintenance window ends after the freeze cutoff, so there is no recovery time.

The message is simple: do not wait until late tonight. If you have roster business to complete, complete it before 10 PM PST.

Who Can Still Be Added?

The PGF rules allow for last-minute roster additions under specific conditions. A player with a formal release from a non-qualifying team in the same or younger age group can still be added to a qualifying team's roster before the freeze closes. This is the primary use case for families who have been monitoring the roster situation up to today.

Teams that have already finalized their rosters do not need to take any action. The freeze simply locks what is already confirmed in the system.

What PGF Nationals Is

For families new to the tournament, the 2026 PGF National Championship is the largest and most prestigious tournament in travel softball. It draws elite 18U, 16U, and 14U programs from across the country to compete for a national title.

Week 1 of the 2026 tournament — July 19 through approximately July 26 — features the 18U and 16U age divisions at Great Park in Irvine, California. Week 2 will feature the 14U division. The tournament is a culmination of an entire travel softball season, with teams that have spent months qualifying for the right to compete.

What's Next

After tonight's roster freeze at 11:59 PM PST (or practically, 10 PM PST before maintenance begins), PGF Nationals officially begins July 19 at Great Park in Irvine. The tournament runs through the end of July. For the 18U and 16U families who have been competing and qualifying all season, the final chapter starts in six days. Make sure your roster is ready tonight.
