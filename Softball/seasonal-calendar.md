# I Love Softball — Seasonal Calendar

Use this to calibrate story selection and tiering throughout the season.

## Season Timeline

| Period | Dates (approx) | What's Happening | Tiering Notes |
|--------|----------------|------------------|---------------|
| Preseason | Late Jan – mid Feb | Opening weekend tournaments, early polls | Openers are Tier 1-2; rankings are Tier 2 |
| Tournament Season | Mid Feb – mid Mar | Mary Nutter, Clearwater, Puerto Vallarta, Judi Garman | Ranked matchups Tier 1; results Tier 2; previews Tier 3 |
| Conference Play | Mid Mar – mid May | SEC, Big 12, Pac-12, ACC, Big Ten weekly series | Conference openers Tier 1; mid-season Tier 2; standings Tier 3 |
| Selection Show | Mid May | NCAA Tournament bracket reveal | Always Tier 1 |
| Regionals | Mid-late May | 16 regional sites, double-elimination | Every regional Tier 1-2 |
| Super Regionals | Late May – early Jun | Best-of-3 to reach WCWS | All Tier 1 |
| WCWS | Early-mid Jun | Women's College World Series in OKC | Everything Tier 1 |
| Offseason | Mid Jun – Jan | Transfer portal, coaching changes, recruiting | Tier 2-3 news-driven |

## Key Annual Events

### February
- Opening weekend tournaments (multiple sites)
- Mary Nutter Collegiate Classic (Cathedral City, CA)
- Clearwater Invitational (Clearwater, FL)
- Puerto Vallarta College Challenge (Mexico)
- First NFCA/USA Softball polls

### March
- Judi Garman Classic (Fullerton, CA)
- Conference play begins (mid-March)
- Mid-season All-America watch lists
- Conference series format: Fri-Sat-Sun 3-game sets

### April
- Conference races heat up
- Player/Pitcher of the Year races
- Mid-season coaching awards
- Senior Day celebrations

### May (Early)
- Conference tournaments
- Regular season finales
- Final poll rankings before NCAAs

### May (Mid-Late)
- NCAA Selection Show (always Tier 1)
- Regionals (16 sites, double-elimination)
- Super Regionals (best-of-3, hosted by top 8 seeds)

### June
- Women's College World Series (OKC)
- Championship Series (best-of-3 final)
- Awards season: NFCA All-Americans, USA Softball Player of the Year

### Offseason (Jul–Jan)
- Transfer portal activity (peaks July, December)
- Coaching carousel
- Recruiting: NLI signing periods (November, April)
- USA Softball national team events
- WPF (Women's Professional Fastpitch) coverage

## Story Selection Reminders

- **Ranked matchups always tier up** — any game between top-25 teams is at minimum Tier 2
- **Upsets are always Tier 1** — unranked team beating a top-15 team is automatic lead story
- **Milestones tier up** — career records, program records, perfect games always Tier 1-2
- **Weather/cancellation impact** — tournament changes due to weather affect multiple teams, worth covering
- **Regional bias awareness** — cover all conferences, not just SEC/Pac-12
