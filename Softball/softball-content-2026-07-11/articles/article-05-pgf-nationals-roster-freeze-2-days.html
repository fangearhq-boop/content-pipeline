PGF Nationals Roster Freeze Is in 2 Days — What Coaches and Families Need to Know

By Jordan Reeves | I Love Softball | July 11, 2026

The PGF Nationals roster freeze is 48 hours away. Monday, July 13, at 11:59 PM PST is the hard deadline — and today, Saturday July 11, and tomorrow, Sunday July 12, are the last two windows to make any roster moves before rosters are locked for Great Park.

PGF Nationals begin July 19 at Great Park, Irvine (8000 Great Park Blvd, Irvine CA 92618). Week One covers 18U and 16U divisions.

The Freeze Date: What It Means

The roster freeze locks every team's active player list for the tournament. Any coach who is considering a late add, a pickup player, or a position change must submit those moves before 11:59 PM PST on Monday night. Once the freeze hits, rosters are final for Week One.

The critical distinction this weekend: the freeze is not tomorrow. Tomorrow is Sunday, July 12. The freeze is Monday, July 13. That means coaches who planned to handle everything "tomorrow" still have Sunday to work with — but the margin has narrowed. If paperwork, transfer portal transactions, or communications take time to process, Sunday is the last safe window. Monday is the absolute backstop.

Other Active Roster Rules

Automatic releases have been active since July 1. Players from non-qualified or non-invited teams were automatically released from those rosters on that date and are now eligible to be added to participating teams — subject to the July 13 freeze deadline.

The transfer portal remains open through July 31, which extends past the freeze date. However, a player moving through the transfer portal after the July 13 deadline will not be eligible for Week One at PGF Nationals. The portal stays open, but the tournament roster window does not.

What Families Should Do Now

If your daughter's team has an open roster spot, confirm with your coach whether that spot will be filled before Monday night. If your daughter is currently on a roster and you have any uncertainty about her status — particularly if her team was recently re-classified — reach out to your club director before Sunday evening.

For families who have been waiting on late decisions about which team their daughter will compete with at nationals, today and tomorrow are the final windows. Do not wait until Monday morning.

What's Next

Roster freeze: Monday, July 13 at 11:59 PM PST. PGF Nationals Week One (18U and 16U) begins Saturday, July 19 at Great Park, Irvine (8000 Great Park Blvd, Irvine CA 92618). The transfer portal remains open through July 31. The September 1 contact window for the Class of 2028 opens in approximately 52 days.
