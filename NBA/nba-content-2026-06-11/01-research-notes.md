### STORY 1: Game 4 Recap — Knicks 107-106 Spurs

**Verified Facts (HIGH confidence):**
- Final score: Knicks 107, Spurs 106 (June 10, Madison Square Garden)
- Series: Knicks lead 3-1
- Jalen Brunson: 36 points (lead scorer)
- OG Anunoby: 33 points (playoff career-high), 7 three-pointers (playoff career-high), hit game-winning tip-in with 1.2 seconds left
- Spurs halftime lead: 76-49 (largest road halftime lead in Finals history, excl. 2020 bubble per CBS Sports)
- Spurs first half: 28-of-47 (59.6%) from field; scored 76 points
- Spurs second half: 8-of-39 (20.5%) from field; scored 30 points
- 46-point swing from first to second half tied for largest in shot clock era (Elias Sports Bureau)
- OG Anunoby tipped in Brunson's long 3-point attempt at 1.2 seconds remaining
- Comeback of 29 points is the largest in NBA Finals history (previous record: 24 points)
- Source: ESPN, NBA.com, CBS Sports (multiple confirming)

**Verified Facts (MEDIUM confidence):**
- Spurs' 14 three-pointers in the first half set a Finals first-half record (per CBS Sports, single source)
- This came as "only the second time in Finals history" that road team won Games 1-3; now NYK lead 3-1

**Research notes:**
- Game started as total Spurs domination; Wemby and company could not miss in H1
- Knicks mounted comeback in second half; MSG erupted
- Anunoby catch the ball off Brunson's 3-point miss and tipped it with 1.2 seconds left
- Spurs' second-half shooting collapsed dramatically
