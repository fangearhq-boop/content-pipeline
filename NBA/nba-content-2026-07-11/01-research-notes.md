### STORY 3: Victor Wembanyama $252M Extension

**Sources:**
- ESPN: Spurs, Wembanyama reach $252M max extension (July 11, 2026)
- CBS Sports: Wembanyama agrees to 5-year, $252M extension
- Bleacher Report: Wemby, Spurs agree to $252M contract
- NBC Sports: Wemby's extension leaves $50M on table

**Key Facts:**
- San Antonio Spurs and Victor Wembanyama agreed to a 5-year, $252 million max rookie-scale extension
- The extension runs through the 2031-32 season, with a player option in year five
- Wembanyama chose the 25% max rather than the 30% supermax — leaving approximately $50 million on the table
- The supermax would have been approximately $303 million
- The $252M deal is the third-largest rookie extension in NBA history, behind Cade Cunningham ($269M) and Evan Mobley ($269M)
- Wembanyama's discount signals commitment to San Antonio's long-term project
- Confidence: HIGH (ESPN, CBS Sports, Bleacher Report all confirm)
