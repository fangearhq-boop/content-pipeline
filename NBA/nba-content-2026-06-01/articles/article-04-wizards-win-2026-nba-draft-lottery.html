Washington Wizards Win 2026 NBA Draft Lottery, Land No. 1 Overall Pick

By Jake Torres | Hoop Heroes | June 1, 2026

The Washington Wizards are holding the No. 1 overall pick in the 2026 NBA Draft — and for a franchise that has been struggling in recent years, it could be the franchise-altering moment they have been waiting for.

Washington won the 2026 NBA Draft Lottery on May 10, converting a 14.0% chance into the top selection. It is the first time the Wizards have owned the No. 1 pick since they selected John Wall out of Kentucky back in 2010. The 2026 NBA Draft is scheduled for June 23-24.

The Full Lottery Order

The top five picks in the 2026 NBA Draft are set: No. 1 Washington Wizards, No. 2 Utah Jazz, No. 3 Memphis Grizzlies, No. 4 Chicago Bulls, No. 5 Los Angeles Clippers.

The Jazz, Grizzlies, and Bulls all come away from the lottery in strong positions to add top-tier prospects. The Clippers at No. 5 will also be able to select a high-quality player in a draft class regarded as one of the deepest in recent memory.

What It Means for Washington

The Wizards have been in full rebuild mode for several years. Landing the No. 1 pick gives the franchise a chance to build around a cornerstone talent. The last time Washington had the first selection, John Wall gave the Wizards a decade-long anchor.

With the No. 1 pick in 2026, Washington will have their choice of the entire draft class. The biggest decision in franchise history in over a decade now sits in front of their front office.

What's Next

The 2026 NBA Draft takes place June 23-24. Washington will be on the clock first, with the eyes of the basketball world watching. Between now and then, scouting, workouts, and trade rumors will dominate alongside the Finals. For Wizards fans, June 23 cannot come soon enough.
