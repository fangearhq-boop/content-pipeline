### STORY 3: Kawhi Trade Frozen — Two Investigations

**Long-Form Post (9:30 PM ET)**
The Kawhi Leonard situation has gotten even more complicated.

The NBA is now investigating not one but two separate endorsement deals connected to Alex Sanberg and Aspiration Sports, according to a report from HoopsHype. The second deal, previously undisclosed, adds another layer to an already complex investigation. Sanberg was convicted and sentenced to 14 years in prison related to the fraud scheme.

The Clippers and Raptors reached a trade agreement weeks ago: Kawhi Leonard to Toronto in exchange for Brandon Ingram, Gradey Dick, two first-round picks, two second-round picks, and a pick swap. It's a deal both teams want to complete. But neither team can move forward until Commissioner Adam Silver concludes the investigation and clears the transaction.

There is no public timeline on when that resolution might come. The longer it drags on, the more uncertainty it creates for both franchises — the Clippers trying to move forward with their rebuild, and the Raptors hoping to reunite with the player who brought them their only championship in 2019.

For now, everyone waits.
