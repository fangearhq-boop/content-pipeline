### STORY 3: Austin Reaves — Countdown to Decision

**Long-Form Post:**
The Austin Reaves free agency clock is ticking.

By June 29, Reaves must decide whether to decline his $14.9 million player option for next season and become an unrestricted free agent. The expectation in league circles: he declines, and the Lakers make him the richest offer on the market.

LA can offer a five-year maximum contract worth approximately $239 million. Outside teams — including the Brooklyn Nets, who had been mentioned as competition — can only offer four years at up to $178.5 million.

The money advantage is massive. But the latest reports say Reaves' decision "will not come down solely to a dollar figure." His partnership with Luka Doncic, his love of Los Angeles, and his identity with the Lakers organization all factor in.

Averaged 23.3 points per game last season. This is a max player staying home.
