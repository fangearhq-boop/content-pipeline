### STORY 1: Knicks Sweep Cavaliers 130-93 — First NBA Finals Since 1999
- **Tier:** 1
- **Category:** Playoff Results / ECF Sweep
- **Why it matters:** The New York Knicks obliterated the Cleveland Cavaliers 130-93 Monday night in Game 4 of the Eastern Conference Finals, completing a sweep and advancing to the NBA Finals for the first time in 27 years. Karl-Anthony Towns led six Knicks in double figures with 19 points and 14 rebounds. Brunson added 15, Bridges 15, Shamet 16 off the bench. The Knicks led by as many as 45 points. ### STORY 3: Jalen Brunson Named 2026 ECF MVP
- **Tier:** 2
- **Category:** Award / Player Spotlight
