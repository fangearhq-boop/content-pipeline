Doncic revealed this week that when he suffered his Grade 2 left hamstring strain on April 2, doctors told him to expect an eight-week recovery timeline. Do the math: eight weeks from April 2 puts his return in the last week of May 2026. The current Thunder-Lakers series began on May 5. In an effort to speed the process, Luka flew to Spain to receive specialized cell therapy from Dr. Javier Barrio — the sports doctor at Real Madrid, Luka's former club. The desperation behind that trip says everything. LeBron James is giving everything he has. The team is trying. But this postseason is running out of road for the King's Lakers. Do you think Luka comes back at all in the 2026 playoffs?
