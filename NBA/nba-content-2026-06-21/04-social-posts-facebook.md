His partnership with Luka Doncic — arguably the best player in the world — and his comfort in Los Angeles carry real weight. After averaging 23.3 PPG last season, Reaves has cemented himself as a legitimate max player, but he's also proven himself as someone who values fit and winning alongside great teammates.

Multiple league sources still expect the Lakers to keep Reaves. The next nine days will tell the story.
