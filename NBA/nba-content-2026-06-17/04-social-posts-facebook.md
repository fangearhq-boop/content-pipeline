**Long-Form Post**
Window: 4:00 PM ET

There's a new development in the Austin Reaves free agency situation — and Lakers fans are going to like it.

New reports suggest the Brooklyn Nets may not pursue Reaves as aggressively as previously anticipated, putting the Los Angeles Lakers in the driver's seat to retain their 28-year-old shooting guard.

Here's the financial picture:
• Lakers (exclusive window open now through June 30): can offer 5 years / ~$239M

The Nets had been the most-discussed rival suitor, but if they're cooling, this could resolve faster than the market anticipated.
