### STORY 1: Knicks 115, Cavaliers 104 (OT) — Biggest ECF Comeback Since 1997
- **Tier:** 1
- **Category:** Playoff Results / Historic Comeback
- **Why it matters:** The New York Knicks trailed by 22 points with 7:52 left in the fourth quarter and won in overtime, 115-104, in ECF Game 1 at Madison Square Garden. They outscored Cleveland 44-11 over the final 7:40 of regulation and OT. It is the largest fourth-quarter comeback in Conference Finals history since 1997, and the largest comeback for the Knicks in the NBA Playoffs since 1970.
- **Angle:** Jalen Brunson scored 38 points — 15 in the fourth quarter on 7-of-9 shooting — and either scored or assisted on 23 of the Knicks' final 32 points. Donovan Mitchell had 29 points and a career-high 6 steals, but was held to just 3 points in his final 14 minutes.
