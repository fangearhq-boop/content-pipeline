### STORY 1: Wembanyama's Historic Playoff Debut — Spurs Roll Past Blazers in Game 1
**Tier:** 1 | **Priority:** Breaking/Trending
**Status:** NEW STORY

Victor Wembanyama scored 35 points in his playoff debut — a Spurs franchise record — as San Antonio dominated Portland 111-98 in Game 1 on April 19. He shot 13-for-21 from the field including 5-for-6 from three, adding 5 rebounds, 2 blocks and 1 assist. His 21 first-half points set a league record for most in the first half of a playoff debut since 1997. He broke Tim Duncan's previous Spurs franchise record of 32. Game 2 saw Portland bounce back 106-103 (Scoot Henderson 31 pts), tying the series 1-1.

**Key Facts:**
- Wembanyama 35 pts, 13/21 FG, 5/6 3PT, 5 reb, 2 blk (Game 1)
- Broke Tim Duncan's Spurs record of 32 pts in playoff debut
- 21 first-half points: league record since 1997
- First Spurs playoff game without Pop as coach since 1999
- First Spurs playoff appearance and win since 2019
- Supporting cast: Stephon Castle 17, De'Aaron Fox 17, Devin Vassell 15
- Portland: Avdija 30/10 reb (Game 1); Henderson 31 pts (Game 2, W 106-103)
- Series tied 1-1
