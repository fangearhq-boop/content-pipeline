### STORY 1: Austin Reaves Signs Historic $185M Max Deal with Lakers

#### Long-Form Post — 9:15 AM ET

The year-by-year breakdown: $41.3M in 2026-27, $44.6M in 2027-28, $47.9M in 2028-29, and a player option for $51.2M in 2029-30.

He didn't know how the numbers would come together. And yet every morning he woke up, he said, it was the same thought: "I hope we can get this done today."

They did.

Reaves, 28, averaged 23.3 PPG, 5.5 APG, and 4.7 RPG this season on 49% shooting. He went undrafted in 2021 out of Oklahoma. He has now signed a deal that the greatest undrafted players in league history — guys who went on to All-Star careers — never came close to matching.

Rob Pelinka said it right: "He started his journey here as a Laker, and has made it very clear to us that he wants his journey to continue as a Laker."
