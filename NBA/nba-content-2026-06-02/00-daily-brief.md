### STORY 3: Gregg Popovich's Shadow — The Legend Still Drives These Spurs
**Tier:** 2
**Category:** Human Interest / Spurs Feature
**Why It Matters:** Gregg Popovich, 77, suffered a stroke in November 2024 that ended his coaching career. He now holds the title of President of Spurs Basketball and functions as coach emeritus — regularly attending practices on a cane, working alongside Tim Duncan in rehab, and advising head coach Mitch Johnson. Victor Wembanyama has spoken emotionally about Pop's influence. The Spurs are in the Finals because of what Popovich built — and he is still in the building.

**Tier:** 2
**Category:** Statistical Feature / Knicks
