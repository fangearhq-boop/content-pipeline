East Play-In Race Heads to Final Day: Hawks and Raptors Hold the Line

By Marcus Cole | Hoop Heroes

April 11, 2026

One regular-season game remains, and the Eastern Conference play-in race has not finished deciding who escapes the bracket chaos and who enters it.

After Friday's results, the Atlanta Hawks hold the No. 5 seed — the last position that comes with a direct playoff berth without passing through the play-in filter. The Toronto Raptors cling to No. 6, the other guaranteed spot, and will host the Brooklyn Nets on Sunday in a game they must win to protect it. Every team from seventh through tenth will be competing in the SoFi Play-In Tournament starting April 14, needing two wins just to reach the first round of the playoffs.

What Sunday Decides

The final day of the regular season, April 12, will sort the remaining East picture. For the Hawks, the goal is simple: win and lock up No. 5. For the Raptors, a win over Brooklyn keeps them at No. 6 and out of the play-in entirely — a critical outcome given that direct playoff spots allow teams to arrive in the postseason fresher and on better footing.

If Toronto loses on Sunday, they could drop below No. 6 and into the play-in bracket, adding a brutal best-of-one-or-two gauntlet before any real playoff run begins. The Nets are not a playoff team, but they have played competitive basketball late in the year and won't simply roll over.

The Play-In Field

The Charlotte Hornets and Miami Heat have already been confirmed as the No. 9 and No. 10 seeds — they will enter the play-in on April 15, needing to win one game to advance to face the 7/8 loser and earn the eighth playoff spot. The Orlando Magic and Philadelphia 76ers are locked into seeds 7 and 8, where they will face a slightly more direct path through the play-in bracket.

The SoFi Play-In Tournament broadcasts exclusively on Prime Video and has, in recent years, delivered some of the most dramatic single-game moments of the entire postseason. For every team involved, there is no margin for error — one loss on the wrong night ends your season before the brackets even properly begin.

What's Next

The NBA regular season concludes Sunday, April 12. After that, the SoFi Play-In Tournament runs April 14-17 on Prime Video. The first round of the NBA Playoffs begins April 18. The East bracket — with the Detroit Pistons as No. 1, the Boston Celtics at No. 2, and the New York Knicks at No. 3 — will be fully set after Sunday's final results. For the Hawks and Raptors, Sunday is not just a game. It is a referendum on whether they earned a clean shot at the postseason.
