### STORY 7: SoFi Play-In Tournament Preview

#### Long-Form Post — 7:00 PM ET

The 2026 SoFi NBA Play-In Tournament is three days away, and it promises to be one of the most unpredictable stretches of basketball of the entire year.

Here is how it works: the teams that finish seventh through tenth in each conference get a second chance at the playoffs — but they have to earn it. The No. 7 and No. 8 seeds face each other; the winner goes directly to the playoffs as that conference's seventh seed. The loser gets one more chance. The No. 9 and No. 10 seeds also face each other, with the winner advancing to face the 7/8 loser. The team that wins that game becomes the eighth playoff seed.

In the West, the Phoenix Suns are expected to be the No. 7 seed, likely facing either the Portland Trail Blazers or the LA Clippers at No. 8. The Warriors — who rested Steph Curry late in the season — are in the mix for No. 9 or 10, meaning Golden State could yet reach the playoffs. All Play-In games air exclusively on Prime Video starting Tuesday, April 14.

In the East, the field will be set after Sunday's final games.

The NBA Playoffs begin April 18. But first — four days of Play-In chaos.
