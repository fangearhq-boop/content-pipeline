## Story Angles

### STORY 1: Knicks 109-93 Cavaliers — ECF Game 2
**Primary angle:** Double career-high game from Hart and Brunson in the same game is a rarity that demands attention. The Knicks are not a one-man team — they have a second scorer who can go nuclear.
**Secondary angle:** Cleveland's 2-0 deficit is historically dire. The pressure now falls on Mitchell and Harden to salvage something at home before the series becomes a formality.
**Article hook:** "Two players set playoff career-highs in the same game. The Knicks needed both."

### STORY 2: WCF Game 3 Tonight
**Primary angle:** Three key players (Fox, Harper, Williams) are all questionable for a Game 3 that could define the Western Conference Finals. Wembanyama may have to carry the Spurs single-handed.
**Secondary angle:** The injury situation is as much a story as the basketball. If the Spurs lose Fox and Harper, they are sending a significantly diminished roster against the defending conference champion.
**Article hook:** "Wembanyama vs. the Thunder. Three question marks at AT&T Center tonight."

### STORY 3: WCF Game 2 — Thunder 122-113 Spurs
**Primary angle:** SGA was dominant (30/9/+14), but the real story was the 21-9 turnover gap and the injuries that changed the game's complexion.
**Secondary angle:** Wembanyama's 21/17 line was impressive but not enough. The Spurs' role players need to handle the ball better for Wemby to carry the load offensively.

### STORY 4: ECF Game 3 Preview
**Primary angle:** Cleveland's season is essentially on the line. Road teams in the ECF have rarely come back from 3-0 in NBA history. The Cavs must win Saturday.
**Secondary angle:** The matchup — Brunson/Hart vs. Mitchell/Harden — played out one way in New York. Home court in Cleveland could change the dynamic.

### STORY 5: Josh Hart Spotlight
**Primary angle:** Hart is the embodiment of the "next man up" Knicks culture. A second-round pick who now has back-to-back impactful ECF performances.
**Secondary angle:** His shooting evolution — 5-of-11 from three in Game 2 — has expanded his threat level beyond hustle plays.
