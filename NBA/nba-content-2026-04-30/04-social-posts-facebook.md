#### Long-Form Post — 9:00 AM ET

Cade and Banchero are the first pair of No. 1 overall picks to each score 40 or more points in the same playoff game since Allen Iverson and Shaquille O'Neal did it in the 2001 NBA Finals. Let that comparison sink in.

Orlando still leads the series 3-2, and Game 6 is Friday night in Orlando. If Banchero plays like that again, the Magic will advance. If Cade keeps this up, we could be headed to a Game 7 that nobody wants to miss.
