## STORY 2: Lendeborg Historic Achievement

- Yaxel Lendeborg is only the 2nd player in history to win both an NCAA national championship and an NBA Summer League championship in the same calendar year (Source: Seminoles.com, Yahoo Sports)
- The first was Davion Mitchell (Baylor 2021 NCAA champion, Sacramento Kings Summer League champion 2021)
- Warriors won Summer League Championship July 20, 94-90 over Memphis Grizzlies
- Lendeborg stats: 21 pts, 10 reb, 2 ast, 2 stl in 23 minutes — Championship Game MVP
- Deivon Smith: 21 pts, 9-for-14 shooting off the bench
- LJ Cryer: 15 pts, 4 three-pointers
- Warriors trailed by 12 in the 4th quarter before rallying
- This is Warriors' 2nd Las Vegas Summer League championship, first since 2013
- Lendeborg was selected No. 11 overall in the 2026 NBA Draft
