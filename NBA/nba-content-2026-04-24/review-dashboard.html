Nikola Jokic reportedly shot 7-for-26 from the floor, and Minnesota outscored Denver 68-34 in the paint while holding the Nuggets to just 11 first-quarter points — a franchise postseason record. Denver built nothing against Minnesota's defense. The full impact of Edwards' injury will be clearer before Game 4.
