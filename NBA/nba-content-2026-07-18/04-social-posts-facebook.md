### STORY 3: Kawhi Trade Frozen

**Long-Form Post** | Window: 11:00 AM ET

The Kawhi Leonard saga just got more complicated.

The NBA's investigation into Kawhi's eligibility to be traded to the Toronto Raptors has expanded to include a second, previously undisclosed endorsement deal — adding another layer to what's already a sprawling case.

The original investigation centered on a $28 million Aspiration endorsement deal that was allegedly structured in a way that violated league rules. Now a second deal is under review. Former team executive Steve Sanberg has already been convicted and sentenced to 14 years in prison, complicating the legal landscape further.

The agreed trade — Kawhi returning to Toronto in exchange for Brandon Ingram, Gradey Dick, two future first-round picks, two second-round picks, and a 2027 first-round swap — remains frozen. The Raptors declined to assume risk of any NBA-imposed penalties. No timeline for the investigation's conclusion has been given.

Kawhi is 35 years old and, for now, in limbo.

### STORY 5: Harden–Cavs Restructure

**Long-Form Post** | Window: 1:00 PM ET

James Harden just turned down $42.3 million. Voluntarily.

The veteran guard declined his player option with the Cleveland Cavaliers and is working on a new multi-year deal with the team. This is not a departure — it's a restructure, and the timing is no coincidence.

With LeBron James expected to announce his free agency destination next week, Cleveland is actively managing its cap sheet to make room. Harden taking a new, likely more team-friendly deal could free up the kind of cap flexibility that makes a LeBron pursuit viable.

Harden was Cleveland's backcourt cornerstone in 2025-26, and the Cavaliers clearly want him back. But they want to build smart. Declining the player option gives both sides a chance to structure something that works financially for the long haul — and potentially puts the Cavs in the best position possible to land the biggest free agent on the market.

The Cavs are making every move they can before LeBron decides. This is one of them.
