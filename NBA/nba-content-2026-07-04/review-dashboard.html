### Long-Form Post 🟡💜 THE POST-LEBRON LAKERS ARE MOVING FAST LeBron is gone — and the Los Angeles Lakers are not standing still. In the last 24 hours, the Lakers have traded Deandre Ayton to the Washington Wizards (receiving Jaden Hardy and two future second-round picks in 2031 and 2032), signed guard Collin Sexton, signed wing Quentin Grimes, and added center Sandro Mamukelashvili. This comes after already landing Walker Kessler from Utah on a 4-year, $130 million deal — the defensive anchor the rebuild needs. What is taking shape in Los Angeles: a Kessler-anchored defense with Sexton and Grimes providing perimeter energy and scoring punch. For the Wizards, Ayton gives them a veteran big man alongside No. 1 overall pick AJ Dybantsa, who just signed his 4-year, $66.91 million rookie deal.
