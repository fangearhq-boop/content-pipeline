### STORY 1: Jeremiah Fears — 40 Points

**Long-Form Post**
Window: 9:00 AM ET

The New Orleans Pelicans had lost eight games in a row. The season was going sideways in the final week of the regular season. Then a 19-year-old named Jeremiah Fears decided enough was enough.

On Tuesday night, the Pelicans rookie guard erupted for 40 points against the Utah Jazz — going 17-of-29 from the field, grabbing 5 rebounds, dishing 6 assists, and snatching 3 steals in a 156-137 blowout victory. He was named NBA Player of the Night.

By the time the night was over, Fears had set the Pelicans franchise record for most points scored in a game by a rookie. He also reportedly became the youngest guard in NBA history to drop 40 points in an NBA game, according to Yahoo Sports and NBA.com.

None of that was expected when the season started. None of it was promised. But basketball has a way of revealing who people are under pressure — and Jeremiah Fears chose the biggest spotlight to show the world exactly who he is.

New Orleans ended an 8-game losing streak. The rookie history books got a new entry. Not a bad Tuesday night.

**Long-Form Post**
