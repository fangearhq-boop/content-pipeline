Kawhi Leonard Trade Still Frozen — Investigation Update
NOT VERIFIEDFACEBOOK
### Long-Form Post The Kawhi Leonard situation remains one of the most complicated stories in basketball. A potential trade away from the Los Angeles Clippers is still frozen — and the reason is a legal and financial scandal that stretches far beyond the basketball court. The NBA is investigating the Aspiration Sports fraud case, which involves convicted executive Alex Sanberg, a 14-year prison sentence, allegations that Sanberg defrauded the Clippers organization of millions through fake endorsement deals and arena naming rights contracts, and a personal $60 million investment by owner Steve Ballmer that he claims was lost to fraud. Commissioner Adam Silver wants the investigation wrapped before approving any Kawhi trade. Multiple teams around the league have made it known they're interested in acquiring Leonard, who has one year remaining on his contract. This creates a genuinely difficult situation for all parties. Kawhi has no timeline on when he can be moved. The Clippers can't clean up their cap situation. And teams hoping to add him can only wait. When do you think the Kawhi investigation resolves?
