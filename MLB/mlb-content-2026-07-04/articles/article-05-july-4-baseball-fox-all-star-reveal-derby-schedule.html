Independence Day Baseball: All-Star Starters Revealed Tonight, Derby Next Week, Game on July 14

By Ryan Calloway

July 4, 2026

The Fourth of July is one of MLB's best broadcast days, and 2026 has an extra layer of significance built in: the complete 2026 All-Star starting lineups are being revealed live on FOX tonight.

Here is everything you need to know about what's on today, this weekend, and across the next 10 days as baseball's All-Star Weekend approaches.

Tonight: All-Star Selection Show, FOX, 7:30 PM ET

The headline event of the July 4 broadcast is the 2026 MLB All-Star Game Selection Show, which begins at 7:30 PM ET on FOX. The show reveals the Phase 2 voting results, announcing the full starting lineups for both the American and National League All-Star teams.

Phase 2 voting closed July 2. Bobby Witt Jr. of the Royals held a 72% lead at AL shortstop, Yordan Alvarez led at NL DH with an OPS of 1.031, and Freddie Freeman led NL first base at 59%. Shohei Ohtani (NL DH) and Ernie Clement (AL 2B) are already confirmed as automatic starters.

FOX also carries regular-season games on the holiday — a full afternoon and evening of baseball leading into the All-Star reveal broadcast.

Sunday July 5: All 15 Games on Peacock

Sunday's slate is unique: every MLB game — all 15 — airs exclusively on Peacock. It's the largest single-day baseball broadcast on the streaming platform, and worth noting for fans accustomed to finding games on local channels. If you're watching baseball on Sunday, you need Peacock.

July 13: Home Run Derby on Netflix, Citizens Bank Park

One week from today, the Home Run Derby makes history by airing on Netflix — the first time the competition has been on a streaming platform. Kyle Schwarber and Junior Caminero are confirmed in the Derby field. The setting is Citizens Bank Park in Philadelphia, which means Schwarber is competing in his home ballpark. Additional participants will be announced after tonight's All-Star selections are released.

July 14: All-Star Game, Citizens Bank Park, FOX

The 2026 MLB All-Star Game is July 14 at Citizens Bank Park — the 96th edition of the Midsummer Classic, hosted by the Philadelphia Phillies. FOX carries the broadcast. Full rosters are announced on July 6, two days after tonight's starting lineup reveal.

Philadelphia hasn't hosted an All-Star Game since 1996. The return comes with a sold-out stadium, a city primed for the spotlight, and a roster of players who have made 2026 one of the most compelling seasons in recent memory.

What's Next

Tonight at 7:30 PM ET on FOX is where the next 10 days begin. Watch for Witt Jr.'s Phase 2 result at shortstop, the full NL and AL starting lineups, and listen for the announcement of reserve selections and pitchers on July 6. Then, mark July 13 (Netflix) and July 14 (FOX) as baseball's two biggest broadcast events of the summer. The All-Star break is the best week on the baseball calendar. It starts tonight.
