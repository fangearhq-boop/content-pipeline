### STORY 1: Red Sox Win 12th Straight — Comeback From 6-3 Down

#### Long-Form Post — 8:00 AM ET

The Boston Red Sox were losing 6-3 entering the seventh inning Saturday night.

They won 7-6 anyway.

Wilyer Abreu — who has quietly become one of the most important bats in this lineup — hit a two-run home run off Evan Cleavinger to get Boston within one. Two weeks ago the Red Sox were being discussed as sellers. Nobody is calling them sellers anymore.
