2. Francisco Lindor Is On The Market. The Mets Are Done Competing.

**Selected:** The Mets Are Selling. Francisco Lindor — Signed Through 2031 — Is Available.

- **Magnitude of the move:** Lindor signed through 2031 — you don't shop franchise cornerstones mid-contract if you're just selling depth. This is a full rebuild.

### Supporting Context
- Lindor's contract: signed through 2031, roughly $30M+ per season remaining
- The Mets finishing below .500 despite massive payroll has happened before; this feels like ownership forcing a rebuild
