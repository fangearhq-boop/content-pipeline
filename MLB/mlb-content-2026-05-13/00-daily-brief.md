Mike Yastrzemski was 2-for-2 with a 2-run HR (5th inning, Chop House in right field) and 3 RBI. Braves are 29-13 (NL's best). Cubs are 27-15. Braves' staff leads MLB in fewest runs allowed per game (3.44). Cubs have had TWO 10-game win streaks this season (first team since 1955 Brooklyn Dodgers to do it in first 40 decisions). Series continues May 13 (game 2).

Schwarber's HR surge is powering the offense. Mattingly, 65, previously managed Dodgers (5 years) and Marlins (7 years). His son Preston Mattingly is the Phillies' GM.
- **Henry Bolte:** The A's called up outfield prospect Henry Bolte for Tuesday's game (May 12). In 37 Triple-A games this season: .348/.418/.658, 12 HR, 7 2B, 3 3B, 17 SB in 19 attempts. Had a 12-for-12 streak in plate appearances at Triple-A (12 straight hits including 9 extra-base hits). Ranked No. 5 among A's prospects by MLB.com. He was drafted in 2022 (2nd round, 56th overall pick).

He was the first SS since 1900 to hit 5+ HRs and steal 5+ bases in his team's first 16 games of the season. Hard hit % of 50.9, avg exit velocity 93.9 mph, barrel % 15.5.
