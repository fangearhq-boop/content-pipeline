# Ballpark Banter — Seasonal Calendar

Use this to calibrate story selection and tiering throughout the MLB season.

## Season Timeline

| Period | Dates (approx) | What's Happening | Tiering Notes |
|--------|----------------|------------------|---------------|
| Hot Stove / Offseason | Nov – mid Feb | Free agency, trades, Winter Meetings, arbitration | Big signings Tier 1; rumors Tier 2-3 |
| Spring Training | Mid Feb – late Mar | Cactus League + Grapefruit League, roster battles | Camp standouts Tier 2; injury news Tier 1-2 |
| Opening Day | Late Mar / early Apr | Season openers across MLB | Always Tier 1 |
| April Baseball | April | Early season, small sample sizes, first impressions | Hot starts Tier 1-2; standings context Tier 3 |
| May-June | May – June | Samples stabilize, contenders emerge, MLB Draft (June) | Series results Tier 2; streaks Tier 1 |
| All-Star Break | Mid July | Midsummer Classic, Home Run Derby | All-Star selections Tier 1; Derby Tier 1 |
| Trade Deadline | Late Jul / early Aug | Last day to make deals | All deadline moves Tier 1 |
| Stretch Run | Aug – Sep | Playoff push, September call-ups | Standings Tier 1; every meaningful game matters |
| Postseason | October | Wild Card, LDS, LCS, World Series | Everything Tier 1 |
| Awards Season | November | MVP, Cy Young, ROY, Gold Glove, Silver Slugger | Major awards Tier 1; others Tier 2 |
| Winter Meetings | Early Dec | Free agency frenzy, trades, Rule 5 Draft | Signings Tier 1; rumors Tier 2 |
| HOF & Arbitration | Jan – Feb | Hall of Fame announcements, arbitration hearings | HOF always Tier 1; arbitration Tier 2-3 |

## Key Annual Events

### January
- Hall of Fame announcement (always Tier 1)
- Arbitration filing deadline and hearings
- HOF weekend coverage and debates

### February
- Pitchers and catchers report (mid-Feb) — always Tier 1 (marks the unofficial start of baseball)
- Position players report (late Feb)
- Cactus League and Grapefruit League games begin (late Feb)
- Early spring training storylines: roster battles, comeback players, new acquisitions

### March
- Spring training games continue
- Roster cuts and option decisions (late Mar)
- World Baseball Classic (every 4 years — check schedule)
- Opening Day (late Mar or early Apr) — always Tier 1

### April
- Opening Day / Opening Week (if late Mar didn't cover it)
- Early-season surprises and breakouts
- First monthly awards watch
- Small sample size caution — temper hot/cold start takes

### May
- Samples start to stabilize (30+ games)
- Interleague play windows
- Contenders and pretenders begin to separate
- Player of the Month / Pitcher of the Month

### June
- MLB Draft (First-Year Player Draft) — prospect coverage ramps up
- College World Series (baseball, not softball)
- Mid-season All-America/All-Star speculation begins
- International signing period (July 15 start — previews begin in June)

### July
- All-Star Game and festivities (mid-July) — always Tier 1
- Home Run Derby — always Tier 1
- Trade deadline (late July / early August) — always Tier 1
- Second-half storylines and adjustments
- International signing period opens (July 15)

### August
- Post-deadline roster analysis
- Stretch run begins in earnest
- Playoff race coverage intensifies
- Waiver trade window activity

### September
- September call-ups (roster expansion)
- Clinching scenarios and magic numbers
- Final regular season series
- Individual milestones and stat chases (batting titles, HR leaders, Cy Young races)
- Postseason berth clinching — always Tier 1

### October
- Wild Card Series
- League Division Series (ALDS/NLDS)
- League Championship Series (ALCS/NLCS)
- World Series
- Everything in October is Tier 1

### November
- Award announcements: MVP, Cy Young, Rookie of the Year, Manager of the Year
- Gold Glove, Silver Slugger, Hank Aaron Award
- Free agency begins (5 days after World Series)
- Early free agent signings and opt-outs
- GM Meetings

### December
- Winter Meetings (early December) — always Tier 1 coverage week
- Free agency frenzy — major signings land
- Rule 5 Draft
- Non-tender deadline (early December)
- International signings continue

## Story Selection Reminders

- **Walk-offs and no-hitters are always Tier 1** — peak fan emotion, always the lead
- **Milestones tier up** — 3000 hits, 500 HR, 300 wins, perfect games are historic
- **Trade deadline content starts 2 weeks early** — rumors are Tier 2+ in mid-to-late July
- **All 30 teams get coverage** — don't default to Yankees/Dodgers/big markets every day
- **Rivalry series always tier up** — Yankees-Red Sox, Dodgers-Giants, Cubs-Cardinals are minimum Tier 2
- **Injury news for star players is Tier 1-2** — especially aces and MVP candidates
- **Small market overperformance is always a story** — Rays, Guardians, Royals type runs deserve coverage
- **October: everything is Tier 1** — there is no Tier 3 content during the postseason
