## Team Sites (Breaking News)

All 30 official team sites follow the pattern `mlb.com/[team]` — check the relevant team site for breaking roster moves, injury updates, and official announcements before secondary sources.

## Search Strategy

When researching daily MLB content, run **8-10 web searches** across news and fan sentiment:

### News Searches (5-7 searches)

1. **Scores/results**: "MLB scores last night" or "MLB results [date]"
2. **Transactions**: "MLB trades today" or "MLB roster moves" or "MLB Trade Rumors"
3. **Standings**: "MLB standings" or "AL/NL wild card standings"
4. **Injuries**: "MLB injury news today" or "[star player] injury update"
5. **Pitching matchups**: "MLB probable pitchers today" or "MLB pitching matchups"
6. **Breaking news**: "MLB news today" or "baseball news [current date]"
7. **Prospects/minors**: "MLB prospect call-up" or "top MLB prospects" (when relevant)

### Fan Sentiment Searches (2-3 searches)

8. **Reddit**: "site:reddit.com/r/baseball" — surfaces top upvoted fan takes and viral moments
9. **Viral moments**: "MLB highlight" or "baseball viral" — catches bat flips, defensive gems, fan interactions
10. **Debate topics**: "MLB hot take" or "baseball debate" — fuel for opinionated content
