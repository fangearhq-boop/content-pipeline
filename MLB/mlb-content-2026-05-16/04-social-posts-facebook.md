That record is 8 — set by Dale Long of the Pittsburgh Pirates in 1956, matched by Ken Griffey Jr. with the Seattle Mariners in 1993, and matched again by Don Mattingly of the New York Yankees in 1987.

Schwarber is the first player this season to reach 20 home runs. He's done it in 45 games. He joins Cy Williams — who hit 20 HRs in the first 45 games of the 1923 season — as the only Phillies players to reach that mark that early.

We are watching something rare. Whether the streak ends tonight or continues, what Schwarber has done in this 7-game stretch is going to be in baseball record books forever.

Watch the game tonight. This is the kind of thing you'll want to tell people you saw.
