### STORY 3: MISIOROWSKI — 105.5 MPH, FASTEST STARTING PITCHER IN STATCAST ERA

#### Facebook Post — 12:30 PM ET

On June 26 against the Cubs, he threw a 105.5 mph fastball. It is the fastest pitch ever recorded by a starting pitcher in the Statcast era — breaking his own previous record of 104.5 mph, which he set earlier this season.

He has thrown 42 of the 50 fastest starting-pitcher pitches ever recorded. All of them in 2026.

When asked about it afterward, Misiorowski said: "I slipped."

He didn't seem impressed. The hitters he faces are.

The Milwaukee Brewers are 53-31, the best record in the NL. Misiorowski's ERA, his strikeouts, his velocity — all of it points to one thing as the second half approaches: if the Brewers are going to October, he's why.
