### Long-Form Post The 60-home-run club has four members. Barry Bonds (73 in 2001). Mark McGwire (70 in 1998, 65 in 1999). Sammy Sosa (66 in 1998, 65 in 1999, 63 in 2001). And Babe Ruth (60 in 1927). Kyle Schwarber has 22 home runs and it's early June. He hit his 20th on May 15 — the earliest date in MLB history any player has reached that milestone. His pace projects to somewhere around 66 HRs over a full season, which would put him in that very exclusive conversation. Aaron Judge is right behind him at 20. The Phillies slugger vs. the Yankees captain — the HR race of the early 2026 season. Schwarber's path to 60 isn't automatic. Paces slow, slumps happen, and the second half is always harder.
