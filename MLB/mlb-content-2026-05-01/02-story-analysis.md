### STORY 4:
**Title:** NL Central All Five Teams Still Above .500 Entering May — History in the Making
**Tier:** 2
**Type:** FOLLOW UP
**Priority Score:** 8

**Angle:** As May begins, all five NL Central teams remain above .500 — an historically rare occurrence. This is the second-latest date in baseball history that all five teams in a division have simultaneously been above .500 (only May 29, 2004 was later). If it holds, it would be the first time in the Divisional Era (since 1969) that a full division finished above .500. The math is cruel: only three NL Central teams make the playoffs. Two winning teams will miss the postseason. The Reds lead, with rookies like Sal Stewart mashing. Every division series matters in ways that don't happen elsewhere.
