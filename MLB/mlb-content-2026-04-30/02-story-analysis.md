### STORY 3:
**Title:** NL Central — The Only MLB Division with All 5 Teams Over .500
**Tier:** 2
**Type:** NEW
**Priority Score:** 9

**Angle:** The NL Central is the only division in baseball with every team over .500, and it is historically remarkable. As of April 28, all five teams are winning: the Reds lead at 16-9, Cubs, Cardinals, Pirates, and Brewers all above the break-even mark. Three playoff spots for five deserving teams — someone with a winning record is staying home.
