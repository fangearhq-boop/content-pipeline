# Chicago Cubs Fan HQ — Seasonal Calendar

Use this to calibrate story selection and tiering throughout the MLB season.

## Season Timeline

| Period | Dates (approx) | What's Happening | Tiering Notes |
|--------|----------------|------------------|---------------|
| Hot Stove / Offseason | Nov – mid Feb | Free agency, trades, Winter Meetings | Big signings Tier 1; rumors Tier 2-3 |
| Spring Training | Mid Feb – late Mar | Cactus League (Mesa, AZ), roster battles | Camp battles Tier 2; injury news Tier 1-2 |
| Opening Day | Late Mar / early Apr | Season opener | Always Tier 1 |
| April Baseball | April | Early season, small samples, first impressions | Hot starts Tier 1-2; standings context Tier 3 |
| May-June | May – June | Sample sizes stabilize, standings take shape | Series wins/losses Tier 2; streaks Tier 1 |
| All-Star Break | Mid July | Midsummer Classic, trade deadline buildup | All-Star selections Tier 1; deadline rumors Tier 1-2 |
| Trade Deadline | Late July | Last day to make deals | All deadline moves Tier 1 |
| Stretch Run | Aug – Sep | Playoff push, expanded rosters (Sep 1) | Every game matters; standings Tier 1 |
| Playoffs | October | Wild Card, NLDS, NLCS, World Series | Everything Tier 1 |
| World Series | Late Oct | Championship round | Everything Tier 1 |
| Offseason | Nov – Feb | Awards, free agency, Winter Meetings, trades | Major signings Tier 1; minor moves Tier 2-3 |

## Key Annual Events

### February – March (Spring Training)
- Pitchers and catchers report (mid-Feb)
- Position players report (late Feb)
- Cactus League games begin (late Feb)
- Roster cuts and option decisions (late Mar)
- Cubs Convention (usually January — check dates annually)

### April (Opening Month)
- Opening Day (late Mar or early Apr)
- Home opener at Wrigley Field (always Tier 1)
- Early-season NL Central matchups
- First monthly awards watch

### May – June
- Interleague play windows
- NL Central series (Cubs vs Cardinals, Brewers, Reds, Pirates)
- MLB Draft (mid-July — prospect coverage ramps up in June)

### July
- All-Star Game and festivities
- Trade deadline (July 31) — Cubs as buyers or sellers?
- Second-half storylines

### August – September
- Stretch run — every NL Central game matters
- September call-ups (roster expansion)
- Clinching scenarios
- Final regular season series

### October
- Wild Card Series
- NLDS
- NLCS
- World Series

### November – January
- Gold Glove, Silver Slugger, MVP, Cy Young awards
- Winter Meetings (early December)
- Free agent signings
- Rule 5 Draft
- Arbitration hearings (January-February)

## Cubs-Specific Hooks

- **Wrigley Field** — 110+ year history, iconic venue, "Friendly Confines"
- **Curse narratives** — 2016 broke the 108-year drought — reference for comeback stories
- **Division rivalry** — Cardinals series are always Tier 1-2 minimum
- **Prospect pipeline** — Iowa Cubs (AAA), Tennessee Smokies (AA), South Bend Cubs (A)
- **Fan culture** — Bleacher Bums, 7th inning stretch, rooftops, Wrigleyville nightlife

## Story Selection Reminders

- **Cardinals series always tier up** — minimum Tier 2, usually Tier 1
- **Walk-offs and extra innings are Tier 1** — fan engagement peaks
- **Milestones tier up** — career records, franchise records, perfect games
- **Trade deadline content starts 2 weeks before** — rumors are Tier 2+ in late July
- **Injury news for key players is always Tier 1-2** — especially pitchers
- **No-hitters, combined no-hitters: always Tier 1**
