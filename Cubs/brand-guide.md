## Social Media Voice

### X/Twitter Voice
- 50% informative (news, stats, recaps) / 50% bold-passionate-humor (takes, jabs, fan energy)
- Conversational, not corporate — write like a fan, not a press release
- Strong opinions backed by stats
- Humor: witty observations, rival jabs (Cardinals > Brewers > White Sox)
- Max 1-2 emoji per tweet, used for emphasis
- Max 1 exclamation mark per tweet
- Exactly 3 hashtags per post
- Primary: #Cubs (every post)
- Secondary pool: #GoCubs #FlyTheW #CubsBaseball #WrigleyField #MLB #NorthSiders #ChicagoCubs #CubNation
- ALL CAPS sparingly for emphasis on key stats or moments (max 1-2 words per tweet)
