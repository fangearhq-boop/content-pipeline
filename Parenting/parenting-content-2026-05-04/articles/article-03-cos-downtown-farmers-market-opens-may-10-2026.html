COS Downtown Farmers Market Opens Sunday May 10 — Free Admission, Mother's Day Weekend

By Marcus Rivera | COS Parenting | May 4, 2026

The Colorado Springs Downtown Farmers Market opens its 2026 season this Sunday, May 10 — which also happens to be Mother's Day. Hours are 9 AM to 2 PM at Acacia Park, 115 E Platte Ave in downtown Colorado Springs. Admission is free, no reservations required.

The market then runs every Sunday through October 25, 2026. If you're looking to establish a weekly family routine that's inexpensive, outdoors, and genuinely connects your kids to local food and makers, this is one of Colorado Springs' better options.

Why Opening Day on Mother's Day Works

Brunch reservations are hard to get and often expensive. The farmers market is neither. Opening day of the season is typically one of the most energetic markets of the year — vendors are excited, the selection is at its spring peak, and the weather in early May in Colorado Springs is often ideal for spending a few hours outdoors.

Acacia Park is centrally located and walkable from most of downtown, with easy street parking and a playground area that gives kids a reason to stay after you've done your browsing. A trip to the market, a coffee from a local vendor, and a walk around the park is a low-cost, low-stress Mother's Day that doesn't require anyone to wear nice shoes.

What to Expect in May

Colorado's growing season means May markets tend to feature cool-weather crops: leafy greens, radishes, spring onions, herbs, and greenhouse tomatoes. Local honey, baked goods, and artisan products are available year-round. As the season progresses into June and July, expect more variety — berries, corn, peppers, and stone fruits appear as the season matures.

The market is family-friendly by design. Kids can see where food comes from, talk to the people who grew it, and make small choices about what goes in the bag. It's a low-key form of food education that tends to land well with younger children who like having a role to play.

What's Next

The Acacia Park Farmers Market runs every Sunday from May 10 through October 25, 2026. There's no reason to pre-plan — just show up between 9 AM and 2 PM on any Sunday. For families who want more information about vendors or seasonal availability, the market is on social media under its downtown COS listings. If you've never been, opening weekend is the easiest possible time to start — the market is at its most welcoming when the season is fresh.
