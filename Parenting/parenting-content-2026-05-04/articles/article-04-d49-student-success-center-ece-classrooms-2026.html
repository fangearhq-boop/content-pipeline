D49 Opening New Student Success Center in August — Two New Early Childhood Education Classrooms

By Jenna Park | COS Parenting | May 4, 2026

School District 49 is expanding its early learning options for the 2026–27 school year. D49's new Student Success Center will open in August 2026, and it includes two new early childhood education (ECE) classrooms — expanding the district's capacity to serve Colorado Springs' youngest learners in the years before kindergarten.

For families with children approaching school age, this is worth knowing about now. Early childhood enrollment in D49 tends to fill as spots become available, and getting on the district's radar before the formal enrollment window opens can make a difference.

Why Early Childhood Education Expansion Matters for D49 Families

D49 serves the northeast part of Colorado Springs — one of the faster-growing areas in the city — and demand for early childhood programming has consistently outpaced available seats. The Student Success Center represents an investment in that capacity, and the two new ECE classrooms add options for families who've been on waitlists or working around limited program availability in the district.

Early childhood education programs in Colorado public schools serve children who are too young for kindergarten but old enough to benefit from structured learning environments. Colorado's universal preschool program, which expanded significantly in recent years, has increased the number of families looking for district-based ECE options. D49's expansion aligns with that broader trend.

What D49 Families Should Do Now

If you have a child approaching kindergarten age — typically turning 5 by August 1, 2026 for the 2026–27 kindergarten class — visit d49.org for enrollment information and timelines. For ECE programs specifically, children are often eligible starting at age 3 or 4 depending on the program type and any special service eligibility.

Even if you're not sure your child qualifies for an ECE program specifically, contacting the district now to understand your options is the right move. D49 has community liaisons who can walk families through the enrollment process, eligibility screening, and which programs are available at the Student Success Center versus other D49 schools.

What's Next

The D49 Student Success Center is scheduled to open in August 2026 for the start of the new school year. Enrollment for D49 programs typically opens in spring — which means families interested in the new ECE classrooms for fall 2026 should contact the district promptly, as seats may be limited given the initial capacity of two classrooms. Visit d49.org for the latest enrollment timelines, eligibility information, and contact details for the early childhood team. The district's phone number and contact forms are available through the main site.
