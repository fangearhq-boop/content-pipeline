## STORY 1: CreateOn Pip-Cubes CPSC Recall

**Facts (HIGH confidence — cpsc.gov primary source):**
- Product: CreateOn Crayola-branded pip-Cubes, magnetic building cubes
- Sets: 24 or 27 cubes per set; Bold Colors and Glitter varieties
- Units recalled: approximately 9,400
- Retailers: Amazon website; Michaels stores nationwide and Michaels website
- Hazard: Magnets can become loose when seams separate; ingestion risk — magnets attract through intestinal walls causing perforations, twisting, blockage of intestines, blood poisoning, death
- Incidents/injuries: No injuries reported to CPSC as of recall date
- Consumer action: Stop using immediately; remove from children's reach; contact CreateOn for free replacement set
- Contact: 800-333-0549 (9:30 AM–4 PM CT, Monday–Friday); pipcuberecall@CreateOn.com; createon.com/recall/
- CPSC page: cpsc.gov/Recalls (search CreateOn Pip-Cubes)

**Confidence:** HIGH — sourced directly from cpsc.gov recall page and createon.com/pages/recalls.

**Story History check:** NEW STORY. Stephan Baby Boo Bunnie recall covered May 3. This is a distinct product and recall.
