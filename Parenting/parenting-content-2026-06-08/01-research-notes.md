### STORY 3: Summer Slide Prevention

**Source 1:** scholastic.com/parents — "Summer Slide Statistics and Prevention"
- Average loss: 25–30% of school learning over summer
- Math experiences more severe loss than reading
- Younger children more affected
- Up to 48.5 points of achievement gap across 5 elementary years linked to cumulative summer loss
- Confidence: HIGH (Scholastic citing research)

**Source 2:** thethinkacademy.com — "How to Prevent Summer Slide in 2026"
- Tip: 20–30 min structured review 3–4 times/week
- Tip: daily reading at least 20 min; let kids choose material
- Tip: math in daily activities (cooking, shopping, counting)
- Confidence: MEDIUM (single source for specific tip details)

**Source 3:** mayoclinichealthsystem.org — "Tips for preventing 'summer slide'"
- Reading 20 min/day
- Consistent structured practice
- Confidence: HIGH (Mayo Clinic — authoritative medical source)

**Source 4:** scientificamerican.com — "Summer Learning Loss Happens, but Kids Quickly Recover"
- Confirms summer learning loss is real; recovery is possible with intervention
- Confidence: HIGH
