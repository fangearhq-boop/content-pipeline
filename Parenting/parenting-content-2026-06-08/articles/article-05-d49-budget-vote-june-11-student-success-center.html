D49 Budget Vote June 11: Student Success Center and New Family Programs at Stake

By Jamie Rivera | June 8, 2026

District 49 families have a public meeting to be aware of this week. The D49 Board of Education meets Thursday, June 11, 2026, at Sand Creek High School to vote on the district's 2026–27 fiscal year budget. The budget includes funding for the Student Success Center, a new multi-service facility set to open in August 2026.

What the Budget Covers

The centerpiece of the 2026–27 budget discussion is the Student Success Center, which will operate out of the former Falcon Elementary of Technology campus in Falcon. The center is designed to serve multiple populations of D49 families in one location.

Programs slated to operate from the Student Success Center include:

  • Preschool: Offered Monday through Thursday for qualifying students
  • Base49: Before and after-school care program for D49 families
  • PEAK Programs: Supplemental academic and enrichment services
  • Elevates 18–21: Specialized support services for young adults with disabilities

The center represents a consolidation of services that D49 families in the Falcon area and eastern Colorado Springs would otherwise need to access at multiple locations.

What Parents Should Know About the Meeting

The June 11 meeting is a public Board of Education session, which means families can attend in person. If you have students enrolled in D49 programs, plan to enroll in preschool or Base49 for the 2026–27 year, or have children who receive PEAK or Elevates services, this budget vote directly affects the programs your family uses.

The meeting agenda and start time are available at d49.org. Families who cannot attend in person can typically review meeting materials and recordings through the district's website after the meeting concludes.

Context: D49's Budget Year

District 49 serves eastern Colorado Springs, Falcon, and surrounding communities. The district has been expanding programming in recent years, and the Student Success Center is one of the more significant infrastructure investments in the current budget cycle. The vote on June 11 sets the financial framework for the entire 2026–27 school year, which begins in August.

Families who have questions about specific programs or services can contact the district through d49.org ahead of the meeting.

What's Next

The D49 Board of Education meeting is Thursday, June 11, 2026, at Sand Creek High School. Meeting details, agenda, and agenda materials are available at d49.org. If the budget passes as proposed, the Student Success Center is expected to open in August 2026 ahead of the start of the school year. Watch d49.org and local news for any updates following the vote.
