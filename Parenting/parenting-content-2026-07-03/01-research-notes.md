## STORY 1: AAP Drowning Prevention Guidelines

**Confirmed (HIGH confidence):**
- AAP published updated "Prevention of Drowning" policy statement in July 2026 issue of Pediatrics
- Source: HealthyChildren.org, PeaceHealth.org, GuidelineCentral.com, Pristines.org
- "Touch supervision" is the key new recommendation emphasis: designated adult fully focused, within arm's reach of infants/toddlers/weak swimmers
- Swim lessons can begin after age 1 because increased mobility at that age raises drowning risk
- There is NO evidence that infant swim lessons (before age 1) reduce drowning incidence
- Four-sided pool fence with self-closing, self-latching gate at minimum 4 feet — isolates pool from house and rest of yard
- Children should not bathe alone until at least age 6
- No single method prevents all drowning — multiple layers of protection needed
- CPR training recommended for caregivers
- Source: HealthyChildren.org/AAP official

**MEDIUM confidence:**
- Exact date of July 2026 Pediatrics publication issue not pinpointed, but multiple sources reference July 2026

## STORY 4: Crayola PipCubes Recall

**Confirmed (HIGH confidence):**
- CreateOn recalls Crayola-branded PipCubes magnetic building cubes
- CPSC official recall page: cpsc.gov/Recalls/2026/CreateOn-Recalls-Pip-Cubes...
- About 9,400 units recalled
- Products: Bold Colors and Glitter varieties; sets of 24 or 27 cubes
- Hazard: magnets can come loose when seams separate; ingested high-powered magnets can attract each other inside body causing perforations, twisting, blockage of intestines, blood poisoning, and death
- 24-piece sets sold at Michaels nationwide and Michaels.com for about $20
- 27-piece sets sold on Amazon.com for about $35
- Sold from May 2025 to July 2025
- Remedy: stop using immediately, take away from children, contact CreateOn for replacement set; CreateOn provides pre-paid return label
- Source: CPSC.gov, FoodPoisoningBulletin.com, Mother.ly, WeareIowa.com

**Contact info:** CPSC.gov (createon.com/pages/recalls) — MEDIUM (specific phone not confirmed in search results)
