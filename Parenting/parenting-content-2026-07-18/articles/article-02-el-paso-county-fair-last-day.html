Today Is Your Last Chance: 121st Annual El Paso County Fair Closes Tonight in Calhan

By Jamie Rivera | COS Parenting

July 18, 2026 — Colorado Springs, CO

The 121st Annual El Paso County Fair wraps up tonight in Calhan. If your family has been planning to go and kept postponing the trip, today is the day.

The fair has been running since July 11 with an attendance goal of 25,000 visitors this year. It's one of the region's longest-running annual traditions, combining rodeo events, demolition derbies, auto races, carnival rides, livestock shows, and live entertainment into one all-day experience. It closes tonight, and it won't be back until next summer.

What the Fair Offers

The El Paso County Fair is a full-scale agricultural and entertainment event. It's not a city festival or a sponsored pop-up — it's a county fair in the traditional sense, with livestock competitions and agricultural exhibits alongside the carnival midway.

For families, the mix of events means there's something across all ages. The demolition derbies and auto races draw older kids and teens who want action and noise. The carnival rides and midway games work well for younger children. The livestock shows offer something educational that's different from anything you'll find in a city park or shopping center.

The rodeo events bring a specific energy that's hard to replicate elsewhere — crowd energy, live animals, athletes working with horses under the open sky. In El Paso County, that connection to ranching and agricultural heritage is real, not theatrical.

Getting There

Calhan is approximately 35 minutes east of downtown Colorado Springs on Highway 24. The drive is straightforward and almost entirely on one road. Plan to arrive earlier rather than later — if this is the final day and attendance has been tracking toward the 25,000-visitor goal, parking and crowds will reflect that.

Check elpasocountyfair.com before you leave for today's specific schedule, admission prices, and any last-day special events. Schedules for demolition derbies and rodeos are typically posted by time, so you can plan your arrival around the events that matter most to your family.

What's Next

Tonight's fair closes out the El Paso County Fair for 2026. If you go today, this week's fair becomes the reference point your kids will compare all future county fairs against.

For families looking ahead, Colorado Springs has more summer events on the calendar through August. The Backpack Bash free supply giveaway runs July 25 and August 1. Back-to-school dates are approaching for all three local districts: D49 on August 4, D11 on August 12, and D20 on August 13. Enjoy the fair season while it's still here.
