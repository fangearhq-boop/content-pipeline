### STORY 2: RECALL — GOPO Toys Pull String Teething Toy (70,410 Units)
**Tier:** 1
**Type:** NEW STORY (new CPSC recall, first coverage)
**Pillar:** National Parenting
**Sources:** cpsc.gov, dailyvoice.com, foxbusiness.com, consumerreports.org
**Posting window:** 10:00 AM MT

Product: GOPO TOYS Pull String Teething Toys — off-white disc shape with grey ball, six multi-colored silicone tentacle pull strings. 70,410 units. Sold on Amazon.com Aug 2023-Mar 2026, $11-15. Batch numbers: 250905, 250530, 250120, 240315, 231005, 230610 (on back of packaging). Hazard: strings reach back of child's throat, causing choking/respiratory distress. 3 confirmed incidents. Violates mandatory standard for toys. Required action: destroy toy (cut all strings, write "DESTROYED" on body), photograph, send photo to recalls@gopotoys.com. CPSC hotline: 1-800-638-2772.

**Tier:** 2

**Pillar:** National Parenting
**Sources:** cpsc.gov, kiro7.com, wftv.com, actionnewsjax.com

Product: CooCooBaby "Classic" and "Deluxe" Baby Loungers. Foam sleeping pad with padded bumpers and cloth cover. 2,355 units. Sold on Amazon.com and CooCooBaby website, Dec 2024-Mar 2026, $35-70. ### STORY 5: D11 Labor Dispute — 300+ Days Without a Master Agreement
**Tier:** 2
**Type:** NEW STORY (300+ day milestone update; May 2026 picket adds fresh angle)
**Pillar:** Local News
**Sources:** krdo.com, koaa.com, coloradoea.org
**Posting window:** 5:00 PM MT

Colorado Springs District 11 (D11) severed its master agreement with the Colorado Springs Education Association (CSEA) in late 2024/early 2025 — ending a contract that had been in place since 1968. As of May 2026, it's been 300+ days without that agreement. Educators and parents picketed the D11 Board of Education meeting in May 2026. The union says the district refuses to engage in collective bargaining. The board says it's invested more than $100M in new compensation since 2022 and provided double-digit pay increases exceeding surrounding districts. Impact for COS families: D11 serves roughly 23,000 students across Colorado Springs' west side, northeast, and downtown neighborhoods. Families considering school choices for fall 2026 should monitor d11.org.
