### STORY 1: COS July 4 Fireworks Cancelled
**Tier:** 1
**Pillar:** Local News
**Byline:** Sarah Morales
**Angle:** COS families woke up July 2 to news that all professional July 4 fireworks are cancelled. The critical fire danger comparison to Waldo Canyon Fire conditions is the emotional hook. El Paso County's inability (or unwillingness) to ban personal fireworks adds complexity. Article focuses on what IS cancelled, why, what families need to know about personal fireworks rules, and what IS still happening.
**Key tension:** City officials asking residents not to use personal fireworks while El Paso County is legally restricted from banning them. Clear, actionable guidance for parents.
**Content plan:** 2 X Text Posts + FB Long-Form + FB Image Caption + 2 Image Concepts + Article

### STORY 2: Cosyland Tower Stools Recall
**Tier:** 1
**Pillar:** National Parenting
**Byline:** Jamie Rivera
**Angle:** 125,200 units sold on Amazon over 4 years — a product many COS families likely own. The entrapment + fall hazard combination is serious: 8 injuries including a fractured arm. Article leads with action (stop use, get repair kit) and provides all contact info.
**Key tension:** Product sold for years on a major platform, appears safe for its function, but has a structural flaw that creates a specific and serious hazard for children.
