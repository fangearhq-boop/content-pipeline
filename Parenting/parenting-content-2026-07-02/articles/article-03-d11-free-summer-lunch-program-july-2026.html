D11 Free Summer Lunch Program Has 29 Days Left — Here's How Colorado Springs Families Can Use It

By Sarah Morales | July 2, 2026

The program runs through July 31 and is open to all kids ages 1 to 18 — no matter which school district. Free breakfast and lunch at multiple COS locations Monday through Friday.

Colorado Springs School District 11's free Summer Food Service Program has been running since May 27, and it has 29 days left. The program ends July 31, 2026.

If you haven't used it yet, or didn't know it existed, now is the time. The program provides free meals to children ages 1 through 18 at no cost, with no registration required and no school district affiliation needed. D11 families, D20 families, D49 families, homeschool families — all qualify.

How It Works

Multiple locations across Colorado Springs offer free breakfast (where available) and free lunch Monday through Friday. Sites are typically at schools, community centers, parks, and libraries. You don't need to sign up in advance — show up, eat, and go.

One option worth knowing about: D11 runs a mobile unit that visits East Library every weekday from 11 a.m. to noon. In addition to lunch, the mobile unit brings age-appropriate books to hand out. For families trying to prevent the summer reading slide — kids lose an estimated 25 to 30 percent of their literacy gains during summer without regular reading — the books-alongside-lunch model makes the stop doubly useful.

The Pikes Peak Library District (PPLD) also partners to offer free summer lunches at library branch locations around Colorado Springs. If you're already taking kids to the library for the Summer Adventure reading program (running through July 31), some locations serve lunch at the same visit.

Important Notes

All D11 summer lunch sites are closed on July 4 in observance of the Federal holiday.

The East Library mobile unit runs through August 1 — one day past the main program's July 31 end date, based on prior reporting from Fox21 and KKTV. Confirm the specific mobile unit schedule by calling D11 Food and Nutrition Services at 719-520-2930.
