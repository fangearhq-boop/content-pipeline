### STORY 1: D11 Orton Academy to Co-Locate at Trailblazer Elementary in 2026-27
**Tier:** 1
**Pillar:** Local News
**Status:** NEW STORY

Colorado Springs School District 11 has approved Orton Academy — a K-8 school that specializes in educating students with dyslexia — to co-locate at Trailblazer Elementary School starting in the 2026-27 school year. Trailblazer has capacity for ~600 students but is currently enrolled at ~220. Some Trailblazer parents were surprised by the announcement, with many saying they first learned about the possibility through Orton's website before receiving an official district email. Parents have raised concerns about sharing common spaces (cafeteria, gymnasium, bathrooms) with older middle school students. The district says the co-location will help both schools by increasing enrollment at under-used Trailblazer and expanding access to dyslexia-focused education.

### STORY 2: CPSC Recalls Multiple Amazon Baby Bath Seats — Drowning Risk
**Tier:** 1
**Pillar:** National Parenting (Safety)
**Status:** NEW STORY

The U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC) has issued recalls for multiple brands of baby bath seats sold on Amazon, citing instability and drowning risk. Affected brands include YCXXKJ (sold by BenTalk, ~9,000 units), CheerKid (sold by Babibaby and Woot), Trankerloop, and NFSVLB. All violate the mandatory standard for infant bath seats because they can tip over while in use. The YCXXKJ seats were sold in blue, gray, pink, and yellow between May 2024 and October 2025 for $34 to $40. Consumers should stop using them immediately and contact the seller for a full refund.

**Key Facts (verified):**
- YCXXKJ baby bath seats recalled — HIGH (CPSC.gov official page confirmed)
- Reason: unstable, tip hazard, drowning risk — HIGH (CPSC.gov)
- ~9,000 units, sold on Amazon (BenTalk) May 2024–Oct 2025, $34-$40 — HIGH (multiple sources incl. The Hill + CPSC)
- CheerKid, Trankerloop, NFSVLB bath seats also recalled — HIGH (CPSC.gov multiple pages confirmed)
- No injuries reported for YCXXKJ recall — HIGH (CPSC.gov)

**Tier:** 2
**Pillar:** Local Events
**Status:** NEW STORY

The City of Colorado Springs Parks, Recreation and Cultural Services has opened registration for the 2026 guided tour season at Corral Bluffs Open Space and Jimmy Camp Creek.
