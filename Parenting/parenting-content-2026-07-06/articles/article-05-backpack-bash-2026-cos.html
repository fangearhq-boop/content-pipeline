Backpack Bash 2026: 12,000 Free Backpacks for El Paso County Students, Starting July 25

By Sarah Morales | COS Parenting | July 6, 2026

Colorado Springs, CO

Back to school is still a few weeks away, but Backpack Bash is already lining up three distribution events across El Paso County this month and next. The annual event distributes 12,000 free backpacks and school supplies to students — no registration, no income verification, and no strings attached. Your student just needs to be there in person.

The first two events are on July 25, and a third follows on August 1. Together, they cover the east, southeast, and central areas of the county.

How It Works

There's no registration, no paperwork, and no income verification required. The only rule: the student needs to be present to receive a backpack. You can't pick one up on behalf of a neighbor's kid or a family member who can't make it.

Backpacks are distributed while supplies last, which means there's a real advantage to arriving early. At 12,000 backpacks across three events, the supply is substantial, but popular locations tend to move through inventory quickly in the first hour.

Who's Behind It

Backpack Bash is organized by Mercy's Gate and COSILoveYou, with support from more than 70 local churches, businesses, and nonprofits. It's been running in Colorado Springs for years and has become one of the most reliable back-to-school resources for families in El Paso County.

The event is entirely free — no ticket, no fee, no catch. If your family could use a backpack and supplies for the coming school year, this is exactly what it's designed for. And if your family is in a comfortable position, sharing this with someone who might benefit is genuinely useful.

What's Next

July 25 is 19 days away, which gives families plenty of time to plan. If you're going to the East location at Sand Creek High School or the Southeast location at PPUW Family Success Center, the main thing to remember is to bring your student, arrive before noon, and plan for the possibility of a wait if you're going at the peak early-morning rush.

For the central location date and address, check cosbackpackbash.com as August 1 approaches. The site is updated when venue details are confirmed.
