El Paso County Fair Turns 120 — July 11–18 in Calhan, $1 at the Gate

By Sarah Morales | COS Parenting | July 6, 2026

Colorado Springs, CO

The El Paso County Fair is celebrating its 120th anniversary this summer, and if your family has never made the 30-minute drive to Calhan for it, this is the year to go. The 2026 fair runs July 11–18 with a full week of rodeo, demolition derby, carnival rides, live music, and the kind of classic county fair atmosphere that's harder and harder to find.

General admission is $1. That's not a typo.

Tickets and Admission

The $1 general admission covers daytime access to the fairgrounds — vendors, exhibits, carnival games, and the community atmosphere. For families who want to stay for the evening entertainment, the Adult Golden Ticket is $69 and covers all eight days of the fair plus every evening event: the rodeo, demolition derby, auto races, and the arena concert.

There are also several ways to save at the gate:

  • First responders, police, firefighters, EMTs, doctors, nurses, and educators (and their dependents) get $2 off admission
  • Bring a non-perishable food donation and everyone in your group saves $2

More information and full ticket details are at elpasocountyfair.com.

What's There

The fair runs all week, which means there's no pressure to do everything in one visit — though many families do. Throughout the week you'll find:

  • Carnival rides and midway games
  • Live music throughout the week
  • Food vendors covering everything from fair classics to local favorites
  • Corn hole and lawn games
  • Livestock exhibits and judging
  • Evening events including the rodeo, demolition derby, auto races, and arena concerts (covered by the Golden Ticket)

The demolition derby in particular tends to be a crowd favorite for families with kids who've never seen one. It's exactly as loud and ridiculous as it sounds, in the best possible way.

Getting There

Calhan is located about 30 minutes east of Colorado Springs on Highway 24. The fairgrounds are easy to find and there's parking on site. It's worth noting that Calhan is at a similar elevation to Colorado Springs — around 6,500 feet — so the usual summer afternoon storm window applies. Arriving in the morning gives you the most comfortable timing for outdoor activities.

What's Next

The fair opens Saturday, July 11, which gives families two full weekends to make it out. If evenings are easier for your schedule, the evening events are the most action-packed and the Golden Ticket price makes it reasonable for a family to commit to more than one night.

Check elpasocountyfair.com for the full daily schedule, individual event times, and any updates before you go. The site also has information on livestock entry deadlines for kids involved in 4-H or FFA programs in the county.
