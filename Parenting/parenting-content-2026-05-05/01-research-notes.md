- Confidence: HIGH (cpsc.gov official recall page)
- Source: cpsc.gov/Recalls/2026/Sanven-Technology-Recalls-Vevor-Baby-Swings

**Fact: Models affected — BB501K (white/gray, white base), BB702A (white/gray, gray base), BB005K (black/gray, black base)**
- Confidence: HIGH (cpsc.gov, mother.ly)
- Source: cpsc.gov official recall

**Fact: Sold on Vevor.com and Amazon.com from January 2025 through August 2025 for $65–$80**

**Fact: Hazard — incline >10 degrees violates Safe Sleep for Babies Act; suffocation risk**
- Confidence: HIGH (cpsc.gov; CDC; AAP)

**Fact: Consumer action — destroy swing cover, mark base "RECALLED," take photos, contact for refund**
- Confidence: HIGH (cpsc.gov)
- Source: cpsc.gov official recall instructions

**Fact: Contact — 855-599-6320, M-F 9 AM–5 PM PT for full refund**
- Confidence: HIGH (cpsc.gov)
- Source: cpsc.gov

**Fact: No injuries reported**
- Confidence: MEDIUM (confirmed in multiple reports; accurate as of recall date)
