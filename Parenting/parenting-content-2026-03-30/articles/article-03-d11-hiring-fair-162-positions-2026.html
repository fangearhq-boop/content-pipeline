D11 Is Holding Its Largest Hiring Fair Ever — 162 Positions Open This Spring

March 30, 2026 · COS Parenting

Colorado Springs School District 11 has announced it's hosting the largest and most comprehensive hiring fair in district history — and the reason is straightforward: D11 currently has 162 open positions it needs to fill before the end of the school year.

The fair is being held at Doherty High School. Open positions span the organization: teachers and paraprofessionals at the classroom level, as well as crossing guards, support staff, and central administration roles. For families with kids in D11 schools, this is news worth knowing.

Why This Matters for Families

Teacher and paraprofessional vacancies affect kids directly. When a classroom teacher position goes unfilled, the options are typically a long-term substitute, a split assignment across classes, or reduced programming. None of those are ideal. When paraprofessional positions stay vacant, students who rely on additional support in the classroom feel it most acutely.

D11 announcing a large-scale hiring push isn't a sign the district is struggling — it's a sign they're being proactive about a challenge that affects school districts across Colorado and the nation. Teacher shortages have been a persistent issue since 2020, and districts that actively recruit, offer competitive pay, and make hiring accessible are more likely to close the gap.

The fact that D11 is calling this their largest hiring fair ever suggests scale and seriousness. They're not just posting jobs — they're bringing multiple departments together under one roof and making it easier for candidates to explore options across the entire district in a single visit.

Who Should Know About This

This isn't only news for people currently in education. D11 is hiring across a wide range of roles, and some of those positions — paraprofessionals, crossing guards, support staff — have more flexible entry points than a full teaching credential requires.

If you know someone in your network who is considering a career change into education, thinking about a part-time or flexible role while their own kids are in school, or has professional expertise they'd like to bring into a school setting, this is the moment to share this with them. D11's website at d11.org lists all open positions and has information about the hiring fair.

The D11 Calendar Context

D11's spring break was March 23-27 — students and staff are just returning this week. The last day of school for the 2025-2026 year is May 22, 2026. That means the district has roughly eight weeks of the current school year left after hiring fair candidates are identified — and the more pressing goal is likely filling positions for the 2026-2027 school year, which will be posted and confirmed in the coming months.

What's Next

Families with students in D11 schools should know that the district is actively working to address vacancies. If you have questions about staffing at your child's specific school, D11's school finder and school board meeting calendars are available at d11.org. For COS parents who work in education, are considering it, or know someone who is — the hiring fair at Doherty High School is the most accessible entry point available right now.
