### STORY 1: CPSC Recall — Bellabu Bear Children's Robes (Burn Hazard)
**Tier:** 1
**Pillar:** National Parenting
**Platforms:** X, Facebook, Article

The U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission has announced a recall of Bellabu Bear bamboo sherpa children's robes due to a burn hazard. The robes — sold in popular licensed character prints including PAW Patrol, Minecraft, Minions, and Batman — fail to meet mandatory federal flammability standards for children's sleepwear. Approximately 1,020 units were sold at Saks Fifth Avenue, boutiques nationwide, and online from January 2024 through July 2025 at about $60. No injuries have been reported. Families should stop use immediately and contact Bellabu Bear for a full refund or store credit.

### STORY 5: Outdoor Play Is the Best Summer Investment — Research Roundup for COS Families
**Tier:** 2
**Pillar:** National Parenting
**Platforms:** X, Facebook, Article

Research from the AAP and children's hospitals confirms what instinct tells most parents: outdoor play is one of the best things kids can do for their health. Benefits include lower obesity risk, stronger bones and immune function, reduced stress, improved focus, and fewer ADHD symptoms. With Colorado Springs offering access to trails, parks, and open spaces throughout Pikes Peak country, families have some of the best outdoor play infrastructure in the nation right outside their doors.
