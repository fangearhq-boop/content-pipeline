Mother's Day in Colorado Springs — Family Events, Brunches, and Free Activities for May 10

By Sarah Morales | May 10, 2026 | COS Parenting

From a wolf tour at 9 AM to circus performances this afternoon, Colorado Springs has options for families at every budget today.

Free and Low-Cost Options

If the budget is tight or you want to keep the day simple, Hillside Gardens is open for its Mother's Day weekend celebration today from 9 AM to 2 PM with free entry. It's a beautiful setting for a morning walk — Colorado Springs has no shortage of stunning garden spaces, and this one is particularly well-suited to families who want a relaxed outdoor experience without a reservation or a bill at the end.

Nature and Wildlife

For something more memorable, the Colorado Wolf and Wildlife Center is hosting a special Mother's Day wolf tour this morning. The event runs from 9-11 AM and includes a wolf experience plus a flower for moms. Pricing is $40 for adults and $20 for kids. This is the kind of outing that creates a genuine family story — few kids forget the first time they've been that close to a wolf, and it's a COS experience that most cities simply can't offer.

Check the Wolf and Wildlife Center's website before heading out to confirm availability and ticketing for today's event.

Call ahead to confirm availability, as Mother's Day brunches typically require advance reservations and same-day walk-in space is limited.

Family Entertainment

The Do Portugal Circus is currently running its run at Park Union through May 24, making it a solid choice for families looking for afternoon entertainment today. The show is animal-free and features trapeze artists, high-wire acts, motorcycle daredevils, clowns, acrobats, and musicians. It's the kind of old-fashioned circus experience that works for kids of most ages and doesn't require a reservation weeks in advance.

What's Next

Whatever your family decides to do with today — brunch, gardens, wolves, or circus — Mother's Day is one of those days worth making a little more intentional than a regular Sunday. Colorado Springs has the resources to make it memorable at any budget. Enjoy the day, COS families.
