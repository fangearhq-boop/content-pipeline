### STORY 1: Target Up&Up Baby Wipes Recall

**Freshness Tags:** Recall, Baby Wipes, Target

**Summary:** Target recalled two types of Up&Up baby wipes on June 4/8, 2026 after FDA testing found bacteria that can cause pneumonia and sepsis in newborns and infants. Fragrance Free and Fresh Cucumber Scented varieties are affected. Families should stop using the wipes immediately and return them to any Target store for a full refund.

**Relevance:** Target is one of the most common places COS parents shop for baby supplies. Up&Up is Target's store brand — widely purchased because of price and convenience. This recall affects thousands of local families who may still have these wipes at home.

**Angles:**
1. How to identify the recalled wipes (manufacturing dates on the package)
2. The specific bacteria risk and who is most at risk (newborns, infants)
3. How to get a refund (any Target store, no box or receipt needed typically)
4. Why this recall is different from typical product recalls (microbial contamination vs physical hazard)
5. What to use while you wait: wet a washcloth, use another brand temporarily
