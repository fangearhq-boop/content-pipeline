## STORY 1: Target Up&Up Baby Wipes Recall

#### Long-Form Post — 8:30 AM MT

```
⚠️ If you've been using Target's Up&Up baby wipes, check your supply today.

Target has issued a voluntary recall of two Up&Up baby wipe varieties after FDA testing found bacterial contamination. The products affected are Up&Up Fragrance Free Baby Wipes and Up&Up Fresh Cucumber Scented Baby Wipes. The bacteria found — Burkholderia cepacia complex and Burkholderia gladioli — can cause serious infections in newborns and infants, including pneumonia and sepsis.

Here's how to know if your wipes are part of the recall:

Up&Up Fragrance Free: Manufactured between November 7, 2025 and May 5, 2026. Expiration dates between May 10, 2028 and November 5, 2028. Available in 20-count, 72-count, 216-count, 800-count, and 1,200-count.

Up&Up Fresh Cucumber Scented: Manufactured December 29–30, 2025. Expiration dates June 29–30, 2028. Available in 72-count, 216-count, and 800-count.

If your wipes fall within these dates, stop using them immediately. Return them to any Target store for a full refund — no receipt or original packaging required.

In the meantime, a wet washcloth works well for diaper changes, or you can temporarily switch to another brand while you sort out the return.

Do you still have these wipes at home? Share this so Colorado Springs families who shop at Target know to check. 👇
```
