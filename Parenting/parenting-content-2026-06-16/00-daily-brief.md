### STORY 1: Otteroo Infant Neck Floats — CPSC Renews "Do Not Use" Warning
**Tier:** 1
**Type:** NEW STORY
**Content Pillar:** National Parenting
**Sources:** prnewswire.com (CPSC press release June 15, 2026), cpsc.gov, consumerreports.org

As of June 15, 2026, the CPSC's first-ever federal safety standard for infant neck floats is now in effect. On the same day, CPSC renewed its "Do Not Use" warning for Otteroo LUMI and MINI — the company has refused to recall. Original 2022 warning followed 1 infant drowning death, 1 serious injury, and dozens of rescue incidents. Infants can slip through if float deflates. Direct action items: stop use immediately, CPSC.gov, 1-800-638-2772.
