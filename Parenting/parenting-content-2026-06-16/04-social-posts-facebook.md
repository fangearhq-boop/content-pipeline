### STORY 1: Otteroo Infant Neck Floats

#### Long-Form Post — 8:00 AM MT

```
If you have an Otteroo LUMI or MINI infant neck float — or know someone who does — please read this.

On June 15, 2026, the CPSC renewed its "Do Not Use" warning for both models as the first-ever federal safety standard for infant neck floats officially took effect. The standard applies to any neck float manufactured after June 15.

Here's what that means in plain terms: Otteroo has refused to issue a recall. They continue to manufacture and sell these products. The CPSC's warning is in place specifically because the company won't take corrective action — which means families are responsible for making the decision themselves.

The hazard is real. If the float partially or fully deflates, an infant can slip through the neck opening and become submerged. The original CPSC warning in 2022 followed one reported infant drowning death, one serious injury, and dozens of incidents where caregivers had to pull infants from the water.

Water activities this summer — pools, splash pads, lakes — are wonderful. But if you're using an Otteroo product, the CPSC's guidance is clear: stop use immediately.

If you have questions or want to report an incident:
→ CPSC.gov
→ 1-800-638-2772

Share this — other Colorado Springs families may not have seen it yet.
```

🕛 Freedom March at 12 PM
🏛️ Museum tours at 1 PM and 2:30 PM
📍 Westside Community Center Campus, Colorado Springs
✅ Free and open to the public

Juneteenth marks June 19, 1865 — the day enslaved people in Texas learned of their freedom, more than two months after the Confederacy's surrender. It became a federal holiday in 2021.

📚 Preschool (Monday-Thursday)
🌅 Base49 — before and after-school care
🌟 PEAK programs
🎓 Elevates 18-21 — transition services for young adults

The Student Success Center consolidation is part of how D49 is restructuring services within those budget constraints.

If your family is interested in any of the Student Success Center programs — especially preschool or Base49 care — now is the time to research enrollment. The budget is done. The building is being prepared. August will arrive faster than it seems.
