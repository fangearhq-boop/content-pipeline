Girls Pro Flag Football Camp Returns to Colorado Springs: June 8-10 at Venezia Park

By Jamie Rivera | June 4, 2026 | COS Parenting

The 2nd annual Girls Pro Flag Football Camp comes to Colorado Springs next week, June 8 through 10, at John Venezia Community Park. The three-day camp is open to girls ages 9 through 18 at all skill levels, with no football experience required. Current and former Women's Pro Football League athletes are coaching, and registration is available now at profootballcamp.com.

What to Expect at Camp

The camp runs from 8 AM to 11:30 AM each day — Monday, Tuesday, and Wednesday, June 8 through 10. The location is John Venezia Community Park at 3555 Briargate Parkway, Colorado Springs, CO 80920.

The program is designed to develop football fundamentals in a non-contact format while building confidence, leadership skills, and teamwork. Coaches leading the sessions include three returning Women's Pro Football athletes: Breaya Quintana, Karen Scott, and Vana Medrano. Additional pro player coaches round out the staff.

The camp's stated purpose is to give girls a safe, positive environment to discover flag football — whether they have played before or are picking up a ball for the first time. All skill levels are welcome.

Free Meet the Pros Event — June 7

The evening before camp starts, on Sunday June 7, a free "Meet the Pros" community event is scheduled at Red's Sunset Grill in Colorado Springs. The event gives participants and their families the opportunity to meet the coaching staff before stepping on the field. No registration is required for the Meet the Pros event.

For families who want their daughter to feel comfortable before the first morning of camp, this is a low-stakes way to connect with coaches and other participants.

What's Next

Registration for the Girls Pro Flag Football Camp is open now at profootballcamp.com. The camp is part of a broader program bringing pro athletes to communities across Colorado to work directly with youth players. If your daughter is interested in flag football beyond the camp, the Girls Play Flag Football Colorado (gpffco.com) organization offers league play in the Colorado Springs area during the spring season. For questions about the camp itself, the registration page at profootballcamp.com includes contact information for the camp organizers.
