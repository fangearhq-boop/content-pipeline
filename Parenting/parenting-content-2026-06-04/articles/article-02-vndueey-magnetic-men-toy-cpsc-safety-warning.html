CPSC Safety Warning: Vndueey Magnetic Men Toy Sets Pose Magnet Ingestion Danger — Check Your Amazon Orders

By Jamie Rivera | June 4, 2026 | COS Parenting

The U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission has issued a safety warning for Vndueey Magnetic Men toy sets sold on Amazon. The flexible stick figure toys contain small magnets that pose a serious swallowing hazard to children. If you purchased one of these sets from Amazon between October 2023 and August 2025, stop using it immediately and keep it away from children.

What the Toy Looks Like

The recalled sets consist of 10 flexible stick figures sold together. The figures come in a variety of colors — red, orange, pink, yellow, blue, bright red, bright green, and transparent. Each stick figure has four small magnets embedded in the toy: one in each hand and one in each foot.

The toys were sold on Amazon by Shanghai Zhidi Network Technology Co., Ltd., operating under the seller name Vndueey, from October 2023 through August 2025 for approximately $25. The CPSC notes they may also have been sold on other websites.

Why Magnets Are Dangerous

Small high-powered magnets in toys present a specific and serious risk to children: if two or more are swallowed, they attract to each other through the walls of the intestines. This can cause the intestinal tissue between the magnets to become compressed, leading to perforations, twisting, and blockage of the bowel. These injuries can cause blood poisoning and can be fatal without emergency surgery.

The severity of magnetic ingestion injuries is why the CPSC has strict mandatory standards for toys containing magnets. The Vndueey toy sets violate these standards.

Important: This Is a Notice of Violation, Not a Full Recall

The CPSC issued a Notice of Violation rather than a formal recall because the manufacturer, the China-based company behind Vndueey, has not agreed to recall the product or offer a remedy. The safety warning stands regardless: parents should not wait for a formal recall announcement to act. The hazard is real whether or not the company cooperates.

The CPSC also maintains a safety warning page at cpsc.gov with the full product details and instructions.

What's Next

The CPSC recommends that families check Amazon order history for any toy purchases involving small magnets. Multiple magnetic toy sets have been subject to recalls and safety warnings in 2025 and 2026. When buying toys online — particularly from unfamiliar sellers — check the CPSC recall database at cpsc.gov before purchasing, and look for toy safety certifications on the product listing. If you are unsure whether a toy your child uses contains high-powered magnets, the CPSC hotline is available at 800-638-2772.
