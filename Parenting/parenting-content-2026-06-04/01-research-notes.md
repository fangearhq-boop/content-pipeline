### STORY 2: Vndueey Magnetic Men Toy CPSC Warning

**Confirmed facts:**
- CPSC issued Notice of Violation (NOT a formal recall — company has not agreed) [HIGH — CPSC.gov]
- Product: Vndueey Magnetic Men toy sets — 10 flexible stick figures, colors: red, orange, pink, yellow, blue, bright red, bright green, transparent [HIGH — CPSC.gov]
- Each figure has 4 small magnets — one in each hand and foot [HIGH — CPSC.gov]
- Sold on Amazon Oct 2023–Aug 2025, ~$25 [HIGH — CPSC.gov]
- Company: Shanghai Zhidi Network Technology Co., Ltd. doing business as Vndueey [HIGH — CPSC.gov]

- Consumer action: stop use, cut all figures in half, email photo to vndueeyrecall@outlook.com for refund [HIGH — CPSC.gov]
- Company has NOT agreed to recall or remedy [HIGH — CPSC.gov wording]
- Note: "may have also been sold on other websites" [MEDIUM — CPSC.gov implies]

### STORY 5: AAP Drowning Prevention Summer Guide

**Confirmed facts:**
- Drowning is a leading cause of death in children ages 1–4 [HIGH — AAP official + multiple medical sources]
- AAP updated drowning prevention guidance in 2026 [MEDIUM — HealthyChildren.org reference; described as updated guidance; single primary source]
- Key recommendations: swim lessons starting after child's first birthday [HIGH — AAP official + healthychildren.org]
- Four-sided pool fencing (isolates pool from house) is most important home safety measure [HIGH — AAP]
- Never leave children alone or in care of another child near any water (pools, bathtubs, buckets, open water) [HIGH — AAP]
- Empty water from buckets, containers immediately after use [HIGH — AAP]
- CPR training for caregivers recommended [HIGH — AAP]
- Layered prevention strategy approach (multiple layers, not just one) [HIGH — AAP]
- Life jacket use for open water [HIGH — AAP]
- Applies to all water not just pools [HIGH — AAP]
