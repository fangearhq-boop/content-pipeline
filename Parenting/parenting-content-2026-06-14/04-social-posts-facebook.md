### STORY 4: Guidecraft Kitchen Helper Tower Recall

#### Long-Form Post — 10:00 AM MT

```
COS parents — if you have a Guidecraft Kitchen Helper Tower, it's time to stop using it and request a free repair kit.

The CPSC issued Recall 26-490 in May 2026 for approximately 25,235 Classic and Contemporary Guidecraft Kitchen Helper Towers. The hazard: the platform inside the tower can loosen over time and detach, creating a fall risk for young children. The CPSC has received 11 fall reports and 3 injuries.

These towers were sold from June 2022 through October 2023 at Amazon, Wayfair, Walmart, Target, Maisonette, and Overstock for around $200. They came in 9 color options.

Here's what to do:
✅ Stop using the tower immediately
✅ Call Guidecraft: 800-524-3555 (Mon–Fri, 8 AM–8 PM ET)
✅ Email: ProductSupport@guidecraft.com
✅ Online: guidecraft.com/pages/product-recall
✅ Remedy: free repair kit (not a full refund)

This is the third toddler tower or stool brand recalled in 2026. If you have a learning tower from any brand, it's worth a quick check on cpsc.gov.

Did you own one of these? Share so other COS parents can see this. 👇
```
