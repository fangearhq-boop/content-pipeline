Free Outdoor Movie and Silent Disco at Hillside Community Center — July 17 at 5:30 PM

By Jamie Rivera | COS Parenting | July 8, 2026

Colorado Springs, CO

The City of Colorado Springs is hosting a free outdoor movie night with a twist next Friday: it's a silent disco movie screening, meaning participants will watch the film through wireless headphones while the audio plays directly in their ears rather than over speakers.

The Outdoor Movie and Silent Disco at Hillside Community Center is scheduled for Friday, July 17, 2026, starting at 5:30 PM. The event is at 925 South Institute Street in Colorado Springs. Admission is free, no registration is required, and the whole family is welcome.

The Movie Is a Secret — For Now

The City hasn't announced the film title. Licensing restrictions prevent disclosing the movie name in advance, but the City's Parks, Recreation and Cultural Services department has been posting clues on its social media account (@CoSpringsParks). If your family wants to know what's playing before you arrive, that's the place to follow along.

Whatever the film turns out to be, the setup is part of what makes this event worth attending: the silent disco format creates a shared experience where everyone is hearing the same thing through headphones, which can feel surprisingly communal for a "silent" event.

What to Bring

The City recommends:

  • Blankets for seating on the grass
  • Lawn chairs

No food or beverage requirements were specified. The event starts at 5:30 PM, which is before sunset in mid-July, so plan to arrive with enough time to get settled before the film begins.

About Hillside Community Center

Hillside Community Center is a City of Colorado Springs facility at 925 South Institute Street (zip code 80903), in a neighborhood near downtown Colorado Springs. The center hosts regular community programming throughout the year. The outdoor space allows for lawn seating and larger community events like this film night.

Context: Park and Recreation Month

This event is part of Colorado Springs' Park and Recreation Month programming for July 2026, a citywide series of free and low-cost events hosted through the Parks, Recreation and Cultural Services department. More events are listed at coloradosprings.gov/parkandrecmonth and at the City's event page at coloradosprings.gov/event/hillside-movie-nights.

What's Next

Mark your calendar for Friday, July 17, at 5:30 PM at 925 South Institute Street. Follow @CoSpringsParks on social media for the movie reveal. No tickets, no registration — just show up with blankets and lawn chairs. Updates on weather or any changes will come through the City's parks social accounts.
