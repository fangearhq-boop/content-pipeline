CPSC Recall: BABESIDE Doll and Stroller Set — Toy Pacifier and Plush Bear Are the Hazards

By Jamie Rivera | COS Parenting | July 8, 2026

Colorado Springs, CO

If you have a BABESIDE Doll and Stroller children's toy set, two parts of that set need to be removed from your child's reach today.

The U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission has recalled approximately 2,200 BABESIDE Doll and Stroller Children's Toy Sets. The sets were sold on Amazon by HYBDOLLS between July 2025 and January 2026 for approximately $40. The recall was announced June 18, 2026.

What the Set Looks Like

The recalled set includes a pink-and-red stroller, a baby doll dressed in pink, and 23 accessories. Among those accessories are the two problematic items: a toy pacifier and a small plush bear. The rest of the set — the stroller, the doll, and the non-hazardous accessories — is not affected by the recall.

What the Hazards Are

There are two separate safety violations in this set:

The toy pacifier violates the federal small parts ban. Under federal law, toys intended for children under 3 cannot contain small parts that pose choking hazards. The pacifier included in this set is a small part that fails that test. Because infant and toddler toys are regularly used by children in the under-3 age range, this is considered a serious hazard.

The eyes on the plush bear can detach. Detachable small parts on stuffed animals are a well-established choking hazard for young children. The CPSC determined the bear's eye attachment does not meet safety standards.

No injuries had been reported at the time of the recall announcement.

What to Do

The CPSC's remedy for this recall is straightforward:

  1. Remove the plush bear and the toy pacifier from children immediately and stop use of both items.
  2. Cut the plush bear in half to destroy it.
  3. Mark an "X" on the toy pacifier in permanent marker.
  4. Take a photo showing both destroyed items.
  5. Email the photo to support@babeside.com. HYBDOLLS will send free replacement accessories with shipping included.

You are not required to return the stroller, doll, or other accessories. The remedy only applies to the plush bear and toy pacifier.

What's Next

The full CPSC recall page for this product is available at cpsc.gov. If you have questions about the replacement process or haven't received a response from support@babeside.com within a reasonable time, the CPSC hotline is also an option — 1-800-638-2772. The CPSC recommends consumers contact the seller directly as the first step.
