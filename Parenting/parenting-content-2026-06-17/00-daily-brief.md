### STORY 1: Guidecraft Kitchen Helper Tower Recall — Free Repair Available
**Tier:** 1
**Type:** NEW STORY
**Content Pillar:** National Parenting
**Sources:** cpsc.gov, schiffmanfirm.com, dailyhornet.com, sknbs.org, consumerreports.org

25,235 Guidecraft Kitchen Helper Towers (Classic and Contemporary, nine colors) recalled due to a fall hazard: the platform inside can loosen or detach over time. CPSC confirmed 11 reports of children falling, including three injuries (bloody nose, bruises). Towers were sold at Amazon, Target, Guidecraft.com, Wayfair, Walmart, Maisonette, and Overstock from June 2022 through October 2023 for approximately $200. REMEDY: Free repair kit. Stop use immediately and contact Guidecraft toll-free at 800-524-3555 (8 AM-8 PM ET, Mon-Fri), email ProductSupport@guidecraft.com, or visit guidecraft.com/pages/product-recall. Recall announced May 14, 2026.

### STORY 5: D20 Names Dr. Susan Field Interim Superintendent — What Families Should Know
**Tier:** 2
**Type:** NEW STORY (first time covered in COS Parenting pipeline)
**Content Pillar:** Local News
**Sources:** krdo.com, kktv.com, koaa.com, fox21news.com, gazette.com

Academy District 20 board removed superintendent Jinger Haberer (May 15, 2026) following a "lack of confidence" vote; Dr. Susan Field was unanimously named interim superintendent (May 19, 2026) for the 2026-27 school year. Dr. Field has 19 years with D20 — she started as principal at Woodmen-Roberts Elementary and has been serving as Assistant Superintendent for Learning Services. D20 serves approximately 26,000 students across nearly 40 schools in northern Colorado Springs. Schools will operate normally. First item of note for families: Fall 2026 enrollment updates and decisions about D20 direction will come from interim leadership.
