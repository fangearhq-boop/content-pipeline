- **Recall date:** July 9, 2026 — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov)
- **Units recalled:** 9,100 — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov)
- **Brand names:** BBRKIN and/or MouTec — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov)
- **Model number:** QHXP029B — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov)
- **Serial number range:** SQC200034980 – SQC202319171; label on lower right corner of front of safe — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov)
- **Safe dimensions:** approximately 14 inches × 12 inches × 57 inches; one shelf; stores approximately 5 firearms — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov)
- **Color/material:** gray steel — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov)
- **Sold at:** exclusively Amazon.com — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov title: "Sold Exclusively on Amazon.com by BBRKIN")
- **Hazard:** Biometric lock can be opened by unauthorized users — serious injury hazard and risk of death (firearms access) — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov)
- **Injuries:** No incidents or injuries reported at time of recall — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov)
- **Remedy:** Stop using biometric feature; remove batteries; use key only; free repair kit available — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov)
- **Contact for remedy:** Email support@bbrkin.com or online at www.securitysafe.store/recallreplacement — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov)
- **Content pillar:** National Parenting

### What NOT to Claim
- The 6-year-old child opening incident: attributed to a broader multi-brand biometric gun safe recall (KATV headline covers multiple brands including Bulldog, Magnum, AWESAFE, etc.). CPSC page specifically for BBRKIN/QHXP029B reports NO incidents. Do not attribute the 6-year-old incident to BBRKIN specifically.

## STORY 2: Babypark Children's Carriers CPSC Warning (2026)

### Core Warning Facts
- **CPSC action type:** WARNING (not a formal recall) — manufacturer refused to cooperate — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov)
- **Product:** Soft infant and toddler carriers (3-in-1 fabric carriers); gray with multicolored leaf design; adjustable shoulder and waist straps — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov)
- **Hazard:** Leg openings large enough that a child can slip through; serious fall hazard — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov)
- **Violation:** Mandatory standard for soft infant and toddler carriers — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov)
- **Sold at:** Amazon.com — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov)
- **Sale period:** April 2024 through May 2026 — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov)
- **Price:** Approximately $27 — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov)
- **Manufacturer:** Products Co., Ltd. doing business as Beiyou Bless, of China — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov)
- **Manufacturer response:** Has NOT agreed to recall or offer any remedy — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov)
- **Consumer action:** Immediately dispose of the product; do not sell or give away — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov)
- **Content pillar:** National Parenting

### Distinction from 2025 Babypark Warning
- January 2025 separate warning: Babypark SLING carriers (different product — ring/sling style sold on Shein.com, ~750 units, structural integrity failure). Today's 2026 warning is for a DIFFERENT product: 3-in-1 soft/fabric carriers sold on Amazon. These are separate CPSC actions on different products.
