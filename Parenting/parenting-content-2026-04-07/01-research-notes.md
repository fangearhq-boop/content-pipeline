### STORY 1: CPSC Recalls — KTEBO Writing Tablets + Zippee Silicone Toys

**Sources:**
- CPSC.gov (official): KTEBO Writing Tablet recall page
- CPSC.gov (official): Zippee Silicone Activity Toys recall page
- NewsNation: KTEBO recall coverage
- ABC27: KTEBO recall coverage
- Motherly: Zippee recall coverage
- GrowingYourBaby.com: Zippee recall coverage

**Verified facts:**
- KTEBO Writing Tablet Toys: battery compartment screw does not stay attached; button/coin battery ingestion risk (chemical burns and death) — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov + NewsNation + ABC27)
- ~10,380 units sold on Amazon (Sept-Oct 2025, $8-$30) — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov)
- 8.5" and 10" sizes in multiple color combinations — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov)
- Action: Stop use; contact writing-tablet-recall@ktebo.com for free replacement set — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov)
- Zippee Silicone Activity Toys by Mobi Games: ball-ended silicone strings can reach back of child's throat — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov + Motherly + GrowingYourBaby)
- ~117,000-143,000 units sold at Amazon, PlayMobi.com, specialty toy stores (Nov 2019-Jan 2024, ~$20) — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov)
- One confirmed incident of a child gagging and vomiting — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov)
- Date codes inside cylinder in MMDDYY format 030620–110823 — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov)
- Action: Stop use; contact Mobi Games for full refund — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov)
- Both violate mandatory toy safety standards — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov)

**Do NOT use:** Exact units sold (range varies by source for Zippee — use "more than 117,000").

### STORY 2: LFTE USA Playground Swings + YITA Dresser Recalls

**Sources:**

- Yahoo/ABC27: LFTE USA coverage
- MyParisTexas: LFTE USA coverage
- furniture-tip-over-accidents.com: YITA coverage
- Newsweek: YITA coverage

**Verified facts:**
- LFTE USA Playground Swing Set Seats: rivets supporting swing seat can fail; one confirmed child injury — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov + Yahoo/ABC27 + MyParisTexas)
- ~7,200 units; sold to Miracle Recreation Equipment and Little Tikes Commercial (Jan-Sept 2025, $80-$95) — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov)
- Model number 999604 ONLY; colors: black, blue, green, red, tan, yellow — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov)
- Sold to commercial/institutional buyers, not individual consumers — affects public playgrounds and school equipment — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov)
- Action: Stop use; contact recall@lfteusa.com for free replacement — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov)
- YITA/Dextrus/ModFusion/Uforic/Yintatech/Yitahome 16-drawer dressers: tip-over and entrapment hazard; violate STURDY Act mandatory standard — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov + furniture-tip-over + Newsweek)
- ~6,800 units; sold Amazon and Walmart (Sept 2023-Aug 2025, $75-$100); colors: gray, black, white, pink, brown — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov)
- Action: Stop using if not wall-anchored; keep away from children; contact YITA for full refund — **HIGH** (CPSC.gov)
