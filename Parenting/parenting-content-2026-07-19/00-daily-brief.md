### STORY 2: Button Battery Toy Recall Alert
- **Content Pillar:** National Parenting
- **Tier:** 1
- **Posting Window:** 10:00 AM MT
- **Angle:** CPSC has issued multiple recalls in 2026 for children's toys containing button/coin cell batteries that children can easily access. Products include LED finger lights (Oitnlaughter brand, sold on Amazon July 2025–April 2026 for ~$14) and LED light-up tutu skirts (sold by Bmrwtg on Amazon). Swallowing a button cell battery can cause internal chemical burns and death. Stop use immediately; contact sellers for full refunds.

### STORY 4: Wade Logan Annyka Fabric Dresser Recall
- **Content Pillar:** National Parenting
- **Tier:** 2
- **Posting Window:** 3:00 PM MT
- **Angle:** The CPSC has recalled the Wade Logan Annyka 9-Drawer Fabric Dresser (sold on Wayfair, September 2023–May 2026, ~$98) due to tip-over and entrapment hazards — a risk of serious injury or death to children if the dresser is not anchored to the wall. Violates the STURDY Act mandatory standard. Consumers should stop using the dresser if unanchored, move it out of children's reach, and contact the importer for refund instructions (email annykarecall@yeah.net with disposal photo).
