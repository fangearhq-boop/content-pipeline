## STORY 2: Button Battery Toy Recall Alert

**Sources:**
- CPSC.gov: "Oitnlaughter Projecting Finger Light Toys Recalled Due to Risk of Serious Injury or Death from Battery Ingestion; Violate Mandatory Standard for Toys; Sold on Amazon by Delightmi"
- CPSC.gov: "Children's LED Tutu Skirts Recalled Due to Risk of Serious Injury or Death from Battery Ingestion; Violates Mandatory Standard for Consumer Products with Coin Batteries; Sold on Amazon by Bmrwtg"
- CPSC.gov: "Meaicezli Play Purse Sets Recalled Due to Risk of Serious Injury or Death from Battery Ingestion; Violates Mandatory Standard for Toys; Sold on Amazon by Angeer-US"

**Key Facts:**

Oitnlaughter Finger Lights (HIGH confidence):
- Product: Oitnlaughter LED Finger Lights — sets of 36, six animal designs (tiger, elephant, rabbit, panda, butterfly, duck)
- Sold: Amazon.com by Delightmi
- Sale period: July 2025 – April 2026
- Price: ~$14
- Hazard: Button cell batteries easily accessible to children; if swallowed causes internal chemical burns and death
- Action: Stop use, take from children, contact Delightmi for full refund; dispose and send photo to company
- Violation: Mandatory safety standard for toys (CPSC)

LED Tutu Skirts (HIGH confidence):
- Sold on Amazon by Bmrwtg
- Hazard: Coin cell batteries in LED lights can be accessed by children; ingestion hazard

Play Purse Sets (HIGH confidence):
- Sold by Angeer-US on Amazon as Meaicezli
- Hazard: Button batteries in play purse sets

**Broader pattern:** CPSC has issued at least five similar recalls in mid-2026 covering toys with accessible button batteries — the common thread is inexpensive toys sold on Amazon with LED lights powered by accessible coin cells.

**Confidence:** HIGH — all from official CPSC.gov recall pages (2026 URLs)
