## Story 2: AAP New Screen Time Guidelines 2026

**Source:** aap.org (official AAP guidance page), edsurge.com, health.choc.org, news.fsu.edu, medicalxpress.com
**Confidence:** HIGH (confirmed via AAP.org directly)

- **Announcement date:** January 20, 2026
- **Key change:** AAP dropped the previous 1-hour-per-day limit model; no longer recommends strict time-based daily limits for children over 5
- **New framework — the "5 Cs":**
  1. Child (age, temperament, needs)
  2. Content (what they're watching/playing — quality matters)
  3. Calm (whether screens overstimulate or help regulate)
  4. Crowding out (whether screens displace sleep, movement, family time)
  5. Communication (ongoing conversation about digital habits)
- **What stays:** Under-18 months: no screens recommended except video calls. Under-5: quality co-viewing still encouraged. Device-free bedrooms/mealtimes remain. Screens off 1 hour before bed.
- **Practical new guidance:** Look at the whole context, not just the clock. Ask what screens are replacing, not just how long.

## Story 5: SDADI Kitchen Step Stool Recall

**Source:** cpsc.gov (official recall page, July 16, 2026), newsnationnow.com
**Confidence:** HIGH (confirmed via CPSC.gov directly)

- **Recall date:** July 16, 2026 (4 days ago — very timely)
- **Importer:** Yiwushi Bihe Trading (China)
- **Brand/models:** SDADI, models LT01 and LT05
- **Colors:** Gray, white, espresso, pink, natural wood
- **Approx. dimensions:** ~35 inches high, 15-18 inches wide, 18 inches deep
- **Units recalled:** 5,952
- **Where sold:** Amazon.com, Walmart.com, Target.com, BestBuy.com
- **Sale period:** April 2024 through September 2025
- **Price range:** $45 to $155
- **Hazard:** Tower stools can collapse or tip over while in use; a child's torso can fit through front/back openings, posing tip-over, fall, and entrapment hazards with risk of serious injury or death
- **Incidents/injuries:** 8 reports of instability or tip-overs; 4 injuries (scrapes, cuts, bruises)
- **Remedy:** Full refund — stop use, store away from children, contact Yiwushi Bihe Trading
- **Consumer contact:** john@SDADI.net
- **NOTE:** Separate April 23, 2026 CPSC warning (not recall) for Bauhini SDADI step stools (~3,600 units) — Bauhini China didn't cooperate; destroy by disassembly, no refund available. Focus the story on the official July 16 recall (LT01/LT05).
