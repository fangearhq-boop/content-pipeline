### STORY 1: Build-A-Bear Heartwarming Hugs Bear Recall
- **Content Pillar:** National Parenting
- **Tier:** 1
- **Posting Window:** 8:00 AM MT
- **Angle:** CPSC recalled the Build-A-Bear Heartwarming Hugs Bear (model 034464) — a weighted plush bear with a side pouch containing 2.5 lbs of ceramic beads. The zipper slider can detach, posing a choking hazard to children. Approximately 36,000 units were sold in the US from January through March 2026 at approximately $48 in Build-A-Bear stores and at buildabear.com. Consumers should stop using the bear immediately and return it to any Build-A-Bear Workshop store for a refund (original payment or gift card). Recall announced April 30, 2026. Contact: 844-541-0144 or buildabear.com/recalls.

### STORY 2: AAP Drops Strict Screen Time Limits — New 2026 Guidelines
- **Content Pillar:** National Parenting
- **Tier:** 1
- **Posting Window:** 10:00 AM MT
- **Angle:** The American Academy of Pediatrics officially dropped its previous 1-hour-per-day screen time limit model in January 2026 and moved to a new quality-and-context framework called the "5 Cs" — Child (age, temperament), Content (what they're watching), Calm (whether screens overstimulate), Crowding out (whether screens are displacing sleep/movement/family time), and Communication (talking about digital habits). No specific daily hour limit is now recommended for children over 5. Under-18-month guidance (no screens except video calls) and device-free bedrooms/mealtimes remain.

### STORY 5: SDADI Kitchen Step Stool Recall — July 16, 2026
- **Content Pillar:** National Parenting
- **Tier:** 1
- **Posting Window:** 4:00 PM MT
- **Angle:** CPSC recalled SDADI kitchen helper tower step stools (models LT01 and LT05, importer: Yiwushi Bihe Trading) on July 16, 2026. The stools can collapse or tip over while in use, and a child's torso can fit through openings on the sides — posing entrapment, fall, and tip-over hazards. About 5,952 units were sold on Amazon, Walmart.com, Target.com, and BestBuy.com from April 2024 through September 2025 at $45–$155. 8 reports of instability or tip-overs; 4 resulting injuries. Remedy: full refund — stop use, store away from children, contact john@SDADI.net.
