## STORY 4: AAP Car Seat Safety 2026

**Source:** aap.org, preventinjury.medicine.iu.edu

**Verified facts (HIGH confidence):**
- AAP Child Passenger Safety updated February 12, 2026
- Key change: removed age-based guideline (previously: rear-facing until age 2)
- New guideline: rear-facing until child reaches maximum height OR weight dictated by car seat manufacturer
- Booster seat: use belt-positioning booster until lap/shoulder belt fits properly
- Proper fit typically when child is at least 4 feet 9 inches tall
- General range for proper fit: ages 8-12

**Source URLs:**
- https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/early-childhood/early-childhood-health-and-development/safe-environments/child-passenger-safety/

## STORY 5: COS Outdoor Family Guide for May

**Source:** weather25.com, tripadvisor.com, visitcos.com, springshomes.com

**Verified facts (HIGH confidence):**
- COS May highs: 48-80°F, avg high 68°F
- COS May lows: 34-52°F, avg low 43°F
- Average 8 rainy days in May (afternoon thunderstorms common)
- Humidity: 32-43% (dry)
- Sunshine: ~10-11 hours per day
- Garden of the Gods: free, paved and dirt trails, dramatic red rock formations
- North Cheyenne Cañon: hiking, Seven Bridges trail (popular with families, can get crowded)
- Colorado Springs elevation: ~6,035 feet (above sea level)
- UV exposure increased ~4% per 1,000 feet of elevation gain
