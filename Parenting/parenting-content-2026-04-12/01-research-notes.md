### STORY 2: CPSC Warning — HiQiLi Wintergreen Essential Oil Poisoning Risk

**Sources:**
- cpsc.gov — "CPSC Warns Consumers to Immediately Stop Using HiQiLi Wintergreen Essential Oil Due to Risk of Serious Injury or Death from Poisoning to Young Children; Violates Mandatory Standard for Child-Resistant Packaging"
- facebook.com/USCPSC — CPSC official Facebook post confirming warning

**Verified Facts (HIGH confidence):**
- CPSC issued a Warning (not a formal recall) to stop using HiQiLi Wintergreen Essential Oil (cpsc.gov)
- Product contains methyl salicylate, which requires child-resistant packaging under the Poison Prevention Packaging Act (cpsc.gov)
- The product's packaging is NOT child-resistant, violating mandatory standards (cpsc.gov)
- Sold online on Amazon.com from May 2022 through August 2025 for about $10 (cpsc.gov)
- Product identification: "HiQiLi High Quality Life" and "Wintergreen Essential Oil" printed on front labels of packaging and both bottles (cpsc.gov)
- Comes in white and blue packaging; amber bottle with screw cap and dropper cap; travel-size bottle also included (cpsc.gov)
- CPSC recommends: immediately stop using, secure out of reach of children (cpsc.gov)

**Verified Facts (MEDIUM confidence):**
- No injuries noted in search results; this is a preventive warning (likely accurate but not confirmed from 2 independent sources)

**Freshness:** Recent CPSC warning. Product sold through August 2025 means many households may still have this. Timely safety story.

### STORY 5: Wintergreen Essential Oil Safety — What Parents Need to Know

**Sources:**
- cpsc.gov — HiQiLi Wintergreen Essential Oil Warning (2026)
- cpsc.gov — GM Gumili Wintergreen Essential Oil Bottles Recalled (2026, sold on Amazon by Gumili Beauty)
- cpsc.gov — Euqee Wintergreen Essential Oils Recalled (2025, Amazon by DLWL-US)
- abc27.com — "Thousands of essential oils recalled due to risk of poisoning"
- cpsc.gov — Poison Prevention Information Center (general methyl salicylate resource)

**Verified Facts (HIGH confidence):**
- Wintergreen essential oil contains methyl salicylate, which is toxic to children (cpsc.gov — Poison Prevention + multiple recall pages; 2+ sources)
- Methyl salicylate must be in child-resistant packaging under the Poison Prevention Packaging Act (cpsc.gov — multiple recall pages)
- CPSC has taken action against multiple wintergreen oil brands in 2025-2026: HiQiLi (Warning, 2026), GM Gumili (Recall, 2026), Euqee (Recall, 2025) — 3 separate CPSC actions (cpsc.gov — 3 sources)
- GM Gumili: bottles sold on Amazon by Gumili Beauty; violated mandatory child-resistant packaging standard; recall 2026 (cpsc.gov)
- CPSC Poison Prevention page provides general guidance on essential oil storage and child safety (cpsc.gov)

**Verified Facts (MEDIUM confidence):**
- Methyl salicylate is rapidly absorbed through skin and mucous membranes; even small amounts can be toxic to children (general toxicology knowledge, consistent with CPSC warning language)

**Safety guidance (for article practical section):**
- Store all essential oils in locked cabinet or high shelf, not reachable by children
- Never leave caps off essential oil bottles
- If child ingests: call Poison Control immediately 1-800-222-1222
- Do not induce vomiting — call Poison Control first

**Freshness:** Pattern of 2025-2026 CPSC actions against wintergreen oil brands; many COS families use essential oils; actionable safety guide content. Not covered in story-history.md.
