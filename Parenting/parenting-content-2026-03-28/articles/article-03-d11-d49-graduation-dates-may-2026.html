COS Graduation 2026: D11 and D49 Dates Are Set

By COS Parenting | March 28, 2026

Spring break just wrapped and the school year end is now clearly in sight. If you have a graduate in the family — or you're coordinating schedules with grandparents, extended family, or work calendars — here's the full 2026 graduation schedule for D11 and D49.

D11 — Colorado Springs School District 11

All D11 high school graduation ceremonies are held at Ed Robson Arena on the Colorado College campus.

D11 Graduation Schedule — May 2026
School Date Time Venue
Palmer High School Tuesday, May 19 9:00 AM Ed Robson Arena, Colorado College
Mitchell High School Tuesday, May 19 3:00 PM Ed Robson Arena, Colorado College
Coronado High School Wednesday, May 20 9:00 AM Ed Robson Arena, Colorado College
Doherty High School Wednesday, May 20 3:00 PM Ed Robson Arena, Colorado College
Achieve, Tesla, Bijou, Odyssey Thursday, May 21 TBD Ed Robson Arena, Colorado College

Ed Robson Arena is located at 715 N. Cascade Ave, Colorado Springs, CO 80903 (on the Colorado College campus). Parking is available on and around campus; arrive early on graduation days as multiple ceremonies may share venue access windows.

D49 — Falcon School District 49

D49 uses two graduation venues depending on the school: the COS City Hub for smaller programs on May 15, and Broadmoor World Arena for the larger high schools on May 23.

Pikes Peak Early College Friday, May 15 8:30 AM COS City Hub
Springs Studio for Academic Excellence Friday, May 15 11:30 AM COS City Hub
Patriot Applied Learning Campus Friday, May 15 3:00 PM COS City Hub
Sand Creek High School Saturday, May 23 9:00 AM Broadmoor World Arena
Falcon High School Saturday, May 23 1:00 PM Broadmoor World Arena
Vista Ridge High School Saturday, May 23 5:00 PM Broadmoor World Arena

D20 — Academy District 20

The D20 calendar shows the last day of school as May 28, 2026. Graduation ceremony dates for D20 high schools are typically announced closer to the end of the year — check asd20.org for updates as they're released.

What's Next

With graduation dates confirmed, families can start planning travel, celebrations, and the all-important coordinating of grandparents' schedules. Both Ed Robson Arena and Broadmoor World Arena are ticketed events — cap and gown pickup, ticket distribution, and rehearsal schedules are typically communicated by each school in April.

For the most current information, including any updates to times or special accessibility arrangements, check directly with your graduate's school or visit the official district website: d11.org or d49.org.
