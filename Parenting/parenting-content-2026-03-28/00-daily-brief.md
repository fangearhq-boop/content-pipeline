### STORY 2: Evenflo LiteMax 30 Car Seat Recall — 14,020 Seats
- **Tier:** 1
- **Status:** NEW STORY
- **Pillar:** National Parenting
- **Posting window:** 10:00 AM MT

**Summary:** Evenflo recalled approximately 14,020 LiteMax 30 Factory Select infant car seats on March 3, 2026 (NHTSA Recall ID: 26C002). The issue: the Spanish-language instruction manual has incorrect weight and height limits — it states 4–35 lbs/17–32 inches when the correct limits are 3–30 lbs/15.7–32 inches. The English manual and the label on the seat itself are correct. These seats were distributed only through car seat check events and community programs, not retail stores. Remedy: Evenflo will mail corrected Spanish manuals free of charge; owner letters expected March 31, 2026. If your family received a seat at a car seat check event, check the manual language. Contact: 1-800-233-5921 or evenflo.com.

- **Tier:** 2
- **Status:** NEW STORY
- **Pillar:** Local Events
- **Posting window:** 3:00 PM MT

**Summary:** Work is underway to expand Blodgett Open Space on the northwest side of Colorado Springs. The project will add 14 miles of new and improved trails, including dedicated mountain bike-only trails from a new quarry trailhead and hiking-only trails on the northern portion. A new 90-space North trailhead parking lot is being built, plus an additional 50-stall Quarry Trailhead lot. Trails will open in phases throughout summer 2026. The project is funded through the TOPS program and a $250,000 State Trails Grant. Goal: eventually connect Blodgett to Wilson Ranch Park and the regional trail network.
