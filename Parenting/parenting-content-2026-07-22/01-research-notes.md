## STORY 5: Rosemont Reservoir — Fishing Access for COS Families

**Source: CSU.org, cpw.state.co.us, KOAA, KRDO, uncovercolorado.com, snoflo.org**

- **Location:** Penrose-Rosemont Reservoir SWA, approximately 12 miles SW of Colorado Springs along Gold Camp Road; south side of Pikes Peak at 9,600+ ft elevation [HIGH — CSU.org, uncovercolorado.com]
- **Fishing access:** Open mid-May through October, 5 a.m.–9 p.m. [HIGH — cpw.state.co.us, snoflo.org]
- **Allowed:** Flies and lures only; no bait fishing [HIGH — cpw.state.co.us]
- **License required:** Valid Colorado hunting/fishing license OR State Wildlife Area (SWA) pass for ages 16 and older [HIGH — cpw.state.co.us]
- **Hike:** ~0.5-mile hike from parking lot to reservoir [HIGH — uncovercolorado.com, multiple sources]
- **Prohibited:** Swimming, wading, all water contact; ice fishing [HIGH — cpw.state.co.us]
- **Expanded recreation:** Colorado Springs City Council approved resolution for camping + non-motorized boating expansion in partnership with CPW in early 2026 [HIGH — KOAA, Colorado Politics]
- **Campground status:** As of Feb 2026, in Teller County land-use permitting phase (Spring/Summer 2026 estimated); target opening was "around July 4, 2026" per CPW aspirational goal — current actual status unconfirmed [MEDIUM — KRDO Feb 2026]
- **What families should verify:** Check cpw.state.co.us/state-wildlife-areas/rosemont-reservoir-swa or call CPW for current campground/boating access status [LOW — inferred]
