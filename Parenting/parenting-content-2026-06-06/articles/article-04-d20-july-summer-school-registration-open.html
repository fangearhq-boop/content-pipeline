Academy District 20 families still have time to enroll their middle and high school students in summer school for the July session. Registration is currently open and is handled online through Infinite Campus — the same system D20 families use for attendance and grade tracking during the school year.

The June session deadline has passed, but the July session remains available to students who need it. If your family has been waiting to see whether summer school makes sense, now is the time to make that decision.

What D20 Summer School Offers

D20 summer school is designed to serve students in three main situations: those who need to recover credit after a difficult school year, students who want to fulfill a graduation requirement ahead of schedule, and students who want to explore a subject of interest during the summer months.

Credit recovery is the most common use case — if a student did not pass a required course during the school year, summer school provides a structured path to earn that credit before the next grade level begins. Enrichment coursework gives motivated students the chance to get ahead or explore areas outside the standard curriculum. Graduation requirement fulfillment is particularly relevant for rising juniors and seniors who want to clear requirements early.

How to Register

Registration is completed online through Infinite Campus. Colorado Springs families with existing D20 accounts can log in through the district's parent portal. If you do not have an Infinite Campus account or have forgotten your credentials, contact your student's school directly for assistance.

Before registering, visit asd20.org/summer-school to review current course offerings, confirm the July session registration deadline, and check any prerequisites or requirements for specific courses. The district updates this page with current session information, so it is the most reliable source for deadlines.

A Note on Timing

The June session deadline has already passed. If your student needs summer school credit and you are reading this now, the July session is the option available to you. Do not delay checking the current July deadline — summer school registration closes before the session begins, and seats in specific courses may be limited.

Students who are on the fence about whether they need credit recovery should connect with their school counselor now. Counselors can review grades, explain options, and help families determine whether summer school is the right next step before the school year clock starts again in August.

What's Next

Visit asd20.org/summer-school for current course listings, the July registration deadline, and registration instructions. Register through Infinite Campus. If you have questions about whether your student qualifies or which courses are appropriate, contact your school's guidance counselor. D20's main line is also reachable through asd20.org.
