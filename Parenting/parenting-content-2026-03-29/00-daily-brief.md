- **Posting window:** 12:00 PM MT

**Summary:** The AAP released its 2026 Recommended Childhood and Adolescent Immunization Schedule, now officially separating from the federal CDC schedule for the first time. Key changes: clesrovimab added as a new RSV prevention option for infants (with revised guidance based on whether mom received RSV vaccine); HPV vaccination recommended starting ages 9-12; COVID-19 universally recommended for children 6-23 months. The schedule is endorsed by 12 medical organizations. The AAP separately issued a public statement calling the CDC's new plan to reclassify vaccines into tiers "dangerous and unnecessary" — saying it upends the standard expert review process. Families: check with your pediatrician for the latest guidance.
