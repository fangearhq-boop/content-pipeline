## STORY 3: AAP 2026 Immunization Schedule + CDC Controversy

**Verified Facts (HIGH confidence):**
- AAP released 2026 Recommended Childhood and Adolescent Immunization Schedule in March 2026
- Schedule is now formally separate from the federal CDC schedule
- New addition: clesrovimab (new RSV monoclonal antibody option for infants) with revised dosing guidance based on maternal vaccination status
- HPV vaccination recommended starting at ages 9-12 (confirmed in new schedule)
- COVID-19 vaccination universally recommended for children ages 6-23 months
- Schedule endorsed by 12 medical organizations including ACOG and the AMA
- CDC announced plan to reclassify childhood vaccines into three categories (recommended for all; recommended for high-risk; shared clinical decision-making)
- AAP publicly stated CDC plan is "dangerous and unnecessary" and "upends the longstanding expert review process"

**Sources:** Contemporary OB/GYN (AAP 2026 schedule), AAP News (CDC vaccine controversy statement)
