### STORY 2: CPSC Recall — Vevor Baby Swings — Suffocation Hazard
- **Tier:** 1
- **Status:** NEW STORY (Not covered in April 13 run; different from Bellabu Bear recall)
- **Content Pillar:** National Parenting (Safety)
- **Angle:** Sanven Technology has recalled approximately 1,020 Vevor Baby Swings due to a suffocation hazard. The swings were marketed for infant sleep but have an incline angle greater than 10 degrees, violating the mandatory standard for infant sleep products and the ban on inclined sleepers under the Safe Sleep for Babies Act. Sold Jan-Aug 2025 on Amazon and Vevor.com for $65-$80. Models: BB501K, BB702A, BB005K. Remedy: full refund. Call 855-599-6320.

### STORY 3: CPSC Recall — Zippee Silicone Activity Toys — Choking Hazard
- **Tier:** 2
- **Status:** NEW STORY (Not covered previously)
- **Content Pillar:** National Parenting (Safety)
- **Angle:** Mobi Games has recalled approximately 117,500 Zippee silicone activity toys due to a choking hazard. The silicone cylinder toys have spherical ends that can reach the back of a child's throat. One incident: a child placed the toy in their mouth and choked. Sold Nov 2019-Jan 2024 on Amazon, PlayMobi, and specialty toy stores for about $20. Remedy: full refund from Mobi Games.
