## Story 3: Fasando Baby Lounger CPSC Warning

### Verified Facts (HIGH confidence)
- **CPSC action:** Warning (not a formal recall — manufacturer has not agreed) [CPSC.gov]
- **Product:** Fasando baby loungers, sold by Fasando (Qiyangshiqiguangyuekejiyouxiangongsi, China) [CPSC.gov]
- **Platform:** Amazon.com [CPSC.gov]
- **Units sold:** About 6,200 [CPSC.gov; Yahoo News; local12.com]
- **Sales period:** August 2024 through July 2025 [CPSC.gov]
- **Price:** About $35 [CPSC.gov]
- **Infant death:** CPSC aware of one infant death in 2025 — 2-month-old found unresponsive after being placed to sleep in Fasando lounger on top of adult bed [CPSC.gov; Yahoo News]
- **Hazards:** Sides too low; foot openings too wide; no stand (fall hazard on elevated surfaces) [CPSC.gov]
- **Violation:** Mandatory Standard for Infant Sleep Products [CPSC.gov]
- **CPSC action:** Notice of Violation issued to manufacturer; manufacturer has not agreed to recall or remedy [CPSC.gov]
- **Consumer action:** Immediately stop using; remove foam padding; cut cover in half; dispose [CPSC.gov]
- **Contact:** CPSC.gov or 800-638-2772

### Sources
- CPSC.gov — Official warning page
- Yahoo News — "CPSC Issues Urgent Warning About Fasando Baby Loungers After Infant Death"
- local12.com — "Infant pillow recalled for falling and entrapment hazards, related infant death reported"
