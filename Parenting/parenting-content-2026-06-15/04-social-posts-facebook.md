### STORY 3: Little Grape Land Nursing Pillow Recall

#### Long-Form Post — 8:00 AM MT

```
Parents and caregivers — please share this one. 🚨

The CPSC issued a recall on June 4 for Little Grape Land nursing pillows sold on Amazon. About 1,430 units are affected, and the reason is serious: the pillow's design can obstruct an infant's breathing, posing a risk of suffocation.

What to know:

📦 Sold on Amazon.com from August 2025 through April 2026, for approximately $28-$30
🔍 No labels or markings on the product — identification is by pattern only
🎨 Affected patterns include: rose floral, alligator, bear, butterfly, cactus, construction truck, forest deer, green leaves, little bunny, spring flower, and woodland animal

If you have one:

1. Stop using it immediately
2. Cut it in half
3. Email a photo to recall@evermorepartner.com — you'll receive a full refund

No injuries have been reported as of the recall date, but the CPSC acted because the design violates mandatory safety standards for nursing pillows and infant support cushions.

If you're not sure whether your nursing pillow is affected, check your Amazon order history for purchases between August 2025 and April 2026 under "Little Grape Land."

Have you seen this recall shared in any of your parent groups? 👇
```
