NOT VERIFIEDBRIEF
Little Grape Land Nursing Pillow Recall — CPSC June 4 Suffocation Hazard - **Tier:** 1 - **Angle:** CPSC recalled ~1,430 Little Grape Land nursing pillows (June 4, 2026) due to suffocation hazard — the pillow design can obstruct infant breathing. Sold on Amazon August 2025–April 2026 for ~$28-$30. No labels or markings on product. Patterns include rose floral, alligator, bear, butterfly, cactus, construction truck, forest deer, green leaves, little bunny, spring flower, and woodland animal. Stop use immediately. Cut in half, email photo to recall@evermorepartner.com for full refund. No injuries reported at time of recall.
