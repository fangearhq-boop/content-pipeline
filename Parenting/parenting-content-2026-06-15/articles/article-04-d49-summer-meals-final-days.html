D49 Free Summer Meals End Thursday — 4 Days Left at Springs Ranch Elementary

All children ages 1-18 can eat free at 4350 Centerville Drive through June 19; D11 continues through July 31

By Jamie Rivera

June 15, 2026

Families using D49's free summer meals program are down to four days. The program ends Thursday, June 19. After that, the site at Springs Ranch Elementary closes for the summer.

For families who rely on this program, this week is the last opportunity to use it — and D11's program offers a continuation option through July 31.

D49: What's Available Through June 19

D49 has been serving free meals at Springs Ranch Elementary School (4350 Centerville Drive) throughout the summer. Any child ages 1-18 can eat free — no registration, no ID, no income verification, and no requirement to be enrolled in a D49 school or any school at all.

The schedule through June 19:

  • Breakfast: 7:45-8:15 AM
  • Lunch: 10:30-11:20 AM

Both meals are available each day. Children simply show up during the serving window — no paperwork, no lines beyond the usual cafeteria process.

After June 19: D11 Continues Through July 31

When D49 wraps up Thursday, D11's free summer meal program continues at multiple Colorado Springs sites through July 31. The same basic rules apply: ages 1-18, free, no registration or income verification required, open to children from any school district.

For D11 site locations and hours, visit d11.org. Families who've been using the D49 location at Springs Ranch can transition to a D11 site for the rest of the summer without any enrollment process.

What's Next

Springs Ranch Elementary (4350 Centerville Drive) is open for free breakfast and lunch through Thursday, June 19. No sign-up required. After June 19, check d11.org for free summer meal sites that run through July 31.
