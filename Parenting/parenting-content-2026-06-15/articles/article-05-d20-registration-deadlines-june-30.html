D20 Families: Two Registration Deadlines Close June 30 — 15 Days to Act

Summer school and school bus registration both end June 30 for Academy District 20 students — here's how to register

By Sarah Morales

June 15, 2026

Academy School District 20 families have two separate registration deadlines closing on the same date: June 30. That's 15 days away. Both involve Infinite Campus, and both have real consequences if missed — no guaranteed summer school placement and no guaranteed fall bus service.

Deadline 1: Summer School Registration

D20 summer school covers three tracks: credit recovery, enrichment programs, and graduation requirements. Registration is open through June 30 via Infinite Campus at asd20.org/summer-school.

Summer school is designed for students who need to recover credits, get ahead, or fulfill graduation requirements outside the regular school year. If your student has been putting this off, June 30 is the hard cutoff — there's no late registration process.

Deadline 2: School Bus Registration

Bus registration for the 2026-27 school year also closes June 30. The deadline applies to:

  • Current students: May 1-June 30 registration window
  • New students: June 8-June 30 registration window

To register, you'll need to confirm your current address and pay the required fee in Infinite Campus. Bus assignments are sent to families on August 1. If you miss the June 30 deadline, there's no guaranteed transportation spot for the fall — you'd be on a waitlist if space allows.

The registration portal is at asd20.org/buses-and-transportation.

How to Complete Both in Infinite Campus

Both registrations are handled through Infinite Campus, D20's student information portal. Log in at your district's Infinite Campus URL, navigate to your student's profile, and look for registration or enrollment options. Each registration is a separate process — completing one does not complete the other.

If you haven't logged into Infinite Campus recently and don't remember your credentials, the D20 help desk at asd20.org can assist with account recovery before the deadline.
