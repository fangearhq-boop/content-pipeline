### STORY 1: YCXXKJ Baby Bath Seat Recall
**Tier:** 1 | **Pillar:** National Parenting | **Byline:** Jamie Rivera

**Why it's Tier 1:** CPSC issued both a formal recall AND a separate "stop use immediately" warning for this product. High unit count (~9,000). Amazon product — very likely to be in COS households. Drowning risk is the most urgent category of product safety hazard for infants.

**Key angles:**
- Different from NFSVLB (June 2): different brand, different model, different color options, different contact info
- CPSC escalated to dual recall/warning — higher urgency than typical recall
- Amazon purchasers: sold May 2024–October 2025 — wide window, many potential buyers
- Clear action steps: model number on back, email with two photos

### STORY 2: Vevor Baby Swing Recall
**Tier:** 1 | **Pillar:** National Parenting | **Byline:** Sarah Morales

**Why it's Tier 1:** Safe Sleep for Babies Act violation — the law was specifically created after infant deaths in inclined sleepers (Fisher-Price Rock 'n Play scandal). Angle here is that these products STILL appear on Amazon despite the ban being in effect. Suffocation risk in sleep products is the highest severity infant safety category.

**Key angles:**
- Links to broader Safe Sleep for Babies Act context (explains WHY >10 degree incline is dangerous)
- Double channel: both Vevor.com AND Amazon — buyers may not know which they used
- Models: three distinct color/base combinations — easy identification
- COS connection: "If you bought a baby swing online in early-to-mid 2025, check these model numbers"

**Content plan:** Article + 2 X posts + FB long-form + FB image caption + 2 image concepts
