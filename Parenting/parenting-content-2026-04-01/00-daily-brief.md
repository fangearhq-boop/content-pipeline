### STORY 3: AAP Alert — Vitamin K Refusal Linked to Preventable Newborn Brain Bleeds [TIER 2]
The April 2026 issue of *Pediatrics* (the American Academy of Pediatrics journal) includes a sentinel event report titled "Vitamin K Deficiency Bleeding After Refusal: A Sentinel Event in a Misinformation Era." The article addresses a documented pattern of parents refusing the standard Vitamin K injection at birth — sometimes based on online health misinformation — and the resulting preventable cases of Vitamin K Deficiency Bleeding (VKDB), which can cause serious brain injury or death in newborns. A separate AAP study also highlights that health misinformation is now a top concern among U.S. pediatricians.

**Why it matters:** Vitamin K refusal is a real and growing trend among new parents, often driven by social media content. Colorado Springs parents expecting or recently postpartum should be aware of the published guidance.
