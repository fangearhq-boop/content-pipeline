D11 End-of-Year Guide: Graduation Dates, Last Day, and What to Know Before Summer

By Jamie Rivera | May 12, 2026 | COS Parenting

The final stretch of the 2025-26 school year is here. Colorado Springs families in District 11 have 10 days left before summer officially begins — and there are a few key dates worth having on your radar right now.

The last day of school for D11 students is Friday, May 22. For families in Academy District 20, school wraps up one day earlier on Thursday, May 21. If your family spans both districts — common in Colorado Springs, where siblings sometimes attend different schools — mark both dates now.

D11 Graduation Schedule

If you have a high school senior graduating from a D11 school this year, here's what you need to know. Ceremonies are held at Ed Robson Arena on the Colorado College campus.

Confirm parking and ticketing information directly with your school, as seating and entry procedures at Ed Robson Arena can vary by ceremony. Arriving early — at least 30 to 45 minutes before the stated start time — is advisable for larger ceremonies like Palmer and Coronado.

End-of-Year Logistics for Families

The last days of school in COS bring the usual mix of field days, award ceremonies, and classroom cleanouts. A few practical reminders:

  • Chromebooks and devices: Most D11 schools require students to return district-issued devices before or on the last day. Check with your child's school for specific instructions and due dates.
  • Library books and materials: Outstanding library books or classroom items may result in holds on records. A quick check of your school's communication platform now can save last-minute scrambling.
  • Summer meal programs: D11 has historically offered free summer meal sites for families. Details for summer 2026 should be announced on the district's website in the coming days.

The Labor Dispute: Context for Fall

As the school year closes, it's worth acknowledging the context Colorado Springs families are navigating. District 11 ended its 56-year master bargaining agreement with the Colorado Springs Education Association in December 2024. As of early May 2026 — more than 300 days later — a new agreement has not been reached.

Educators and parents organized a picket at the D11 school board meeting on May 6, 2026. Teachers held a one-day strike in October 2025. The district's position, as expressed in a recent Colorado Springs Gazette editorial, is that schools perform better without a union contract in place. The union disagrees.

For families deciding whether to enroll new students in D11 for fall 2026, or planning for elementary-to-middle and middle-to-high school transitions, it may be worth attending a board meeting or reaching out directly to school administrators this summer to get a clearer picture of staffing plans and class sizes heading into the new year.

What's Next

D11 families: May 22 is the last day of school. D20 families: May 21. Graduation ceremonies at Ed Robson Arena run May 19-21. Summer programs, including free meal sites and enrichment offerings, will be announced through district channels in the coming weeks. Stay connected to your school's communication platform and the D11 website at d11.org for updates.
