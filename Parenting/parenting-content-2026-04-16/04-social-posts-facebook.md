## STORY 2: Goregent Infant Walker Recall — Tier 1

### Long-Form Post

⚠️ **If you bought a Goregent infant walker on Amazon earlier this year, please read this.**

The U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC) issued a recall on March 19, 2026, for approximately 90 Goregent infant walkers. These walkers were sold exclusively on Amazon through the Goregent Official Store in January 2026, priced at around $90.

The hazard is serious: the walker is narrow enough to fit through standard doorways and doesn't stop at the edge of a step — two features that can lead to a dangerous fall for infants. While no injuries have been reported yet, the CPSC is urging families to stop using these walkers immediately.

Here's what the recalled product looks like: green plastic frame, animal-print fabric seat, activity tray with lights and music, a round base, and six wheels.

To receive a full refund, the CPSC says to stop use, disassemble the walker, write "Recalled" on the product, and email GoregentInfantWalkersRecall@outlook.com with your purchase information.

If you're unsure whether your walker is affected, check your Amazon order history for January 2026 — that's the only sales window for this product.

## STORY 4: AAP HPV Vaccination — Tier 2

### Long-Form Post

💉 **The AAP is recommending the HPV vaccine start at age 9 — and there's a good reason why.**

On April 7, 2026, the American Academy of Pediatrics published a blog post titled "The Time Is Now — Starting HPV Protection Early," reinforcing its guidance that pediatricians should offer the HPV vaccine to kids as young as age 9.

This is different from the CDC's updated one-dose recommendation at age 11–12. The AAP continues to recommend a two-dose series starting at 9–12, with the idea that starting earlier gives families more flexibility — and research shows it improves the chances of completing the series by age 13.

Why does it matter? The HPV vaccine protects against cancers, not just sexually transmitted infections. Cervical cancer, throat cancer, and several others are linked to HPV, and protection is strongest when the vaccine series is completed before any exposure.

If your child is 9 or older and hasn't started the HPV series, it's worth bringing up at your next pediatrician visit.

## STORY 5: D11 Budget Season — Tier 2

### Long-Form Post

🏫 **D11 is cutting central administration for the third year in a row — and redirecting those resources to classrooms.**

Colorado Springs School District 11 is deep into budget planning for 2026-27, and the approach is consistent with recent years: trim district-level administration, and put more support into schools and teachers.

KOAA confirmed that D11's budget discussions include continued cuts to central administration, while keeping school-level programs and staffing intact. This is the third consecutive year D11 has taken this approach to right-size district operations.

For teachers, the 2025-26 school year brought the region's most generous compensation package — what district leaders described as "double-digit increases." Starting pay for teachers hit $51,510 for 2025-26.

For families, the bottom line is that classroom resources and school staffing aren't on the cutting block. The focus is on administrative efficiency at the top.
