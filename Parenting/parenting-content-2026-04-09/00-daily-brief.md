### STORY 2: CPSC Recalls Vevor Baby Swings Over Safe Sleep Violation

**Tier:** 1 (Safety Recall — CPSC Official)
**Content Pillar:** National Parenting
**Platforms:** X, Facebook, Article
**Angle:** Sanven Technology recalls Vevor baby swings (models BB501K, BB702A, BB005K). The seat incline exceeds 10 degrees, violating the Safe Sleep for Babies Act. Sold on Vevor.com and Amazon January through August 2025 for $65-$80. No injuries reported, but inclined sleepers have been linked to 94+ infant deaths since 2005.
**Why it matters:** HIGH urgency — parents with infants may have these swings. Inclined infant products are a known and documented hazard.
**Posting window:** 10:00 AM MT
**Key facts (HIGH confidence — CPSC.gov official recall):**
- Recalling company: Sanven Technology Co., Ltd.
- Brand: Vevor; Models: BB501K, BB702A, BB005K
- Hazard: incline exceeds 10 degrees; violates Safe Sleep for Babies Act
- Sale period: January–August 2025; channels: Vevor.com, Amazon.com; price: $65–$80
- Injuries: none reported from this product
- Context: inclined sleepers linked to 94+ U.S. infant deaths 2005–2019
- Remedy: full refund; contact (855) 599-6320 or recalling@vevor.com
