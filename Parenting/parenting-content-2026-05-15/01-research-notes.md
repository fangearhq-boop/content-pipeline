### Colorado SUN Bucks (Summer EBT)
- **[HIGH]** Program: SUN Bucks (Summer EBT), administered by Colorado Department of Human Services (cdhs.colorado.gov/summer-ebt)
- **[HIGH]** Amount: $120 per eligible child (cdhs.colorado.gov/summer-ebt)
- **[HIGH]** Benefits issued: May 2026; returning participants added to existing EBT card end of May (cdhs.colorado.gov)
- **[HIGH]** Eligibility: children in NSLP free/reduced meal program, or household receives SNAP or Colorado Works (cdhs.colorado.gov)
- **[HIGH]** Auto-enrollment for most eligible families; some may need to apply manually (cdhs.colorado.gov)
- **[HIGH]** Benefits valid: 122 days from issue date; usable at any EBT-accepting grocery store (cdhs.colorado.gov)
- **[HIGH]** Support: 1-888-328-2656; cdhs_sebt_supportcenter@state.co.us (cdhs.colorado.gov)
- **[MEDIUM]** New participants receive new EBT card mailed to address on file (cdhs.colorado.gov)
