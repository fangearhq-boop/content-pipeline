D11 School Year Ends May 22 — What COS Families Need to Wrap Up This Week

By Sarah Morales | COS Parenting | May 15, 2026

Colorado Springs School District 11 families have one week left in the 2025–2026 school year. The last day of school is Friday, May 22 — seven days from today. Before then, graduation ceremonies take place May 19 and 20, and families across the district have some practical decisions to make before summer begins.

For parents with kids in D49, Sand Creek High School graduation is May 23 at Broadmoor World Arena, with the last school day approaching shortly after. D20 graduation season is largely complete, with most ceremonies having taken place May 12–16 at Clune Arena on the US Air Force Academy.

Things to Handle Before May 22

Summer childcare and camp registration: If your kids need structured care starting in June, this is the week to act. Many Colorado Springs programs have June openings still available, but the window is closing. Programs at the YMCA of the Pikes Peak Region, Colorado Tinkering School, and other providers listed at coloradospringssummercamps.com are filling now.

School meal programs: Free and reduced-price meals through D11 schools end on May 22. Starting May 23, families who rely on school meals will need to identify alternatives. USDA Summer Food Service Program sites operate throughout Colorado Springs over the summer — check with D11 directly at d11.org or contact Colorado 211 for a list of summer meal locations near you.

Library and enrichment sign-ups: The Pikes Peak Library District's Summer Adventure program opens June 1 and is free for all ages. Registering early gets your family access to summer event slots that can fill quickly.

D49 and D20 Updates

Sand Creek High School in D49 holds its graduation ceremony on Saturday, May 23 at Broadmoor World Arena. D49 families should confirm their specific last-day date at d49.org. D20 families: with graduation ceremonies completed and the school year wrapping up, check asd20.org for any final-week details specific to your school.

What's Next

The D11 last day of school is Friday, May 22. The PPLD Summer Adventure program sign-up begins June 1 at ppld.org. COS outdoor pools open Memorial Day weekend starting May 24 at Monument Valley and Wilson Ranch pools through PPYMCA — ppymca.org for passes and hours. Summer meal program details are available through d11.org and Colorado 211.
