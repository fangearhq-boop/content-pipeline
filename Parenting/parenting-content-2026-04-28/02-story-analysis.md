- **Tier:** 1
- **Content Pillar:** National Parenting (Safety)
- **Primary Angle:** CPSC is urging parents to destroy — not return — a "Relaxing Baby" swim float sold on Amazon after a 2-year-old drowned. The seller won't cooperate with a formal recall, so CPSC went directly to consumers.

- **Key Fact for Lead:** One child death. Seller unresponsive. CPSC had to issue a warning rather than a recall.

### STORY 5: Bedtime Routine Research — 1+ Hour More Sleep
- **Tier:** 2
- **Content Pillar:** National Parenting
- **Primary Angle:** Multinational research shows kids with a consistent nightly bedtime routine get more than one hour of extra sleep per night — and the more consistent the routine, the bigger the benefit.
- **Secondary Angle:** Pairs with AAP's 2026 framework shift on screen time (less about strict limits, more about timing and context) — screen-free wind-down is now part of the evidence-based recommendation.
- **Key Fact for Lead:** 1+ hour more sleep per night from a nightly routine. Dose-dependent: every night > some nights > no routine.
- **What's Next:** Practical takeaway — dim lights, close blinds, read stories, start early and stay consistent.
