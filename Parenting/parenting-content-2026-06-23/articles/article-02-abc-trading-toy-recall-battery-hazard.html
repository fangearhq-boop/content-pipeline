CPSC Recalls ABC Trading Light-Up Glasses and Electronic Pet Toys — Button Cell Battery Hazard

By Sarah Morales | COS Parenting | June 23, 2026

The U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC) has issued recalls for ABC Trading's WSDZ Light-Up Glasses and two electronic pet cage toy models — the Dinosaur Tribes and My Pet Bird Cute Bird Tribes — due to a deadly button cell battery ingestion hazard. About 84,700 units are affected.

Parents who have these products at home should stop using them immediately and follow the refund instructions below.

Which Products Are Recalled

Three specific products are included in the ABC Trading recall:

  • WSDZ Light-Up Glasses — plastic glasses with a push button that activates lights; model 6300RP
  • Electronic Pet Cage — Dinosaur Tribes — silver plastic cage with a blue bottom containing a red dinosaur and yellow egg; model 8266 (ZH998-22)
  • My Pet Bird Cute Bird Tribes — pink plastic cage with a blue bird inside; model ZH998-23

All three products were sold at TOYZ and Joissu Product stores nationwide from November 2022 through October 2025, priced between $5 and $9.

What Makes This Dangerous

The hazard is that the battery compartments on all three products can be opened too easily by children. The batteries inside are button cell or coin batteries — small, disc-shaped batteries that children can mistake for toys or candy.

When swallowed, button cell batteries cause internal chemical burns. The damage can begin within two hours and can be fatal. This is not a hazard that resolves on its own or gives caregivers time to observe and wait — if a child swallows a battery, seek emergency medical attention immediately.

What to Do

Stop using all recalled products immediately. Here are the steps to receive a full refund:

  1. Dispose of the product in the trash
  2. Take a photo of the disposed toy in the trash
  3. Email the photo to recallabc@gmail.com
  4. Receive a full refund of the retail price

For questions, contact ABC Trading toll-free at 323-581-3688, Monday through Friday from 9:30 AM to 6:00 PM PT. You can also visit abctradinginc.com/recall-2 or the CPSC website at cpsc.gov/Recalls.
