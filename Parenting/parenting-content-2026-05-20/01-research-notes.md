## STORY 2: YCXXKJ Bath Seat Recall

### Verified Facts (HIGH)
- Brand: YCXXKJ; sold on Amazon by BenTalk — source: CPSC.gov official recall page
- Units: approximately 9,000 — source: CPSC.gov
- Hazard: unstable, tips over while in use → drowning risk; violates mandatory standard for infant bath seats — source: CPSC.gov
- Description: blue, gray, pink, yellow; two detachable arm restraints; four suction cups; three toy attachments (duck, turtle, whale) — source: CPSC.gov
- Action required: DISCARD immediately — firm is no longer responding to consumers — source: CPSC.gov
- Source link: cpsc.gov/Recalls/2026/YCXXKJ-Baby-Bath-Seats-Recalled-Due-to-Risk-of-Serious-Injury-or-Death
- This is a separate recall from: Cosyland tower stools (May 19), BabyBond gates (May 16), Amazon baby bath seats with 9 brands (May 16/18 coverage)

### Verified Facts (MEDIUM)
- Also related: NFSVLB bath seats (~1,430 units) also recalled (Amazon, May-Oct 2025, $25-40, model ZY2025) — separate recall but same category; no refund program for BenTalk/YCXXKJ
- Trankerloop bath seats also recalled in similar timeframe — source: CPSC.gov
