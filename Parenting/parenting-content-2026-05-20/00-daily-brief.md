### STORY 2: CPSC Recall — YCXXKJ Baby Bath Seats (~9,000 Units) — Firm Not Responding, Discard
**Tier:** 1
**Pillar:** National Parenting
**Platforms:** X (2 posts), Facebook (Long-Form + Caption), Article
**Byline:** Jamie Rivera

CPSC has recalled approximately 9,000 YCXXKJ-brand baby bath seats sold on Amazon. The seats are unstable and can tip over while in use, posing a risk of serious injury or death due to drowning. The firm is NO LONGER RESPONDING to consumers — CPSC is directing families to DISCARD the product rather than seek a refund. Seats come in blue, gray, pink, and yellow with two detachable arm restraints, four suction cups, and three toy attachments (duck, turtle, whale).

**Key facts:**
- Brand: YCXXKJ (sold on Amazon by BenTalk)
- Units: ~9,000
- Hazard: unstable, tips over during use → drowning risk
- Colors: blue, gray, pink, yellow
- Features: detachable arm restraints, 4 suction cups, 3 toys (duck, turtle, whale)
- Action: DISCARD immediately — firm no longer responding to consumers
- Source: CPSC.gov official recall page (cpsc.gov/Recalls/2026)
- Note: Different from Cosyland tower stool recall (covered May 19) and BabyBond gate recall (covered May 16)
