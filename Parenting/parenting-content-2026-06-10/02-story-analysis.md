### STORY 2: Guidecraft Kitchen Helper Tower Recall — 25,000+ Units, Fall Hazard

**Freshness Tags:** product-recall, toddler-safety, kitchen-tower

**Summary:**
CPSC recalled approximately 25,235 Guidecraft Kitchen Helper Towers in May 2026. The platform inside can loosen and detach over time, posing a serious fall hazard for toddlers. The towers were sold at Amazon, Wayfair, Walmart, Target, and Guidecraft.com from June 2022 through October 2023 for about $200. There have been 11 reports of children falling, including 3 injuries. Stop using immediately. Free repair parts available — call Guidecraft at 800-524-3555.

**Relevance:**
Kitchen helper towers / toddler learning towers are extremely popular with COS families. If families bought one between June 2022 and October 2023, they need to check it right now.

**Content Pillar:** National Parenting (Safety)

**Five Angles:**
1. The hazard: what's failing and why (platform loosens over time with use)
2. How to check if yours is recalled (item number + lot number on foil sticker)
3. The remedy: free repair kit — not a refund, but fixable
4. The purchase window: bought at Amazon/Walmart/Target 2022-2023
5. The broader context: this is a high-use product that takes regular weight bearing

**Engagement Hooks:**
- Question: "Do you have a Guidecraft Kitchen Helper Tower? Check the sticker on the bottom — now."
- Hot take: "25,000+ towers with a fall hazard for toddlers, and it could be in your kitchen right now."
