### STORY 2: Guidecraft Kitchen Helper Tower Recall — 25,000+ Units, Fall Hazard
- **Tier:** 1
- **Content Pillar:** National Parenting (Safety)
- **Type:** NEW STORY
- **Angle:** CPSC recalled approximately 25,235 Guidecraft Classic and Contemporary Kitchen Helper Towers on May 14, 2026. The platform inside can loosen over time, become unstable, and detach — posing a serious fall hazard to toddlers. There have been 11 reported incidents, including 3 injuries (bloody nose, bruises). The towers were sold at Amazon, Wayfair, Walmart, Target, Guidecraft.com, and other retailers from June 2022 through October 2023 for about $200. Remedy: free repair parts. Stop using immediately. Contact Guidecraft at 800-524-3555 (Mon–Fri, 8 AM–8 PM ET) or visit CPSC.gov for details.
- **Sources:** CPSC.gov, dailyhornet.com, gazette.com

### STORY 5: D11 Orton Academy Moving to Trailblazer Elementary for 2026-27
- **Tier:** 2
- **Content Pillar:** Local News
- **Type:** NEW STORY
- **Angle:** Orton Academy — a D11 charter school specializing in teaching students with dyslexia, opened in 2020 — will move into Trailblazer Elementary School for the 2026-27 school year.
