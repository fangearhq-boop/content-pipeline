Guidecraft Kitchen Helper Tower Recall — 25,235 Units Have a Fall Hazard for Toddlers

By Sarah Morales | June 10, 2026

If there's a Guidecraft Kitchen Helper Tower in your home, check it today. The U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission recalled approximately 25,235 of these toddler learning towers in May 2026, citing a fall hazard that has already led to 11 reported incidents and 3 injuries.

Stop using the tower until you've confirmed it's not part of the recall and, if it is, until you've received and installed the free repair parts from Guidecraft.

What the Hazard Is

The platform inside the recalled Kitchen Helper Towers can loosen over time with normal use. When the platform loosens, it can become unstable — and in some cases, detach entirely. A toddler standing on a suddenly unstable or collapsing platform faces a serious fall risk.

This is not a design defect that appears immediately. It develops over time, which means a tower that seemed fine when you bought it may have developed the problem in the years since. With 11 reported incidents — including 3 injuries (a bloody nose and bruises) — this is a hazard worth taking seriously.

Which Products Are Recalled

The recall covers the Classic and Contemporary Guidecraft Kitchen Helper Towers in nine color variations. These were sold from June 1, 2022 through October 31, 2023 at:

  • Amazon.com
  • Wayfair
  • Walmart and Walmart.com
  • Target and Target.com
  • Guidecraft.com
  • Maisonette
  • Overstock

The towers sold for approximately $200. To confirm whether your specific unit is included in the recall, check the foil sticker on the underside of the platform — it shows the item number and lot number. The full list of recalled item and lot number combinations is available at CPSC.gov.

What to Do

If your tower is part of the recall: stop using it immediately and contact Guidecraft for free repair parts. The company will provide the parts and installation instructions at no charge. Do not use the tower until the repair is complete.

Contact Guidecraft toll-free at 800-524-3555, Monday through Friday from 8 AM to 8 PM ET. You can also find detailed recall information at CPSC.gov by searching "Guidecraft Kitchen Helper."
