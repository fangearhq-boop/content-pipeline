D11 Graduation Week Finale: Achieve, Tesla, Bijou and Odyssey Celebrate Today

By Sarah Morales | COS Parenting | May 21, 2026

Today's D11 Graduation Schedule
SchoolTime (MT)Venue
Achieve High, Tesla STEM High, Bijou School9:00 AMEd Robson Arena, Colorado College

Colorado Springs District 11's graduation season wraps up today with two ceremonies at Ed Robson Arena on the Colorado College campus. Achieve High School, Tesla STEM High School, and Bijou School hold a combined commencement at 9:00 AM MT this morning. Odyssey Elementary Charter School follows at 3:00 PM MT this afternoon.

It's the final chapter of a graduation week that has moved across Colorado Springs day by day. Palmer High School's Class of 2026 walked on Monday. Coronado High School graduates celebrated Tuesday morning, with Mitchell High School close behind in the afternoon. Now the smaller schools and charter programs close out the season with their own ceremonies at the same beloved arena that's hosted D11 commencements for years.

About Today's Schools

Achieve High School is D11's alternative high school, designed for students who benefit from a smaller, more flexible learning environment. Tesla STEM High School focuses on science, technology, engineering, and mathematics — one of the district's more specialized academic programs. Bijou School serves students with specialized learning needs. Together, they represent the diversity of pathways D11 has built for its students.

Odyssey Elementary Charter School, one of Colorado Springs' longest-running charter programs, caps the day with its 3:00 PM ceremony. Its graduation marks the end of an academic chapter not just for the students, but for the families who've invested years in the program.

End of School Year

The last day of school for all D11 students is tomorrow, Friday, May 22. Whatever the week has looked like for your family — whether you had a graduate or you're just finishing out the year — Friday closes the 2025-2026 school year for District 11.

Families who couldn't attend in person can watch the livestream at d11.org/calendars/graduations.

What's Next

D11's last day of school is Friday, May 22. Summer officially begins for COS families. For those looking to keep kids engaged during the break, the Pikes Peak Library District's Summer Adventure program opens for registration now and kicks off June 2 — just a week and a half away. Visit ppld.org/summer to sign up.
