### STORY 3: CPSC Recall — Vevor Baby Swings (Suffocation Hazard, ~1,020 Units)
**Tier:** 1
**Content Pillar:** National Parenting
**Platforms:** X, Facebook, Article
**Type:** NEW STORY (different from Cosyland stool May 19 and YCXXKJ bath seat May 20)

Sanven Technology has recalled approximately 1,020 Vevor baby swings due to a serious suffocation hazard. The swings have an incline angle exceeding 10 degrees, violating the mandatory standard for Infant Sleep Products and the ban on inclined sleepers under the Safe Sleep for Babies Act. The swings were sold on Vevor.com and Amazon.com from January 2025 through August 2025 for $65-$80.

**Key facts:**
- Product: Vevor Baby Swings (model numbers BB501K, BB702A, BB005K)
- Colors: White/gray with white or gray base; black/gray with black base; "VEVOR" trademark on base
- Hazard: Incline angle exceeds 10 degrees — suffocation risk — violates Safe Sleep for Babies Act (inclined sleeper ban)
- Units: ~1,020
- Sold: Vevor.com and Amazon.com, January 2025-August 2025, $65-$80
- Action: STOP USE IMMEDIATELY — contact Sanven Technology for FULL REFUND
- Remedy: Cut swing cover in half, write "Recalled" + date on base with permanent marker, send photo to recalling@vevor.com, then dispose
- Consumer contact: 855-599-6320 (9 AM-5 PM PT, Mon-Fri)
- Different from: Cosyland tower stool (May 19), YCXXKJ bath seat (May 20)
- Source: CPSC.gov official recall, cpsc.gov/Recalls/2026/Sanven-Technology-Recalls-Vevor-Baby-Swings

### STORY 4: CPSC Recall — Bellabu Bear Children's Sherpa Robes (Burn Hazard, ~1,300 Units)
**Tier:** 2
**Content Pillar:** National Parenting
**Platforms:** X, Facebook, Article
**Type:** NEW STORY

Bellabu Bear of Costa Mesa, California has recalled approximately 1,300 children's sherpa bamboo robes due to a burn hazard — the robes don't meet federal flammability standards for children's sleepwear. Sold in popular licensed prints including PAW Patrol, Minecraft, Minions, Batman, and more. Sold at Saks Fifth Avenue and boutiques from January 2024 through July 2025 for about $60.

**Key facts:**
- Product: Bellabu Bear bamboo sherpa children's robes, sizes S/M and M/L
- Prints: PAW Patrol, Garfield, Minecraft, Minions, Batman, Justice League, Wonder Woman, Blaze and the Monster Machines, Trolls, Dora, Nutcracker, hearts, Santa, stripes, bows, dragons
- Neck label: "Bellabu Bear," "BB-NW12109," size
- Hazard: Fail mandatory flammability standard for children's sleepwear — burn risk
- Units: ~1,300
- Sold: Saks Fifth Avenue, Bellabubear.com, Letthembelittleboutique.com, Saksfifthavenue.com, boutiques; January 2024-July 2025; ~$60
- Incidents: None reported, but testing confirmed noncompliance
- Action: STOP USE IMMEDIATELY — contact Bellabu Bear for FULL REFUND or store credit
- Consumer contact: 888-703-7752 (9 AM-4 PM PT, Mon-Fri); help@bellabubear.com
- Source: CPSC.gov official recall, cpsc.gov/Recalls/2026/Bellabu-Bear-Recalls-Childrens-Robes
