### STORY 3: Amazon baby bath seat recalls — nearly 9,000 units pose drowning risk
**Tier:** 1
**Pillar:** National Parenting
**Platforms:** X, Facebook, Article
**Status:** New story
**Classification:** NEW STORY

The U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission has recalled multiple brands of baby bath seats sold on Amazon due to a tip-over risk that poses a risk of serious injury or death by drowning. Recalled brands include YCXXKJ (sold by BenTalk on Amazon, models priced $34-$40), NFSVLB, CheerKid (sold by Babibaby and Woot), and Trankerloop (~2,380 units, recalled February 26, 2026). Combined, nearly 9,000 units are affected. The seats are unstable and can tip over while in use. Consumers should stop use immediately and contact sellers for a full refund. Report the product at CPSC.gov or call 800-638-2772.
