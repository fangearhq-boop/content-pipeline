Colorado Springs families with young children have a live show to look forward to this weekend: PAW Patrol Live "Heroes Unite" comes to Pikes Peak Center on Saturday May 23 and Sunday May 24.

Shows run Saturday at 10:00 AM and 2:00 PM, and Sunday at 2:00 PM. The venue is Pikes Peak Center at 190 S. Cascade Ave in downtown Colorado Springs. Tickets are available at AXS.com or in person at the Pikes Peak Center box office.

What Is the Show About?

PAW Patrol Live "Heroes Unite" follows Chase, Marshall, Skye, and the rest of the Mighty Pups as they use their Mighty Pup Powers to stop Supervillain Scientist Victoria Vance from taking over Adventure City. The story centers on teamwork and problem-solving — themes that land well with the show's core preschool and early-elementary audience.

Live PAW Patrol shows are designed for children roughly ages 2-6, though older siblings and adults who have spent years watching the cartoon alongside their children will find plenty to enjoy. The production typically features elaborate set pieces, interaction between the performers and the crowd, and the kind of energy that is difficult to replicate at home on a screen.

Planning Your Visit

Pikes Peak Center is located in downtown Colorado Springs at 190 S. Cascade Ave. Parking is available in the surrounding area, including paid lots near the venue. Families coming to the 10:00 AM Saturday show may find parking easier than the afternoon performances.

Bring earplugs for children who are sensitive to loud environments — live shows are louder than home viewing, and even children who love PAW Patrol can find the experience overwhelming if unprepared. Consider bringing a small snack and arriving 15-20 minutes before showtime to get settled without rushing.

This weekend falls on the holiday weekend immediately before Memorial Day, and it coincides with D11's last week of school — making it a natural way to celebrate the start of summer. Tickets sell quickly for popular shows at this venue, so booking ahead through AXS.com is recommended.

What's Next

Pikes Peak Center hosts family-friendly shows throughout the year. Check pikespeakcenter.com for the full event calendar. For this weekend, the practical advice is straightforward: if your family has young PAW Patrol fans and the schedule allows it, this is a strong pick for how to spend a Saturday or Sunday in Colorado Springs.
