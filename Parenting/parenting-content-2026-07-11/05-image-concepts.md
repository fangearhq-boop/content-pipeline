### STORY 1: WonderStone Infant Walker Recall

#### Concept 1 — X Post / Facebook

**Prompt:**
A close-up scene of a generic infant walker in a home hallway near a doorway threshold. The walker is a simple, colorful plastic frame with a fabric seat — no brand markings or logos. The hallway transitions to a step or small landing visible in the background, suggesting the hazard context. The mood is cautionary but not frightening — slightly desaturated tones suggesting concern. Color palette: warm wood floors, neutral grays, soft blue plastic frame. Natural indoor lighting from a window out of frame. The bottom third of the image is open, clean floor space. Flat editorial photography style. No people, no text, no branded identifiers.

**Dimensions:** 1080x1350 (X/FB)

---

#### Concept 2 — Article Hero

**Prompt:**
Wide-format overhead view of a home hallway with an infant walker positioned at a doorway threshold — the walker sits at the edge of a step, illustrating a fall hazard. Generic plastic frame design with no brand markings. Warm natural light from above. Color palette: warm honey wood tones, soft neutral grays, and a pop of gentle blue from the walker seat. Clean open floor space in the bottom quarter of the frame. Minimal, editorial flat-lay photography style. No people, no text, no logos.

### STORY 5: Heat Advisory

#### Concept 1 — X Post / Facebook

**Prompt:**
A blazing Colorado summer afternoon scene — a sun-drenched park with dried grass and a bright, hazy sky. A drinking water fountain or water station in the foreground is the visual anchor. The mood is hot, urgent, and practical. The landscape suggests extreme heat: bleached golden grass, intense midday sun glare, and a heat haze visible on the pavement. Color palette: deep burnt gold, pale sky blue washing to white at the horizon from heat haze, warm amber sunlight. The bottom third of the image is open pavement. Editorial documentary photography style. No identifiable people, no text, no logos.

**Dimensions:** 1080x1350 (X/FB)

---

#### Concept 2 — Article Hero

**Prompt:**
Wide panoramic Colorado summer landscape under an intensely bright sun — rolling foothills with bleached grass and Pikes Peak in the distant background, partially obscured by haze. The sky is a brilliant but washed-out blue, with a bright sun near the center. The scene communicates extreme summer heat through color and atmosphere rather than explicit elements. Color palette: bleached gold and tan grass, hazy pale blue sky, bright harsh sunlight, and distant purple-gray mountain silhouette. The bottom third is open, clean foreground grass or dirt. Cinematic landscape editorial photography style. No people, no text, no logos.

**Dimensions:** 1200x630 (Article hero)
