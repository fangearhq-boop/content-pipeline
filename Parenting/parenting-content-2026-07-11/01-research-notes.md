## STORY 2: Colorado Cellphone Law HB25-1135

- **[HIGH]** Bill name: HB25-1135, official title "Communication Devices in Schools"
- **[HIGH]** Policy adoption deadline for districts: July 1, 2026 — all public schools had to post a student device policy publicly by this date
- **[HIGH]** Law's legal effective date: August 12, 2026 (not July 1 — the July 1 date is the policy adoption deadline)
- **[HIGH]** The law does NOT mandate a uniform ban — each district defines its own policy
- **[HIGH]** D49 policy: Approved July 10, 2025 (before the deadline, so exempt from needing a new policy). Bans phones K-8 during the school day. High schoolers may use phones at lunch.
- **[HIGH]** D11: Was already cited as a model district with a Student Cell Phone Policy before HB25-1135 passed
- **[MEDIUM]** D20: Subject to the same requirement; specific policy details were not confirmed via search (leadership disruption this summer may have complicated the process)
- **[HIGH]** Schools that adopted a policy on or before August 31, 2025 are exempt from writing a new one under the law
