**Posting Window:** 5:00 PM MT
**Platforms:** X, Facebook, Article

Portal Pool at Portal Park (3535 North Hancock Ave) is closed all summer 2026 and won't reopen for years. A coal mine shaft from 1945 sits as little as 25 feet below the pool surface. Spray grounds: Deerfield Hills, Venezia Park, Uncle Wilber Fountain.

### STORY 5: 1 in 5 US Teens Use AI Chatbots for Mental Health Advice — Most Haven't Told Anyone
**Tier:** 2
**Content Pillar:** National Parenting
**Status:** NEW STORY (JAMA Pediatrics June 10, 2026; not previously covered)
**Sources:** JAMA Pediatrics (June 10, 2026), RAND Corporation press release
**Posting Window:** 8:00 PM MT
**Platforms:** X, Facebook, Article

JAMA Pediatrics (June 10, 2026): 1 in 5 US adolescents and young adults (ages 12–21) — ~8.2 million young people — used AI chatbots for mental health advice. Usage jumped from 13.1% to 19.2% year-over-year (more than 40% increase). Chatbots used: ChatGPT, Gemini, Character.AI, Meta AI. 63% had not disclosed this to anyone. 43% sought advice at least monthly. Researchers recommend clinicians proactively ask about AI chatbot use and parents initiate conversations before teens turn to chatbots as primary support.
