Two Fridays Left: First & Main Summer Concert Series Wraps July 17 — Here's What's Playing

By Sarah Morales | COS Parenting | July 7, 2026

Colorado Springs, CO

If free live music, local vendors, and a relaxed Friday evening sounds like your kind of summer night, you've got two more chances to catch the First & Main Summer Concert Series before it wraps for 2026.

The series runs every Friday from 5 to 7 PM at 3305 Cinema Point in Colorado Springs, and it's been a tradition at First & Main Town Center for more than 25 years. This year's run started June 5 and closes out on July 17. Two shows remain.

What's Left on the Schedule

Date Artist Time Location
Friday, July 10 Soapdish 5–7 PM 3305 Cinema Point, COS
Friday, July 17 Sun Jr. (Series Finale) 5–7 PM 3305 Cinema Point, COS

Admission is free. The concerts are all-ages, making them one of the easier family-friendly activities to show up to without a plan — you don't need tickets, you don't need to register, and parking at First & Main is free.

What to Expect

Beyond the music, the series includes local vendors set up around the outdoor stage area, plus door prizes. Marvelous Marc brings balloon animals, which tends to be a reliable hit with younger kids who haven't gotten there before the music ends.

The venue is an outdoor plaza setting, so bring a blanket or folding chairs if you plan to stay for the full two hours. Evenings in Colorado Springs in July are usually pleasant, but temperatures can drop once the sun gets low — a light layer for kids is a smart call.

What's Next

After July 17, the series is done for the year. If you've been putting it off, this Friday (July 10) and next Friday (July 17) are the last two opportunities. More information about the series and First & Main Town Center events is at firstandmaintowncenter.com.
