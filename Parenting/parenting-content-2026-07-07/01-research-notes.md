## STORY 2: CPSC Recall — GOPO Toys Pull String Teething Toy (~70,410 Units)

**Confidence:** HIGH

### Key Facts
- Approximately 70,410 GOPO TOYS Pull String Teething Toys recalled by CPSC
- Description: off-white disc with gray ball in center, 6 multi-colored silicone tentacle pull strings, 7 soft push buttons
- Sold on Amazon August 2023 – March 2026, $11–$15
- 3 choking/respiratory distress incidents reported; no deaths
- Batch numbers affected: 250905, 250530, 250120, 240315, 231005, 230610 (printed on back of packaging)
- Remedy: Cut all silicone strings, write "DESTROYED" on body, send photo to recalls@gopotoys.com for full refund

## STORY 3: Cover All Coloradans Hits 25,000-Child Enrollment Cap

**Confidence:** HIGH

### Key Facts
- Cover All Coloradans provides healthcare for immigrant children and pregnant women who cannot access traditional Medicaid
- Program launched January 1, 2025
- Previously enrolled approximately 20,000–28,000 children
- New 25,000-enrollment cap effective July 1, 2026
- Age limit reduced from under 19 to under 18
- Cost escalation: originally estimated $14.7 million; projected $104.5 million for 2026–27 fiscal year
- Advocates warn the cap will result in more ER visits and uncompensated care for hospitals
- Colorado Department of Health Care Policy and Financing (HCPF) administers the program

## STORY 5: CPSC Recall — Uuoeebb Infant Walker (~2,650 Units)

**Confidence:** HIGH

### Key Facts
- Approximately 2,650 units recalled
- Sold on Amazon by BaoD (Hunan Suihuo E-commerce Co.), December 2024 – September 2025, $60–$90
- Colors: gray, black, or pink; collapsible; round base; 8 wheels; 7 adjustable height settings
- Production Batch 7654 printed on tracking label under the seat
- Hazards: walkers can fit through standard doorways, fail to stop at a step edge; leg openings allow child to slip through until head is entrapped
- Manufacturer (BaoD) is unresponsive to CPSC
- Remedy: Stop use immediately; email Uuoeebbrecalls@outlook.com for full refund
