## Story-by-Story Analysis

### STORY 1: Target Gigglescape Recall
**Angle:** Safety alert with clear action step. Lead with the confirmed choking incident to establish urgency. The remedy is simple (return to Target) — make that the practical takeaway. Target is a universal retailer for COS families.
**Sensitive flags:** None — CPSC source is authoritative, no speculation needed.
**Quick Reference table:** Yes — product name, recall date, units, hazard, where sold, remedy.

### STORY 2: CMZoo Little Paws Preschool
**Angle:** Lead with the local excitement angle — a zoo-based preschool is genuinely unusual and the "nature-based" framing will resonate with COS families who value outdoor education. Class size of 15 is a strong selling point. Mention that registration is open NOW and August 24 is only a month away.
**Sensitive flags:** Tuition is $220–$461/month — acknowledge this is a private program and not free. Don't editorialize on affordability.
**Quick Reference table:** Yes — ages, days, hours, tuition, class size, first day, how to apply.

### STORY 3: AAP Iron Deficiency Guidelines
**Angle:** Frame this as something parents should bring up at their child's next well-child visit, not as cause for alarm. The 2010 guidelines were last updated when many current parents of young children were in middle school — this is a meaningful update. The teen screening expansion is particularly important for families with adolescent daughters.
**Sensitive flags:** Avoid advising specific medical action. The CTA should be "ask your pediatrician" not "start supplements."
**Quick Reference table:** Yes — who gets screened (infants/teens), new thresholds, treatment approach.

### STORY 4: Bronc Day Festival
**Angle:** Lean into the "free + nearby" framing for Colorado Springs families. Green Mountain Falls is a 20-minute drive and the whole event is free. The 88-year history is a nice local character detail; the "Let Freedom Ring" theme ties into the broader national anniversary year (U.S. 250th). Families with younger children will appreciate the all-day format — something for every part of the day.
**Sensitive flags:** None.
**Quick Reference table:** Yes — dates/times, location, cost, parking info if available (note: Gazebo Lake Park is street/lot access; no specific parking data found — omit from table).

### STORY 5: BeMySecret Cars & Anime Con
**Angle:** The "kids under 10 free" hook is the strongest family-friendly angle. Lead with the free admission, then explain what the event is. The car show component broadens the audience beyond anime fans — this is genuinely a multi-interest family outing. 5th anniversary is a nice milestone detail.
**Sensitive flags:** This is a ticketed commercial event. Keep framing informational, not promotional.
**Quick Reference table:** Yes — dates, hours, location, ticket prices by age.
