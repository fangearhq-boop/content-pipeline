### STORY 1: Cosyland Children's Tower Stool Recall — 125,200 Units, Serious Injury Risk
**Tier:** 1 | **Pillar:** National Parenting / Safety
**Status:** NEW STORY
CPSC announced May 14, 2026 that 125,200 Cosyland children's tower stools (models CS0003 and CS0092-4) are recalled. The stools can collapse or tip over, and a child's torso can fit through front/back openings — posing fall, tip-over, and entrapment risks. 25 reports of stability issues and falls; 8 injuries including a fractured arm. Sold on Amazon for about $70 from April 2021 to November 2025. Free repair kit (protective nets, stabilizing feet) available from Cosyland Official. Source: CPSC.gov.
