Fall Youth Sports Registration Is Open in Colorado Springs — Soccer, Football, and Weigh-Ins Start Today

By Jamie Rivera | COS Parenting | May 26, 2026

Registration for fall youth soccer and tackle football opened May 12. The deadline is July 11 — and mandatory football weigh-ins begin today at the Sports Office.

Colorado Springs families: if you want your kids playing soccer or tackle football this fall, registration is open now through July 11. Colorado Springs Parks, Recreation, and Cultural Services (PRCS) opened sign-ups for both programs on May 12, and if you have a child who wants to play tackle football, there's one more thing to do today — weigh-ins started this morning, May 26, at the Sports Office.

Space is limited in some divisions, so families are encouraged to register sooner rather than waiting until the deadline.

Youth Soccer

The fall youth soccer program costs $86 per participant and is open to kids from pre-kindergarten through ninth grade. The program is coed at all age levels. Division breakdowns are pre-K and kindergarten combined, first and second grade combined, and a seventh through ninth grade division.

Late registration opens July 17 and adds a $5 fee per participant.

Tackle Football

Fall tackle football costs $126 per participant and is structured by grade level with separate boys and girls divisions at older ages. The divisions are first/second grade (coed), third/fourth grade (coed), fifth/sixth grade (boys and girls), and seventh/eighth grade (boys and girls).

All tackle football participants are required to complete an official weigh-in at the Sports Office before the season begins. Weigh-ins started today, May 26, and run through July 25. If your child is signing up for tackle football, schedule that weigh-in sooner rather than later — spots can fill up.

How to Register

Registration is available online at ColoradoSprings.gov/YouthSports and also in person. The website has full details on division placement, what gear is required, and how to navigate the registration process if your child is signing up for the first time. The Sports Office is also available by phone if you have questions about which division is right for your child's age and experience level.

What's Next

If you need time to decide, you have until July 11. But with limited spots in some divisions and the tackle football weigh-in window already open, the sooner you register, the more flexibility you'll have. Head to ColoradoSprings.gov/YouthSports to see which divisions still have open spots, get your child enrolled, and if you're going the tackle football route, get your weigh-in on the calendar.
