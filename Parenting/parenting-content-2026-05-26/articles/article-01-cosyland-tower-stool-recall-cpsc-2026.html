CPSC Recall: 125,000 Cosyland Children's Tower Stools Recalled Over Entrapment and Fall Hazard

By Sarah Morales | COS Parenting | May 26, 2026

The Consumer Product Safety Commission recalled 125,200 Cosyland-brand children's tower stools on May 14 after reports of collapse, tip-over, and child entrapment. Here is what Colorado Springs families need to know.

If you have a Cosyland children's tower stool at home — the kind designed to let kids reach counter height — stop using it. The Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC) issued a recall on May 14, 2026, covering approximately 125,200 units due to serious safety hazards that have already led to injuries.

The recalled models are CS0003 and CS0092-4, sold on Amazon.com from April 2021 through November 2025 for approximately $70 each. The products were imported by Cosyland Official.

What the Hazard Is

There are two distinct hazards with these stools. First, the stools can collapse or tip over while a child is using them, posing a fall risk. Second — and more dangerous — a child's torso can fit through the openings on the front and back sides of the stool. A child who falls or slips could become entrapped in these openings. CPSC classifies both hazards as risks of serious injury and death.

The agency has received 25 reports involving stability issues and falls. Eight injuries have been confirmed, ranging from scrapes and bruises to a fractured arm.

How to Get the Free Repair Kit

Cosyland is offering a free repair kit to affected consumers. The kit includes protective nets that cover the openings on the stool's sides, stabilizing feet to reduce tip-over risk, and step-by-step installation instructions.

To request your kit:

  • Call 866-677-3889, Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM Pacific Time
  • Email customerservice@cosyland.com
  • Visit cpsc.gov/Recalls for the full recall notice

Cosyland will mail the repair parts directly to consumers. Do not use the stool until the repair kit has been installed and the stool has been made compliant with the instructions provided.

How to Check Your Stool

The model number is printed on a label on the stool itself. Check the label against the recalled models: CS0003 and CS0092-4. If your stool matches either of these model numbers and was purchased on Amazon between April 2021 and November 2025, it is covered by this recall. If you are unsure, contact Cosyland directly using the information above.

What's Next
