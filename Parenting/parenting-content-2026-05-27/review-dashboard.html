Bellabu Bear Children's Sherpa Robes Recalled — Burn Hazard (1,300 Units)
NOT VERIFIEDFACEBOOK
### Long-Form Post **Window:** 8:00 AM MT 🚨 Colorado Springs parents — if your kids have a Bellabu Bear sherpa robe, please read this now. About 1,300 Bellabu Bear children's bamboo sherpa robes have been recalled by the CPSC due to a burn hazard. The robes fail the mandatory flammability standards for children's sleepwear. The robes that are affected come in prints that will look very familiar: PAW Patrol, Minecraft, Minions, Batman, Justice League, Wonder Woman, Blaze and the Monster Machines, Trolls, Dora, as well as holiday and novelty prints including Nutcracker, hearts, Santa, stripes, bows, and dragons. They come in sizes S/M and M/L, and were sold for about $60 at Saks Fifth Avenue stores and boutiques nationwide, and online at Bellabubear.com, Letthembelittleboutique.com, and Saksfifthavenue.com from January 2024 through July 2025. To identify a recalled robe, check the neck label for "Bellabu Bear," "BB-NW12109," and the size. Stop using the robe immediately and contact Bellabu Bear for a full refund or store credit. They'll provide a prepaid shipping label so you can return it at no cost to you. Call 888-703-7752 (9 AM to 4 PM PT, Monday through Friday), email help@bellabubear.com, or visit bellabubear.com/pages/recall-childrens-sherpa-robes. Do you have a Bellabu Bear robe at home — or know someone who might? --- ### Image Post Caption **Window:** 8:00 AM MT CPSC recall alert: ~1,300 Bellabu Bear children's bamboo sherpa robes recalled for burn hazard. PAW Patrol, Minecraft, Minions, Batman and more character prints included. Sizes S/M and M/L. Stop use now — call 888-703-7752 or email help@bellabubear.com for a refund.

Vevor Baby Swings Recalled — Suffocation Risk from Unsafe Incline
NOT VERIFIEDFACEBOOK
### Long-Form Post **Window:** 12:00 PM MT 🚨 Parents of babies and toddlers: another swing recall to know about. Sanven Technology has recalled about 1,020 Vevor baby swings due to a suffocation hazard. The swings have an incline angle greater than 10 degrees, which violates the mandatory federal safety standard for infant sleep products and the ban on inclined sleepers under the Safe Sleep for Babies Act. Babies placed in inclined sleepers at unsafe angles can have their chin fall to their chest, restricting their airway. The three affected models are: BB501K (white and gray with a white base), BB702A (white and gray with a gray base), and BB005K (black and gray with a black base). They were sold on Vevor.com and Amazon from January 2025 through August 2025 for $65 to $80. Stop using the swing immediately. To get your full refund, cut the swing cover in half, write "Recalled" with permanent marker on the swing's base, and send a photo to recalling@vevor.com. Then dispose of the swing. You can also call 855-599-6320 (9 AM to 5 PM PT, Monday through Friday). Were you aware of the Safe Sleep for Babies Act restrictions on inclined sleepers? --- ### Image Post Caption **Window:** 12:00 PM MT Recall: ~1,020 Vevor baby swings (models BB501K, BB702A, BB005K) recalled for suffocation hazard — unsafe incline violates Safe Sleep for Babies Act. Sold on Amazon and Vevor.com Jan–Aug 2025. Stop use and call 855-599-6320 for a full refund.
