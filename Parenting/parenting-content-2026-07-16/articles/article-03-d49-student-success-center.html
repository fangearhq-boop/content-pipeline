D49 Is Opening a Student Success Center This August — Here's What COS Families Need to Know

By Jamie Rivera | COS Parenting

Summer still has a few weeks left, but Colorado Springs school districts are already moving toward the fall. District 49 is adding something new this year: a Student Success Center opening for the 2026-27 school year, with D49 Early Childhood Education launching two new classrooms in the facility this August.

What's New at D49

The D49 Student Success Center is a new facility opening in time for the 2026-27 school year. D49 Early Childhood Education will open two new classrooms at the Student Success Center in August 2026, expanding early learning access for the district's youngest students.

D49 serves families in the eastern Colorado Springs area and has been among the fastest-growing districts in El Paso County. The expansion of early childhood programming at a dedicated facility reflects that growth and the district's investment in early learning.

For families who have a child approaching preschool or kindergarten age, it's worth visiting d49.org to check enrollment details and program eligibility before the school year begins.

Back-to-School Dates for All Three COS Districts

With August approaching, here's a quick look at when COS students head back:

District First Day Notes
D49 (District 49) August 4 New K, 6th, and 9th graders: August 3; Preschool: August 10
D11 (Colorado Springs School District 11) August 12 d11.org for full calendar
D20 (Academy School District 20) August 13 asd20.org for full calendar

Making the Most of the Remaining Weeks

With D49's first day on August 4, families in that district have about three weeks left of summer. That's still enough time to squeeze in a few last-minute adventures before back-to-school routines kick in. For D11 and D20 families, there's a bit more breathing room — about four weeks.

If you haven't already, now is a good time to check the district websites for any school supply lists, immunization requirements, or enrollment paperwork that needs to be completed before the first day. D49, D11, and D20 all maintain updated back-to-school pages with specific supply lists and schedule details by grade level.

What's Next

Keep an eye on d49.org for more details about the Student Success Center and how to access early childhood programs. For families whose kids are entering the preschool or kindergarten years, reaching out to the district's Early Childhood Education team before August is the best way to understand your options.

For all three districts, the back-to-school pages at d11.org, asd20.org, and d49.org are the most reliable sources for calendars, supply lists, and enrollment updates.
