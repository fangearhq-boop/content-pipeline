### STORY 1: CPSC Emergency Warning — Tuyedoqe Travel Bassinet (Stop Use + Destroy)

**Tier:** 1 (Breaking/Safety)
**Content Pillar:** National Parenting (Safety)
**Platforms:** X, Facebook, Article
**Angle:** The CPSC issued an emergency consumer warning to immediately stop using Tuyedoqe Travel Bassinets — and to destroy them before disposal. The manufacturer refused to issue a recall, so the CPSC issued a Notice of Violation. Hazards include strangulation risk from the restraint system and a fall hazard because the sides are too low to safely contain an infant.
**Why it matters:** This is not a routine recall — the company refused to cooperate, making consumer awareness the only available protection. About 250 units were sold on Amazon, April-October 2025, for approximately $40.
**Action required:** Stop use immediately. Remove foam padding. Cut the cover in half. Dispose of both pieces. Do not resell or donate.
**Posting window:** 8:00 AM MT (morning safety alert)
**Key facts (HIGH confidence — CPSC.gov):**
- ~250 units sold on Amazon.com, April–October 2025, ~$40
- Hazards: strangulation (restraint), fall (sides too low for infant containment), fall (no stand — use on elevated surfaces dangerous)
- Company refused recall → CPSC issued Notice of Violation
- Action: stop use, remove foam padding, cut cover in half, dispose, do not resell or donate

### STORY 5: CPSC Recalls — Olympia Pack-N-Stroll Utility Wagon + Uuoeebb Infant Walkers

**Tier:** 1 (Breaking/Safety)
**Content Pillar:** National Parenting (Safety)
**Platforms:** X, Facebook, Article
**Angle:** Two more recalled products COS families should know about: Olympia Tools' Pack-N-Stroll Utility Wagons are recalled because a gap between the organizational tray and sidewall can trap a child's head, and there's no restraint system. Uuoeebb Infant Walkers — sold on Amazon — are recalled because they can pass through standard doorways and fail to stop at step edges, and leg openings can allow infants to slip until the head becomes entrapped.
**Why it matters:** Utility wagons are popular at theme parks and outdoor events; the Olympia recall is a timely reminder to check your gear. Walker recalls are always high-priority — all infant walkers sold in the U.S. must meet federal safety standards.
**Posting window:** 10:00 AM MT (national parenting)
**Key facts (HIGH confidence — CPSC.gov):**
- Olympia Pack-N-Stroll Utility Wagons: recalled for head entrapment + fall hazard (gap between tray/sidewall; no restraint). Contact Olympia for remedy.
- Uuoeebb Infant Walkers (sold on Amazon by BaoD): recalled for fall + entrapment hazard. Can pass through standard doorways; fail to stop at step edge; leg openings allow infant to slip/head entrapment. Contact seller for remedy.
- Both violate mandatory federal safety standards (CPSC.gov confirmed)
