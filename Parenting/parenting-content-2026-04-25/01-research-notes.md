### STORY 3: Pew Research on Teens and Social Media

**Source 1:** Pew Research Center — "Teens' Experiences on TikTok, Instagram and Snapchat" (released April 15, 2026)
- Survey of 1,458 teen-parent dyads; conducted Sept. 25–Oct. 9, 2025
- Ages 13-17
- ~9 in 10+ teens use each platform for entertainment
- Mental health: most teens say platforms neither hurt nor help; only 9% (Snapchat/TikTok) and 11% (Instagram) say it hurt their mental health
- Sleep: 37% of TikTok users say it hurts their sleep; 26% Snapchat; 24% Instagram
- Parent-teen gap: 44% of parents of TikTok users think teen spends too much time; 28% of teens agree
**Confidence: HIGH** (direct from Pew Research, primary source)

### STORY 5: Colorado Preschool Supreme Court Case

**Source 1:** CPR News — "Colorado Catholic preschools' case over UPK headed to the Supreme Court" (April 20, 2026)
- Two Catholic preschools in Colorado sued the state in 2023
- Issue: Colorado's Universal Preschool (UPK) program excludes faith-based programs that discriminate based on LGBTQ family status in admission
- Supreme Court agreed to hear the case
- Schools argue: First Amendment religious freedom violation
- This is a First Amendment/religion and government case affecting preschool access statewide
