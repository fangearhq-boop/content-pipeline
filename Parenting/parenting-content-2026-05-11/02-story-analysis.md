**Core Angle:** CPSC Recall No. 26-465: EEMB USA is recalling 312,100 lithium coin battery packs sold on Amazon because the pouches are not child-resistant — violating Reese's Law. Coin batteries are among the most dangerous items children can swallow.

**Hooks:**
- 312,100 units sold on Amazon Aug 2023–April 2026; $3–$9 — almost everyone has bought batteries on Amazon

- Full refund: info@a2batt.com or eemb.com/recall
- Call to action: check your home NOW, store up high, out of reach
