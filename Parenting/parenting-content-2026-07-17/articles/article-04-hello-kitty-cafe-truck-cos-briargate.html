What to Expect

The Hello Kitty Cafe Truck is a touring event that makes limited stops in select cities. The Colorado Springs stop at Briargate is confirmed for today only, with no multi-day extension indicated at time of publication.

The featured item for this stop is the 10th Anniversary glass mug with a glitter handle. The truck will also have Hello Kitty macarons and branded merchandise available. No ticket or reservation is needed — you simply show up during the event hours.

Because all items are sold while supplies last, earlier in the day is generally better for the featured anniversary mug and popular items. The truck runs until 7:00 PM, so families have a wide window, but limited-edition items at these stops tend to sell out.

One Important Note Before You Go

The Hello Kitty Cafe Truck is credit card only. No cash is accepted. If you're bringing kids who might want to make their own purchase, make sure you have a card on hand before you get in line.

What's Next

The Promenade Shops at Briargate is in north Colorado Springs. The truck is there today, Saturday, from 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM. Follow the Hello Kitty Cafe Truck on social media for updates on availability and future stops in Colorado.
