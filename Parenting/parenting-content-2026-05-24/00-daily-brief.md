### STORY 2: CPSC Recall — ABC Trading Toy Headband and Dinosaur Toy Have Dangerous Button Batteries
**Tier:** 1
**Content Pillar:** National Parenting
**Platforms:** X, Facebook, Article

The Consumer Product Safety Commission recalled two ABC Trading children's toys on May 22, 2026, due to button cell battery hazards. The recalled toys — a pink bow toy headband (model 6300RP) and an electronic pet cage toy featuring a dinosaur (model 8266) — have battery compartments that can be easily opened by children. If swallowed, button cell batteries can cause internal chemical burns, serious injury, and death. The toys were sold at TOYZ and Joissu Product stores nationwide from November 2022 through October 2025 for between $5 and $9. No incidents or injuries have been reported. Families who own these toys should stop use immediately and contact ABC Trading for a full refund.

**Key facts:**
- CPSC recall: May 22, 2026
- Product 1: Pink bow toy headband, model 6300RP — push button on bow activates lights; pink with white polka dots
- Product 2: Dinosaur electronic pet cage, model 8266 (ZH998-22) — silver plastic cage with blue bottom, red dinosaur and yellow egg inside
- Also recalled: bird toy model ZH998-23 — plastic cage with pink top/bottom, blue bird inside
- Hazard: button cell battery compartments easily opened by children; if swallowed: internal chemical burns, serious injury or death
- Sold: TOYZ and Joissu Product stores nationwide; November 2022–October 2025; $5–$9
- No incidents or injuries reported
- Remedy: full refund; stop use, take photo of disposed product, email photo to recallabc@gmail.com
- Source: CPSC.gov, Newsweek (May 22, 2026), RTTNews, ConsumerAffairs recall roundup May 22, 2026
