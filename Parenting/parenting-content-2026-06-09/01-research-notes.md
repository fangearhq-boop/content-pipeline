## STORY 3: CPSC Pool Safety Recalls

### Verified Facts (HIGH confidence)
- Pool Drain Covers: Broqixin brand, sold by Eicong on Amazon; violate Virginia Graeme Baker Pool and Spa Safety Act; pose entrapment and drowning hazards; remedy: stop using pool immediately, contact Eicong for full refund; recalled June 2026 — Source: cpsc.gov
- Blue Wave above-ground pools 48 inches and taller: recalled due to drowning hazard — Source: cpsc.gov/Recalls/2026/Blue-Wave
- Both recalls issued around June 4-5, 2026

### Verified Facts (MEDIUM confidence)
- Broqixin/Eicong drain covers sold specifically on Amazon (confirmed per CPSC headline)
- Blue Wave is a specific brand of above-ground pools (confirmed in cpsc.gov title)

## STORY 5: Tiny Land Play Tent Recall

### Verified Facts (HIGH confidence)
- Product: Tiny Land children's play tents (multiple models)
- Hazard: fiberglass poles can shed fibers → risk of skin and eye irritation
- Sold: Amazon.com and Tinylandus.com, November 2025 through March 2026
- Price range: $54 to $70
- Incidents reported: 8 reports of skin or eye irritation; 0 serious injuries
- Remedy: stop using immediately; disassemble and keep poles away from children; contact Tiny Land for free repair kit with 4 replacement plastic poles (shipping included)
- Contact: tinylandus.com/pages/recalls
- CPSC recall announced June 2026
