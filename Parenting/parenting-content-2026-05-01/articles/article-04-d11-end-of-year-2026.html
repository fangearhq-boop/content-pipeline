The 2025-26 school year is in its final stretch for families across Colorado Springs. Colorado Springs School District 11 (D11) students have about 15 school days remaining. The last day of school is Friday, May 22, 2026. District 49 (D49) families share the same last day — May 22 — with a 3-hour early release.

D11 Graduation Ceremonies

D11 high school graduation ceremonies are held at Ed Robson Arena at Colorado College. For most schools, ceremonies are scheduled for May 19 and 20, with additional schools on May 21. The ceremony for Adult and Family Education is scheduled for May 28 at RJWAC (Robson-Harrington JJAEP Auditorium and Conference Center).

If you're a family member attending a graduation ceremony, plan for parking at Colorado College to be in high demand. Colorado College's campus is accessible from Nevada Avenue and Cascade Avenue. For families who can't attend in person, D11 typically livestreams ceremonies through its website.

The Final Weeks

The last few weeks of school tend to pack in a lot: field days, end-of-year performances, awards assemblies, and class celebrations. Check your school's communications and the D11 calendar at d11.org for any school-specific events between now and May 22.

It's also worth confirming any early-release days, field trip dates, or volunteer sign-ups with individual schools. The district calendar sets the framework, but each school manages its own end-of-year programming.

Summer Planning

With about three weeks until the last day of school, now is a good time to get summer plans finalized. Colorado Springs summer camps and programs tend to fill quickly — many popular options through the city's parks and recreation department, the YMCA, Cheyenne Mountain Zoo, Garden of the Gods, and other local organizations have limited spots. If you haven't already looked into summer camps, enrichment programs, or childcare options for late May through early August, check the city's recreation site at coloradosprings.gov and individual program websites now.

D11's extended learning programs and the community learning centers at some schools may also have summer offerings. Check with your school's front office for details.

What's Next

The full D11 academic calendar is available at d11.org. For D49 families, check d49.org for school-specific end-of-year schedules and graduation information — D49's graduation ceremonies at World Arena are a separate schedule. If your family has a 2026 graduate, congratulations — and if you're a younger-grade family counting down to summer, it's almost here.
