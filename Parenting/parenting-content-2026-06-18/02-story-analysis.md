**Angle:** What the AAP/CDC vaccine schedule divergence means for COS families at the pediatrician

**Key Angles:**

- Reassurance angle: this isn't chaos — pediatricians are clear on what they recommend

**Tier Justification:** T1 — directly impacts every COS family with children in medical care; high confusion factor in the news means actionable explainer has high value
