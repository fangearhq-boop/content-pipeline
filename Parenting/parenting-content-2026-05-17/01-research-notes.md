**Verified facts (HIGH confidence):**
- AAP Digital Ecosystems policy: January 2026 (from Pediatrics Vol. 157, Issue 2) — confirmed
- No screens under 18 months except video calls — confirmed
- Ages 2-5: limit to 1 hour high-quality content daily — confirmed
- Ages 6+: quality, context, and displacement of healthy activities are key — confirmed
- Family media plans recommended — confirmed AAP + CHOC
- Policy warns about commercial digital ecosystem design that prioritizes engagement over children's health — confirmed
- Displacement concern: screens displacing sleep, movement, in-person relationships — confirmed

**MEDIUM confidence:**
- Specific shift "away from strict hour limits toward quality/context" was the explicit framing — confirmed CHOC article
