### STORY 4: Joyful Journeys Baby Lounger Recall

1. CPSC.gov — Official recall page: "Joyful Journeys Recalls Baby Loungers"
2. cwcolumbus.com — "Baby loungers sold on Amazon and at Wal-Mart recalled over safety concerns"
3. growingyourbaby.com — "9,300 Joyful Journeys Baby Loungers Recalled"
4. classactionlawsuithelp.com — Joyful Journeys Baby Lounger Recall class action info

**Verified facts (HIGH confidence):**
- ~9,300 units recalled — confirmed CPSC.gov, cwcolumbus.com, growingyourbaby.com (consistent across all three)
- Sold at Amazon.com and Walmart.com — confirmed CPSC.gov
- Date range: December 2023 through October 2025 — confirmed CPSC.gov
- Price: $40-$60 — confirmed CPSC.gov
- Hazard: sides too low (fall risk), foot openings too wide (entrapment) — confirmed CPSC.gov
- Violates Mandatory Standard for Infant Sleep Products — confirmed CPSC.gov
- Styles: green avocado, blue dinosaur, blue lambs, blue zoo animals, green minky, neutral elephants, pink zoo animals, white minky — confirmed CPSC.gov
- Remedy: cut cover/foam/pad in half, email photos — confirmed CPSC.gov
- Contact: joyfuljourneysrecalls@myjoyfuljourneys.com — confirmed CPSC.gov
