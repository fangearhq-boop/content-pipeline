CPSC Has Recalled Multiple Magnetic Toys in 2026 — Here's What COS Families Need to Know

By Jamie Rivera | June 29, 2026

Three children's toy recalls involve magnetic hazards serious enough to cause death. All three were sold on Amazon — check your home now.

The U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission has been issuing recalls on children's magnetic toys throughout 2026, and the hazard they all share is one of the most serious in toy safety: ingested high-powered magnets. When two or more of these magnets are swallowed separately, they can attract each other through the intestinal wall, causing perforations, bowel obstruction, blood poisoning, and death.

This isn't a theoretical risk. The CPSC's language on these recalls is consistent and unambiguous: "risk of serious injury or death." Three products sold on Amazon are among the recalled items COS families should check for right now.

Montessori Busy Board Toys (Small Fish / Amazon)

The most recent recall, issued June 25, 2026, involves a Montessori-style busy board sold on Amazon by a seller called Small Fish. The magnets used in the toy can detach from the board. If a child swallows two or more of these magnets at different times, the magnets can attract each other through the intestinal wall — with potentially fatal consequences.

If you have this toy: stop using it immediately, take it away from children, and contact Small Fish for a full refund. Dispose of the product and send a photo of the disposal to confirm the claim.

Lterfear Multifunction Pounding Game — Model D888 (Findriver + Weeksome / Amazon)

This recall was issued January 8, 2026, covering 3,500 units. The Lterfear Multifunction Pounding Game is a wooden toy featuring four built-in games: whack-a-mole, fishing, a xylophone, and a carrot-cutting activity. It was sold on Amazon by two sellers (Findriver and Weeksome) between June and September 2025, priced between $24 and $28. The high-powered magnets in the toy can detach and be swallowed.

If you have this toy: stop use immediately and email findriverpoundingtoysrecall@outlook.com to arrange a full refund.

Joyreal Busy Board — SKU MX049-3 (Indream Store / Amazon)

This busy board is a wooden base measuring approximately 6.6 inches by 6 inches, with 18 LED lights, a power switch, a brightness knob, a mirror, and two keys. The hazard here is different: a small mirror attached to the board can detach and become a choking hazard; additionally, that mirror has sharp edges that pose a laceration risk. This recall violates the small parts ban for toys.

If you have this toy: stop use immediately and email indreamrecall@outlook.com. Look for "SKU MX049-3" and "Series no.20250730" written on the underside of the toy to identify the recalled version.

Why Magnets Are Especially Dangerous

High-powered rare earth magnets — the type used in many modern children's toys — are far stronger than the weak refrigerator magnets most families are used to. When a child swallows just one, it will likely pass through the digestive system without incident. But when two or more are swallowed at different times, they can locate each other through intestinal walls and attract with enough force to pinch tissue between them.

The result can be internal perforations, twisting or blockage of the intestines, and sepsis from gut bacteria entering the bloodstream. Surgery is often required. Deaths have occurred. The CPSC has spent years working to get these products off the market, and the 2026 recall wave reflects ongoing enforcement as high-powered magnetic toys continue to be imported and sold online.

What to Do If You're Not Sure

All three recalled products were sold on Amazon — not in specialty toy stores. If you've ordered any educational toys, busy boards, or wooden pounding toys for a young child in the past two years, check your Amazon order history. You can also search the CPSC's full recall database at cpsc.gov to see whether any toys you own are included in 2026 safety actions.

When in doubt: take the toy away from children until you can confirm it's safe. The risk isn't worth waiting to find out.

What's Next

The CPSC continues to issue magnet-related toy recalls throughout 2026. Bookmark cpsc.gov/Recalls and check it periodically if you regularly purchase toys from Amazon third-party sellers. For any recalled toy, the fastest way to find remedy instructions is to search the product name directly on cpsc.gov — each recall page includes a specific contact and remedy process.
