## STORY 1: Tuyedoqe Travel Bassinet CPSC Warning

**Source:** CPSC.gov official warning page, CPSC X/Twitter (@USCPSC), Threads @uscpsc

### Verified Facts
- CPSC issued a formal WARNING (not recall) urging immediate stop of use [HIGH — CPSC.gov official]
- Product name: Tuyedoqe Travel Bassinet [HIGH]
- Manufacturer: Tuyedoqe, China [HIGH]
- Hazards: (1) Restraints inside pose strangulation hazard; (2) Sides too low to contain infant, posing fall hazard; (3) No stand, posing fall hazard when used on elevated surfaces [HIGH — CPSC.gov]
- Units sold: approximately 250 [HIGH — CPSC.gov]
- Sold on: Amazon.com [HIGH]
- Sale period: April 2025 through October 2025 [HIGH]
- Price: approximately $40 [HIGH]
- Available colors: pink, green, gray, sky blue, wine red [HIGH]
- Features: foam sleeping pad and mesh canopy with three toy bears attached [HIGH]
- CPSC issued Notice of Violation to Tuyedoqe [HIGH]
- Tuyedoqe refused to recall or offer remedy to consumers [HIGH — CPSC/Threads]
- Consumer action: stop use immediately; remove foam padding; cut cover in half; dispose of both [HIGH — CPSC.gov]
- This is a WARNING not a recall — company non-compliance means no refund available [HIGH]

### Context
- This differs from a standard recall where consumers get refund/replacement
- Company non-cooperation means parents must destroy the product themselves
