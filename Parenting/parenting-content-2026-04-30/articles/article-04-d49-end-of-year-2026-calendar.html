D49 Families: Here Are Your End-of-Year and Graduation Dates for 2026

By Jenna Park | COS Parenting | April 30, 2026

The end of the 2025-26 school year is coming faster than it feels. If your family is in District 49, here's what you need to know and plan around for the next several weeks — from preschool wrap-up through senior graduations at World Arena.

Key Dates at a Glance

DateEvent
Friday, May 15Graduation ceremonies for PPEC, SSAE, and Patriot High School — held at COS City Hub
Wednesday, May 20Last day for preschool students
Friday, May 22Last day for students (grades K-12) — 3-hour early release for all students
Saturday, May 23Graduation ceremonies for Falcon High School, Sand Creek High School, and Vista Ridge High School — held at World Arena
Monday, May 25Memorial Day — district closed
Thursday, May 28Last day for teachers

What D49 Families Need to Plan For

The May 22 last day is a 3-hour early release for all students — that means school gets out significantly earlier than a normal school day. If you work during the day, plan your childcare or pickup arrangements well in advance, especially if your usual pickup time falls outside of whatever the early dismissal window is for your specific school. Check your school's website or communication channels for the exact early release time at your building.

For preschool families, the end comes a bit earlier: May 20 is the last day. Preschool dismissal might require a different logistics plan since it's mid-week, and wrapping up earlier than siblings in K-12.

The World Arena graduation ceremonies on May 23 are the big event for families with seniors at Falcon High School, Sand Creek High School, or Vista Ridge High School. Parking and traffic at World Arena for major events fills up quickly — build extra time into your arrival plan and check with the school for any ticket or guest restrictions in advance.

Graduation Venue Details

Two graduation venues are in use this year. COS City Hub hosts the May 15 ceremonies for PPEC, SSAE, and Patriot High School. World Arena at 3185 Venetucci Boulevard hosts the May 23 ceremonies for Falcon, Sand Creek, and Vista Ridge. If you haven't attended an event at either venue, check them out ahead of time to get a sense of parking options and arrival logistics.

What's Next

Add these dates to your family calendar now, and watch for communication from D49 and your individual school about specific ceremony times, ticket availability, and early release schedules. The school year is almost done — summer is almost here.
