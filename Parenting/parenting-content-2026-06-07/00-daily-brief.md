### STORY 1: RECALL — Vevor Baby Swings (Suffocation Hazard, Safe Sleep Violation)
- **Tier:** 1
- **Type:** NEW STORY
- **Pillar:** National Parenting (Safety)
- **Angle:** CPSC recall of Vevor baby swings sold January–August 2025 on Amazon and Vevor.com ($65–$80); three models (BB501K, BB702A, BB005K); incline angle exceeds 10 degrees, violating mandatory infant sleep products standard and the Safe Sleep for Babies Act inclined sleepers ban; poses deadly suffocation risk; remedy: full refund — destroy swing cover, write "Recalled" on base, send photo to recalling@vevor.com; contact Sanven Technology 855-599-6320, Mon–Fri 9 AM–5 PM PT
- **Sources:** CPSC.gov, motherly.com, myparistexas.com (CPSC syndicated)
