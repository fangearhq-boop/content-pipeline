### Long-Form Post ⚠️ If you have a Wiifo Children's Tower Stool at home, stop using it now — the CPSC has issued a recall. The Consumer Product Safety Commission recalled approximately 9,700 Wiifo-brand Children's Tower Stools (Model LT005) on April 23, 2026 due to entrapment and fall hazards. The stools can collapse or tip while a child is using them — and the side openings are large enough that a child's torso can become trapped. That's not a minor risk; it's a serious injury hazard. The CPSC received 22 reports of stools collapsing, with 6 injuries including contusions and scrapes. These stools were sold exclusively on Amazon between June 2022 and March 2026 for about $60. They come in white, natural, and light wood finishes. Check the underside label for "Model LT005." If you have one: stop using it, disassemble it, take a photo, and send that photo to support@wiifo.net to claim your full refund. One more thing: this is a different recall from the Guidecraft Kitchen Helper Tower recall issued May 14, 2026. If you acted on the Guidecraft recall, you still need to check for the Wiifo.
