### STORY 1: SHEIN Recalls Michley Children's Pajamas — Burn Hazard, Flammability Violation
**Tier:** 1
**Status:** NEW STORY
**Pillar:** National Parenting
**Sources:** cpsc.gov, wrnjradio.com, yahoo.com/news
**Posting Window:** 8:00 AM MT
**Platforms:** X, Facebook, Article

SHEIN Distribution Corporation is recalling Michley Children's Pajamas due to a burn hazard — the one-piece pajamas violate the mandatory flammability standard for children's sleepwear. Sold on SHEIN.com from May 2025 through December 2025 for about $25. Sizes 80–130 in 4 designs: green dinosaur, pink bunny, yellow giraffe, purple rabbit. Stop use immediately. Destroy by cutting in half, send photo to uscsteam@shein.com for full refund. cpsc.gov; 1-800-638-2772.

### STORY 2: BABESIDE Doll and Stroller Toy Recalled — Choking Hazard for Under-3s
**Tier:** 1
**Status:** NEW STORY
**Pillar:** National Parenting
**Sources:** cpsc.gov, wrnjradio.com, finance.yahoo.com
**Posting Window:** 10:00 AM MT
**Platforms:** X, Facebook, Article

About 2,200 BABESIDE Doll and Stroller Children's Toys recalled by CPSC on June 18, 2026. Violates the small parts ban — intended for children under 3 but the small pacifier and detachable bear eyes pose a deadly choking hazard. Sold on Amazon by HYBDOLLS. Stop use immediately. Destroy: cut bear in half + write "X" on pacifier in marker + send photo to support@babeside.com for free replacement accessories. cpsc.gov; 1-800-638-2772.
