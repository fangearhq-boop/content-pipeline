D20 Bus Registration and Summer School Deadline Is June 30 — 10 Days Left to Act

By Sarah Morales | June 20, 2026

Local News

Academy District 20 families have 10 days left to complete two time-sensitive tasks before the June 30 deadline: bus registration for the 2026–27 school year and summer school enrollment. Missing this window creates real problems in August — students without completed bus registration don't receive assignments, and summer school enrollment closes with no exceptions on June 30.

Bus Registration — What You Need to Do

D20 bus registration for the upcoming school year is handled through the Infinite Campus parent portal. If you haven't logged in yet, now is the time.

The most important thing to verify is your student's address. D20 uses the address on file in Infinite Campus to determine bus routes and stop assignments. If your family has moved, if there's an error in the system, or if your student's address hasn't been updated recently, the assignment that comes through in August may be wrong — or your student may not be assigned a bus at all. Correcting the address after the fact, once routes are finalized and assignments have been sent, is significantly harder than confirming it now.

Bus assignments are released on August 1. Families who complete registration by June 30 will receive their assignments on that date. Those who miss the deadline are not guaranteed placement before the school year begins.

Summer School — Credit Recovery and Enrichment

D20's summer school program covers two distinct tracks: credit recovery for students who need to make up coursework, and enrichment programs for students looking to get ahead or explore new subjects over the break. Both tracks share the same June 30 enrollment deadline.

If you're unsure whether your student qualifies for or would benefit from summer school, the best starting point is asd20.org, where program details and enrollment are available. Teachers and school counselors can also advise on whether credit recovery applies to your student's situation.

Why This Is the Third Reminder

We first flagged these deadlines on June 5, followed up on June 17, and are back again today at the 10-day mark because, in our experience covering D20 news, this is the window where families who intended to handle it but haven't yet are most likely to act. The June 30 deadline isn't a soft one — D20 doesn't extend enrollment or registration periods after the fact.

If you set a reminder and then forgot, or if you saw the earlier posts and thought "I'll do it this weekend," this weekend is it. The Infinite Campus portal is straightforward to navigate, and bus registration in particular takes only a few minutes once you're logged in and have confirmed the address is current.

What to Do Right Now

Log in to Infinite Campus at asd20.org and check your student's address first — that's the step most families overlook. Then complete the bus registration if you haven't already. If summer school is on the table, review the program options and get enrollment submitted before June 30.

Questions about bus assignments or transportation can be directed to D20 Transportation. Questions about summer school programs can be routed through your student's school or through the district office. All contact information is available at asd20.org.
