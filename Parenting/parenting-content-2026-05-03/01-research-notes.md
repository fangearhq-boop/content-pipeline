## STORY 2: CPSC Stephan Baby Recall

### Verified Facts (HIGH confidence — multiple sources)
- **Product:** Stephan Baby Boo Bunnie and Friends children's toys (cpsc.gov, wbrc.com, wkyt.com, Fox news, azfamily.com — multiple)
- **Quantity recalled:** About 227,500 in US; 1,400 in Canada (cpsc.gov, multiple)
- **Hazard:** Removable water-filled plastic cube can break into smaller parts → choking + laceration hazard (cpsc.gov, multiple)
- **Incident count:** 20 reports of cube breaking; 1 child swallowed sharp pieces (cpsc.gov, multiple)
- **Sold at:** Amazon, Target, Walmart, independent stores (cpsc.gov, multiple)
- **Sale dates:** August 2017 – January 2026 (cpsc.gov, multiple)
- **Recall company:** CBC Group (cpsc.gov — single)
- **Consumer action:** Stop using plastic cube immediately; remove from child reach (cpsc.gov, multiple)
- **Refund:** Proof of purchase = full refund; no proof = $10 check or $20 stephanbaby.com credit (cpsc.gov)

## STORY 4: AAP Screen Time Guidelines 2026

### Verified Facts (HIGH confidence — AAP.org + multiple mainstream health sources)
- **Updated guidelines released:** Early 2026 / February 2026 (EdSurge: "2026-02-05"; FSU press release: 2026/02; CHOC, CHLA, multiple health sites)
- **Under 18 months:** No screens (AAP; multiple sources confirm this is unchanged)
- **Ages 2–5:** Limit to 1 hour high-quality content per day (AAP; CHOC, CHLA, multiple)
- **Ages 6+:** Shift from strict time limits to quality and context (AAP; multiple)
- **Old rule replaced:** Previous 2-hour-per-day limit for ages 6+ is discontinued (multiple)
- **5 Cs framework:** Named framework endorsed by AAP (HealthyChildren.org, CHOC, multiple)
- **Device-free bedrooms and mealtimes:** Recommended (multiple sources)
- **Co-viewing:** Encouraged as gold standard for all ages (multiple sources)

### Verified Facts (MEDIUM confidence — single source)
- **"5 Cs" official names:** Quality, Context, Conversation, Co-viewing, and Child development — specifics from CHOC single source; framework confirmed multi-source
