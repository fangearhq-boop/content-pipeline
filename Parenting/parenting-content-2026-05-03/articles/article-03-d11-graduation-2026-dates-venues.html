About Ed Robson Arena

Ed Robson Arena is the athletics and event center at Colorado College, located in Colorado Springs. It's the primary venue for D11 graduation ceremonies and has the capacity to accommodate large graduation audiences. Families attending should plan for parking — the Colorado College campus area can fill up quickly during morning and afternoon ceremony times, especially on days with two ceremonies scheduled.

Check the D11 website at d11.org closer to the ceremony date for specific ticket allocations, parking guidance, and any updates on ceremony procedures. Individual schools may also send families their own communications with ceremony-specific details.

Planning Ahead for Your Graduate's Day

With two ceremonies on both May 19 and May 20, the morning ceremonies (9:00 AM) may have some traffic overlap with the afternoon ceremonies (3:00 PM). Building extra time into your arrival plan is wise, especially if family members are traveling from out of town.

For the May 21 ceremonies at Achieve, Tesla, Bijou, and Odyssey — times are listed as TBD as of this writing. Watch your school's communication channels for that information, and check d11.org for updates.

Congratulations to the D11 Class of 2026. Graduation is a big moment for the whole family — not just the senior crossing the stage.

What's Next

If you have a D11 graduate, now's the time to confirm ticket allocations, book any post-ceremony family dinners, and plan for the logistics of getting a whole crew to Ed Robson Arena. The last day of school is May 22 — just days after the ceremonies — so the whole end-of-year stretch comes quickly. Visit d11.org for the official, up-to-date calendar and any ceremony updates as they're announced.
