CPSC Recall: SEGMART Mini Toddler Trampolines — Remove the Accessories Now

By Sofia Reyes

April 27, 2026

If your family owns a SEGMART mini toddler trampoline, the Consumer Product Safety Commission has issued a recall you need to act on today. Three specific models sold between December 2025 and February 2026 are being recalled due to a strangulation hazard from accessory straps — and at least one child has already been injured.

Which Products Are Recalled

The recall covers SEGMART 55-inch mini round toddler trampolines sold as part of a promotional accessory bundle. The affected model numbers are SOSTT051BR, SOSTT051CR, and SOSTT051YP. These trampolines were sold exclusively online — through Walmart.com and TikTok Shop — from December 2025 through February 2026, at a price of approximately $140.

The promotional bundle included a hammock, punching bag, hand rings, and basketball board as bonus accessories. Those four accessories are the source of the hazard.

The Hazard

Young children can become entangled in the straps attached to the hammock, punching bag, and hand ring accessories, creating a strangulation risk. The CPSC has already received one report of a child whose neck became entangled in the hammock straps, resulting in a rope burn injury.

The good news is that the trampoline frame and enclosure net are not part of the recall. Families can continue using the trampoline itself — the problem is specific to the accessory bundle items and their attachment straps.

What to Do Right Now

If you own one of the affected models, the CPSC's recommended steps are straightforward:

  1. Stop using the hammock, punching bag, hand rings, and basketball board immediately.
  2. Cut the straps on all four accessories to render them unusable.
  3. Photograph the destroyed accessories.
  4. Submit the photos to SEGMART to receive a $30 partial refund for the accessories.

To confirm whether your trampoline is one of the recalled models, check the model number printed on the trampoline frame or the original packaging. The three affected model numbers begin with "SOSTT051."

The full recall notice is available at CPSC.gov, where you'll also find SEGMART's contact information for submitting your refund photos.

What's Next

SEGMART has not announced a replacement accessory program. Families who want to add activities to their toddler's trampoline should look for accessories specifically certified for use with indoor mini trampolines and designed without long flexible straps or ties. The CPSC recall page will be updated if additional incidents are reported or if the remedy process changes.
