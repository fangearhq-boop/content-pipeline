Garden of the Gods Art Festival Returns May 16-17 — Kids 11 and Under Free

By Marcus Rivera

April 27, 2026

One of Colorado Springs' best outdoor spring events is back for 2026. The Garden of the Gods Art Festival returns to Rock Ledge Ranch Historic Site on May 16 and 17, bringing more than 150 juried fine artists to one of the most scenic settings in the region — with Pikes Peak looming behind the red sandstone formations of Garden of the Gods.

What to Expect

The festival runs 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. on both Saturday and Sunday. This is a true fine art event — all artists are juried, meaning they've been selected through a competitive review process. You'll find pottery, jewelry, textiles, original paintings, sculpture, and glass art from artists who've traveled from across the country to exhibit here.

Beyond the art booths, the festival includes live music performances throughout the weekend, food trucks, and activities for kids. The combination of top-tier art in that setting — with the red rock formations right there as a backdrop — makes it unlike most outdoor markets in the region.

Children 11 and under are free. General admission is ticketed; adult pricing is available at dashevents.com/garden-of-the-gods-art-festival.

Plan Your Saturday: Art Festival + Touch-A-Truck

May 16 offers a natural double-header for families. Touch-A-Truck — the annual interactive vehicle event for kids — takes place the same morning at Lower Gold Camp Road, starting at 9:30 a.m. Touch-A-Truck typically wraps up mid-morning, which lines up well with the art festival's 10 a.m. opening.

If your family has younger children who love climbing on fire trucks and construction equipment, doing Touch-A-Truck first and then heading to Rock Ledge Ranch for the afternoon is a genuinely full and memorable Saturday. Both events are designed to be family-friendly and work well with mixed ages.

Getting There

Rock Ledge Ranch Historic Site is located in the heart of Colorado Springs, at the east entrance to Garden of the Gods, just off Garden of the Gods Road. There is on-site parking, though weekend events at this location can fill up — arriving before noon on Saturday is a good strategy if you want a close spot.

What's Next

The Garden of the Gods Art Festival is an annual event that's been running for years; if 2026 follows the pattern of previous years, tickets go faster as the weekend approaches. Checking dashevents.com closer to May will give you current pricing and any schedule updates. If you're a fan of a particular art medium — like ceramics or jewelry — the festival website typically lists participating artists in advance so you can plan which booths to prioritize.
