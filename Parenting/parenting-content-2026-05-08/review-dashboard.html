Garden of the Gods Art Festival Returns May 16-17 — Kids Free, Dogs Welcome
NOT VERIFIEDFACEBOOK
### Long-Form Post If your family is looking for a way to spend next weekend outside in Colorado Springs, the Garden of the Gods Art Festival has a lot going for it. The 3rd Annual festival runs Saturday and Sunday, May 16-17 at Rock Ledge Ranch Historic Site, from 10 AM to 5 PM both days. The backdrop is the Garden of the Gods rock formations with Pikes Peak in the distance — one of the best outdoor settings in all of Colorado for an event like this. Here's what we like for families: children under 12 get in free. Adults are $10, seniors and military pay $8 (with ID). If driving and parking sounds stressful, there's a free shuttle running both days from Coronado High School at 1590 W. Fillmore St., departing 9:30 AM and running through 5 PM. The festival features 150 juried artists from across the country — pottery, jewelry, textiles, paintings, sculpture, glass work, and more. There's live music, food trucks, and activities for kids. If your dog is well-behaved and on a leash, they're welcome too. It's the kind of weekend event that's easy to make a full morning or afternoon out of without spending a lot. Are you planning to head out to Garden of the Gods this weekend? --- ### Image Post Caption Garden of the Gods Art Festival, May 16-17 at Rock Ledge Ranch. 150 artists, live music, food trucks, and kids under 12 get in free. CPSC Alert — Stop Using Uuoeebb Infant Walkers Immediately (Fall + Entrapment Hazard)
NOT VERIFIEDFACEBOOK
### Long-Form Post Parents and caregivers: if you have an Uuoeebb brand infant walker at home, please stop using it right now. The Consumer Product Safety Commission has issued both a Recall and a Warning for these walkers, which were sold on Amazon (by a seller called BaoD) between December 2024 and September 2025 for $60 to $90. About 2,650 were sold. The walkers come in gray, black, and pink. They're collapsible with a round base and eight wheels. To check if yours is the affected product, look for a label under the seat that reads "Production Batch: 7654." There are two serious safety problems with these walkers. First, they can fit through standard doorways and do not stop at the edge of steps, meaning a baby can roll right off a stair. Second, the leg openings are large enough that a baby can slip downward until their head becomes trapped. The manufacturer has not responded to the CPSC's requests to offer a fix or refund. That means there's no recall remedy available from the company. The CPSC's guidance is direct: stop using immediately, and do not sell or donate this product. Dispose of it. If you've already given one of these to another family or sold it secondhand, please let them know. Have you ever purchased baby gear on Amazon and later found out it had safety issues? --- ### Image Post Caption CPSC safety alert: Uuoeebb infant walkers (sold on Amazon, 2024-2025) are recalled for fall and entrapment hazards. AAP Releases 2026 Immunization Schedule — Key Changes for COS Families
NOT VERIFIEDFACEBOOK
### Long-Form Post The American Academy of Pediatrics has published its 2026 recommended immunization schedule for children and adolescents, and there are a few changes worth knowing before your next pediatrician visit. Here are the three most relevant updates for Colorado Springs families: RSV protection: clesrovimab has been added to the schedule as an option for protecting newborns and young infants against respiratory syncytial virus. HPV vaccine: the AAP now recommends starting the HPV vaccine series at ages 9 to 12 — earlier than the CDC's recommendation of ages 11 to 12. If your child is in that age range, it's worth asking your pediatrician when they think is the right time to start. COVID-19: the AAP recommends vaccination for all children ages 6 to 23 months, as well as older children who have underlying health conditions that put them at higher risk. The schedule has been endorsed by 12 medical organizations, including the American Medical Association and the American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists. The full schedule is available at HealthyChildren.org. Colorado Springs pediatricians follow the AAP schedule. If you have questions about the changes or where your child stands, your child's doctor is the right starting point. Does your family follow the AAP immunization schedule, or do you have a different approach with your pediatrician? --- ### Image Post Caption AAP released the 2026 immunization schedule for kids.
