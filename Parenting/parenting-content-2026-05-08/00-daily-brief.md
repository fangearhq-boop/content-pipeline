### STORY 3: CPSC Alert — Stop Using Uuoeebb Infant Walkers Immediately (Fall + Entrapment Hazard)
- **Tier:** 1
- **Pillar:** National Parenting
- **Posting window:** 12:00 PM MT
- **Platforms:** X, Facebook, Article
- **Classification:** NEW STORY
- **Summary:** The CPSC has issued a Warning and Recall against approximately 2,650 Uuoeebb brand infant walkers (sold by BaoD on Amazon, Dec 2024-Sept 2025, $60-90). The walkers violate the mandatory safety standard in two serious ways: they can fit through a standard doorway and fail to stop at step edges (fall hazard), and leg openings allow babies to slip down until their heads become entrapped. The manufacturer has been unresponsive to CPSC. Consumers should stop use immediately, do not sell or donate, and dispose of them. They come in gray, black, and pink; look for "Production Batch: 7654" label under the seat.
- **Sources:** cpsc.gov (Recall notice: Uuoeebb Infant Walkers); cpsc.gov (Warning: same product); mother.ly (Uuoeebb infant walkers recalled); thehill.com (Amazon baby products recalled over "risk of serious injury or death")

### STORY 5: AAP Releases 2026 Immunization Schedule — Key Changes for COS Families
- **Tier:** 2
- **Pillar:** National Parenting
- **Posting window:** 5:00 PM MT
- **Platforms:** X, Facebook, Article
- **Classification:** NEW STORY (immunization schedule update; previously covered screen time guidance May 7 and sun safety May 6 — this is different AAP content)
- **Summary:** The American Academy of Pediatrics has published its 2026 recommended childhood and adolescent immunization schedule. Key changes: (1) clesrovimab added for RSV protection in newborns and infants; (2) HPV vaccine age range updated to 9-12 years per AAP policy (the CDC recommends 11-12); (3) COVID-19 vaccine recommended for all children ages 6-23 months and older children in certain risk groups.
