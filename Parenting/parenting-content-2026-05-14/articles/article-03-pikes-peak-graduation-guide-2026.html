Pikes Peak Area Graduation Guide 2026: D11, D20, and D49 Dates, Venues, and What to Know

By Sarah Morales | COS Parenting | May 14, 2026

Colorado Springs, CO

Graduation season is here in the Pikes Peak region. If your family is navigating a ceremony — or you want to plan ahead for the remaining events — here is a consolidated reference for District 11, Academy District 20, and District 49. Some D20 ceremonies have already taken place; D11 and remaining D49 ceremonies are in the next two weeks.

Academy District 20 — Ceremonies May 12-16

D20 held most of its high school graduation ceremonies the week of May 12. Clune Arena at the U.S. Air Force Academy was the venue for most ceremonies, with one held at Pine Creek High School.

District 49 — Ceremonies May 15-23

D49 ceremonies span several weeks, with both smaller specialized schools and large high schools scheduled through May 23. The largest ceremony — Sand Creek High School — closes out the D49 season at the Broadmoor World Arena.

D49 typically posts ceremony information and livestream links at d49.org. For families traveling to the Broadmoor World Arena on May 23, plan for limited parking and consider arriving early.

Colorado Springs District 11 — Ceremonies May 19-20

D11's major high school graduation ceremonies are scheduled for May 19 and 20. These are the largest ceremonies in the region and typically draw significant family attendance.

D11's last day of school is May 22. The district's Adult and Family Education graduation is scheduled for May 28 at 7 PM at Roy J. Wasson Academic Campus Auditorium.

Check d11.org for venue details and any changes closer to the ceremony dates. Livestream options are typically available for families who cannot attend in person.

Pikes Peak Community College

Pikes Peak State College (formerly Pikes Peak Community College) holds its commencement ceremony separately. Check pikespeak.edu for 2026 commencement dates and registration requirements for participating graduates.

What's Next

After graduation season wraps, Memorial Day weekend (May 23-25) brings Territory Days to Old Colorado City — the event's 50th anniversary — and kicks off the summer pool season. Monument Valley and Wilson Ranch pools open May 24. The Panorama Park Splash Pad opens May 25.

For families whose students are finishing middle school or elementary school, check with your individual school about end-of-year events and last-day logistics before May 22.
