### STORY 1: Fasando Baby Lounger CPSC Urgent Warning — Infant Death Reported
**Tier:** 1
**Pillar:** National Parenting (Safety)
**Status:** NEW STORY
**Posting window:** 8:00 AM MT

The CPSC has issued an urgent warning for parents to immediately stop using Fasando Baby Loungers. The CPSC is aware of a 2025 infant death involving a 2-month-old who was found unresponsive after being placed to sleep in a Fasando-branded baby lounger on top of an adult bed. The loungers violate the mandatory standard for Infant Sleep Products — the sides are too low to contain an infant, and enclosed openings at the foot of the loungers are wider than allowed, creating fall and entrapment hazards. Approximately 6,200 units were sold online at Amazon.com by Fasando from August 2024 through July 2025 for about $35. They were sold in colors including grey elephant, baby blue animals, blue animals, gold elephant, pink lion, and yellow elephant. Fasando has NOT agreed to recall or offer a remedy. CPSC urges consumers to immediately stop using the loungers, remove the foam padding, cut the cover in half, and dispose of the cover and padding.

**Pillar:** National Parenting (Safety)
**Status:** NEW STORY
**Posting window:** 10:00 AM MT

Two toy recalls were announced by the CPSC on April 16, 2026, both involving products sold on Amazon — and both posing serious risks to young children. (1) ATOYUS Children's Activity Cubes: Recalled due to choking hazard — xylophone screws and clock hands can detach, and the mallets have spherical ends that violate the small parts ban. Intended for children under age 3. Sold by Melofaver US on Amazon. To receive a refund, consumers must write "recalled" on all sides of the cube, cut the playmat in half, and email a photo to ATOYUSActivityCubeRecall@outlook.com. (2) Sanlebi Pet Vet Playsets: Recalled due to button cell battery ingestion risk. The batteries in the nail grinder and otoscope components can be easily accessed by children. Sold by Letokids on Amazon. Action: remove nail grinder and otoscope immediately, remove/dispose of batteries, send photo to letorecall@outlook.com for a $5 refund or replacement parts.
