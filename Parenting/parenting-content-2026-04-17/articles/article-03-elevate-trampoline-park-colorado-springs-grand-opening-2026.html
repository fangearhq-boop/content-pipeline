Elevate Trampoline Park Opens a New Colorado Springs Location This Saturday

By Keisha Williams | COS Parenting | April 17, 2026

Colorado Springs families are getting a new indoor activity option this weekend. Elevate Trampoline Park is opening a new COS location on Saturday, April 18, 2026, with a grand opening event that includes prizes for early arrivals and a set of launch-day deals worth knowing about before you head over.

What Elevate Trampoline Park Is

Elevate Trampoline Park is an indoor activity center brand with locations across the country. The Colorado Springs facility is a new addition to the regional Elevate footprint, and at 31,000 square feet, it's a substantial indoor venue. The Elevate brand is known for interconnected trampoline courts, foam pits, and active recreation programming designed to accommodate families, birthday parties, and group outings.

Indoor activity centers like Elevate serve a specific need in Colorado Springs — particularly during the shoulder seasons when the weather can be unpredictable. A late-April Saturday in COS can be anything from 70 degrees and sunny to cold and snowy, which makes having a high-energy indoor option a practical addition to the family activity menu.

Grand Opening Day Details

The grand opening on April 18 comes with a few specific perks worth noting if you're planning to attend:

  • Prizes for the first 250 guests: The first 250 guests to arrive are guaranteed to receive prizes. If you want to be in that group, plan to arrive early.
  • Discounted founders memberships: Opening day will feature reduced-price founders memberships for families who want to lock in a recurring membership at a lower rate than will be available post-launch.
  • Promotional birthday party packages: Birthday party packages will be offered at promotional pricing at the grand opening, which may be worth exploring if you've got a spring or summer birthday coming up.

Planning Your Visit

Grand opening days at trampoline parks tend to draw larger-than-usual crowds, particularly when prizes are part of the draw. If you're bringing young children, consider timing your arrival for when the park first opens to avoid the midday peak. Socks are typically required at trampoline parks, so having a pair on hand will save you from purchasing them at the door.

The specific address and hours for the Colorado Springs Elevate location can be found through the Elevate Trampoline Park website. If you're planning to take advantage of the founders membership pricing or the birthday party packages, it's worth confirming current pricing directly with the venue before attending.

If you're planning to attend the grand opening, visit the Elevate Trampoline Park website for the full address, hours, and current admission pricing.
