PPLD's Summer Adventure Is Free, It's for All Ages, and It's Running Right Now

By Jamie Rivera | COS Parenting | June 22, 2026

If you haven't signed your family up for the Pikes Peak Library District's Summer Adventure, the program is well underway — and it's free, it's for everyone from babies to adults, and it runs through July 31.

This year's theme is "Unearth a Story." The idea is simple: complete 30 days of activities over the summer. Activities count broadly — reading a book, listening to an audiobook, attending a library program, making something creative, trying a new activity. You track your progress through the free Beanstack app, which gamifies the whole experience with badges and progress markers that kids respond well to.

What's in It for Your Family

The prizes are genuinely worth the registration. Grand prizes include a Pikes Peak Region Attractions VIP Access Pass — which provides free and significantly discounted admission to attractions around the Pikes Peak region — and a one-night stay for two at The Broadmoor Cloud Camp. For families who live in Colorado Springs, that's a rare opportunity to experience two of the region's premier experiences without paying full price.

Beyond prizes, PPLD is offering free programs throughout June and July: family concerts, Winnie the Pooh performances, dinosaur-themed activities, wildlife programs, hands-on Idea Lab activities, and Storytimes. All of these are available at no cost.

Free Lunches and Books at East Library

There's an additional resource for families to know about. PPLD East Library at 5550 N. Union Blvd. is partnering with Colorado Springs School District 11 to provide free lunches for children and teens Monday through Friday through July 31. Free books are also available to take home at the same location.

This is part of a broader D11 free summer meals effort — see our separate coverage for more details on meal sites across Colorado Springs.

How to Sign Up

Registration takes about five minutes. Families can go to ppld.org/summer, download the Beanstack app, or walk into any Pikes Peak Library District branch and register in person. There are no income requirements and no fees involved at any stage.

What's Next

Summer Adventure runs through July 31, 2026. If your family signs up today, there's still plenty of time to complete 30 days of activities before the deadline. For program details, branch locations, and the full events calendar, visit ppld.org or ppld.org/summer. The East Library free lunch program runs Monday through Friday through July 31 at 5550 N. Union Blvd.
