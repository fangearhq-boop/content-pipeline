Free Summer Meals for All Colorado Springs Kids — No D11 Enrollment Required

By Sarah Morales | COS Parenting | June 22, 2026

Colorado Springs School District 11 is offering free breakfast and lunch this summer to every child ages 1 through 18 in the city — and you don't need to be a D11 student, or any COS district student, to access them.

The program is part of the federally funded Summer Food Service Program, administered by the Colorado Department of Education. It's designed specifically to address food insecurity during summer months when school meal programs aren't operating. Meals are available at multiple locations including schools, community centers, and playgrounds across the city.

How to Find a Meal Site Near You

There are two easy ways to locate a free summer meal site in Colorado Springs:

  • Visit d11.org and navigate to Food and Nutrition Services for the current summer meal site list.
  • Go to kidsfoodfinder.org, which maps free summer food sites across Colorado and allows you to search by ZIP code.

No advance registration is required at most sites. Children simply show up during the meal window and receive breakfast and/or lunch at no cost. There's no paperwork, no income verification, and no need to explain your situation to anyone.

East Library Meal Site: Books, Too

One particularly convenient option for COS families is the meal site at PPLD East Library, 5550 N. Union Blvd. Lunches for children and teens are available there Monday through Friday through July 31, in partnership between D11 and the Pikes Peak Library District.

While you're there, free books are also available for children and teens to take home — making the trip a two-for-one summer resource stop.

Pass It Along

This program is often underused because families don't know it exists, don't realize they qualify, or assume it's only for households in financial hardship. It's not. Any child ages 1 through 18 qualifies. If you know a neighbor, a family at your child's school, or anyone in your community who has kids and might benefit from free summer meals, the best thing you can do is share this information directly.

What's Next

Free summer meals run through the summer. For current site locations, hours, and any program updates, visit d11.org or kidsfoodfinder.org. The PPLD East Library site at 5550 N. Union Blvd. is open for free lunches Monday through Friday through July 31, 2026.
