MeadowGrass Music Festival 2026: Kids Under 12 Free, Memorial Day Weekend in Black Forest

By Marcus Rivera | COS Parenting | April 26, 2026

Memorial Day Weekend in the Pikes Peak region has a well-loved tradition: the MeadowGrass Music Festival. The 17th annual edition runs May 22-24, 2026 at La Foret Conference and Retreat Center in Black Forest — just north of Colorado Springs — and this year, as always, children 12 and under get in free with a paid adult.

That's a meaningful detail for families trying to stretch a weekend budget while still creating an experience worth remembering. Three days of live music, camping in the ponderosa pines, hands-on kids' programming, and the kind of unhurried outdoor atmosphere that doesn't happen very often — all without the anxiety of what it's going to cost you per kid.

What's There for Kids

The Faricy Boys Kid Zone is the heart of the family programming at MeadowGrass, and it's packed with activities that keep kids genuinely engaged rather than just managed. Giant bubbles from Milibo Art Theater, crafts from Concrete Couch, a hay wagon, kid yoga classes, and art activities are all part of the lineup.

Beyond the Kid Zone, the festival grounds offer guided nature hikes — La Foret's Black Forest setting provides real ponderosa pine forest, not a manicured park — and music workshops for families interested in exploring instruments and sound together.

The pace is unhurried. MeadowGrass doesn't have the crushing energy of large commercial music festivals. It's the kind of event where parents can actually relax, kids can roam in a contained space, and the whole family ends the weekend feeling like they've been somewhere that matters.

Music Lineup

This year's headliners: Kyle Hollingsworth Band, Nershi Hann Trio, ALO, and Shook Twins. The MeadowGrass sound typically sits at the intersection of Americana, bluegrass, and jam — accessible for adults who don't identify as hardcore music festival people, while still offering genuine quality for those who do.

Camping

Camping is available in the Ponderosa Forest on the festival grounds, which means you can make this a full Memorial Day Weekend adventure rather than a day trip. Lapp Lodge also has 15 rooms for families who prefer a roof but still want to stay on site.

The campsite-in-the-forest experience is a significant part of what makes MeadowGrass special for families with kids who haven't done much camping. The setting is beautiful, the environment is safe, and the proximity to the festival stages means you're always close to something.

What's Next

Tickets and camping passes for MeadowGrass 2026 are available at eventbrite.com and rockymountainhighway.org. Memorial Day Weekend camping availability tends to go quickly once the weather starts to feel like spring in Colorado, so if you're planning to camp, earlier is better.

La Foret is located in Black Forest, approximately 20-25 minutes from central Colorado Springs. It's an easy drive with kids, and the setting — surrounded by ponderosa pines rather than parking lots — makes the arrival part of the experience.
