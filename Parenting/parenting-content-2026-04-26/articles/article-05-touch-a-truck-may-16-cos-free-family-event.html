Touch-A-Truck Is Coming to Colorado Springs: Free Family Event May 16 at 9:30 AM

By Marta Kim | COS Parenting | April 26, 2026

If your kids have ever pressed their faces against the car window while a garbage truck, fire engine, or construction excavator rumbled by, Touch-A-Truck is exactly what it sounds like: an event where they actually get to climb up, sit inside, and explore the massive machines they've been watching from the curb.

Touch-A-Truck is coming to Colorado Springs on Saturday, May 16, 2026, starting at 9:30 AM at 1045 Lower Gold Camp Rd. Admission is free.

What to Expect

Touch-A-Truck events bring together a lineup of large vehicles from across the community — typically including fire trucks from the Colorado Springs Fire Department, construction equipment, police cars, utility trucks, public works vehicles, and more. Kids get up-close access: they can touch the vehicles, climb into the cab, honk the horn (ear protection recommended for sensitive little ears), and ask questions of the workers and volunteers on hand.

For children who are fascinated by vehicles — which describes the majority of kids in the toddler through early elementary age range — this is essentially a dream afternoon. The combination of real equipment, real workers, and genuine hands-on access tends to hold attention in a way that many paid attractions do not.

Free Admission

Free. No tickets required. No registration required. Show up at 9:30 AM at 1045 Lower Gold Camp Rd and bring the kids. That's the whole thing.

Colorado Springs has a solid calendar of free family events, and Touch-A-Truck ranks among the most popular every year. Families who've been before tend to come back — and to bring new families along with them.

Tips for Families

A few practical notes for families planning to attend:

  • Arrive closer to 9:30 AM if possible — popular events like this tend to draw larger crowds as the morning progresses
  • Bring ear protection or small earmuffs if your kids are sensitive to noise — horns and sirens can be loud at close range
  • Dress kids in comfortable, sturdy clothes they can climb in
  • Sunscreen and water if it's a warm May morning — Colorado Springs spring days can turn sunny and warm quickly

What's Next

Mark Saturday, May 16 on your calendar. Touch-A-Truck at 1045 Lower Gold Camp Rd starting at 9:30 AM — no ticket needed, no registration required. Just show up with your kids and enjoy one of Colorado Springs' most consistently popular free family mornings.
