D49 Graduation 2026: Full Schedule for Colorado Springs Families

By Sofia Reyes | COS Parenting | April 26, 2026

District 49 has released its 2026 graduation ceremony schedule, giving families the dates, times, and venues they need to plan for the big day. Whether you're a parent, grandparent, or sibling making travel arrangements, here's what you need to know — organized by ceremony day.

Friday, May 15: COS City Hub Ceremonies

Three D49 programs will hold graduation ceremonies on Friday, May 15, at the COS City Hub located at 4304 Austin Bluffs Pkwy in Colorado Springs.

  • PPEC: 8:30 AM
  • Springs Studio for Academic Excellence: 11:30 AM
  • Patriot Applied Learning Campus: 3:00 PM

The COS City Hub provides an accessible, centrally located venue for these three programs. If your family has graduates in multiple programs, note that there's a comfortable gap between ceremonies — but plan for limited parking if you're attending multiple events on the same day.

Saturday, May 23: Broadmoor World Arena Ceremonies

Three of D49's high schools will hold graduation ceremonies on Saturday, May 23, at the Broadmoor World Arena, located at 3185 Venetucci Blvd in Colorado Springs. This is a significant venue — and with three schools staggered across the day, families should plan arrival times carefully to ensure parking and seating.

  • Sand Creek High School: 9:00 AM
  • Falcon High School: 1:00 PM
  • Vista Ridge High School: 5:00 PM

If you're attending the Sand Creek ceremony, plan to arrive well before 9 AM. The Broadmoor World Arena can accommodate large crowds, but parking around large events tends to fill up earlier than families expect.

Dates Still TBD

Three programs have not yet published graduation dates: Liberty Tree Academy, Banning Lewis Preparatory Academy, and Power Technical Early Colleges. Families should monitor d49.org for updates. Graduation dates for these programs will be added to the district calendar as they're confirmed.

D49 communicates updates through the district's main website and through individual school communications. If you haven't signed up for your school's email list or parent notification system, now is a good time to do so to ensure you don't miss any updates.

Planning Tips for Graduation Day

Graduation ceremonies at the Broadmoor World Arena are popular, high-attendance events. A few practical notes for families planning ahead:

  • Arrive at least 30-45 minutes before ceremony start time for parking
  • Check the D49 website or your school's communication for ticket/guest capacity information, as this varies by school
  • Make restaurant reservations early if planning a post-ceremony celebration — Colorado Springs venues fill up fast on graduation Saturdays
  • If you're flying family in for May 23, book travel arrangements now

What's Next

The full D49 graduation schedule is available at d49.org. Families of Liberty Tree Academy, Banning Lewis Preparatory Academy, and Power Technical Early Colleges graduates should watch for date announcements through the district website and school-level communications in the coming weeks.

Congratulations to all D49 Class of 2026 graduates and their families. Colorado Springs is proud of you.
