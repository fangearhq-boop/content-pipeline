### STORY 2: CPSC Baby Product Recalls (Amazon/TikTok Shop/Shein)

**Tuyedoqe Travel Bassinets:**
- ~250 units sold on Amazon (April–October 2025, ~$40)
- Hazard: Strangulation and fall hazards; violates mandatory standard for infant sleep products
- Action: Stop use immediately
- Source: CPSC.gov (cpsc.gov/Warnings/2026/CPSC-Warns-Consumers-to-Immediately-Stop-Using-Tuyedoqe-Travel-Bassinets...)
- Confidence: HIGH (CPSC official)

**Uuoeebb Infant Walkers (sold by BaoD on Amazon):**
- Hazard: Can fit through standard doorways; fail to stop at step edges; leg openings allow children to slip through until head becomes entrapped — fall and entrapment hazards
- Action: Stop use immediately
- Source: CPSC.gov (cpsc.gov/Recalls/2026/Uuoeebb-Infant-Walkers-Recalled...)
- Confidence: HIGH (CPSC official)

**Mikario Trading Convertible Baby Gyms (TikTok Shop):**
- Description: 5-in-1 gyms converting from activity center to tummy-time mat; sold in pink
- Hazard: Suffocation hazard; violates mandatory standard for infant support cushions
- Action: Stop use immediately
- Source: CPSC.gov (cpsc.gov/Recalls/2026/Mikario-Trading-Convertible-Baby-Gyms-Recalled...)
- Confidence: HIGH (CPSC official)

**Cubimana Building Toy Sets (Vatos Toys on Shein):**
- Hazard: Button cell batteries accessible to children; swallowing causes internal chemical burns and death; violates mandatory standard for toys
- Action: Stop use; remove and dispose of batteries; contact Vatos Toys for refund
- Source: CPSC.gov (cpsc.gov/Recalls/2026/Cubimana-Building-Toy-Sets-Recalled...)
- Confidence: HIGH (CPSC official)

**Note:** YCXXKJ Bath Seats and Sam's Club pajamas were covered in previous pipeline runs (April 3 and April 4). These four recalls are fresh.
