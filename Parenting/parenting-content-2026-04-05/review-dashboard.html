Stop use immediately. 🔴 Mikario Trading Convertible Baby Gyms (sold on TikTok Shop): These 5-in-1 activity gyms that convert to tummy-time mats have been recalled for suffocation hazard. If a child swallows one, the resulting internal chemical burns can be fatal. Stop use; remove and safely dispose of the batteries; contact Vatos Toys for a refund. All four of these products carry death-risk designations from the CPSC. The full recall list with contact information is at CPSC.gov/Recalls. If you bought any of these — or if you share this with a parent friend who might have — you could be preventing a tragedy. ### Long-Form Post ``` Let's talk about this week in Colorado Springs weather — because it was a lot. We started the week with about 15 inches of snow on April 1st. Then temperatures climbed. Last night's low was close to 28°F. If you're heading to an Easter egg hunt or taking kids to the park this afternoon, morning layers matter — the temperature swings of 20–25 degrees between sunrise and midday are real. The good news? The broader outlook for Colorado Springs in April is actually positive.
