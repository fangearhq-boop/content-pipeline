D20 Families: Transportation Deadline Is June 30 — New Students Apply Starting June 8

By Jamie Rivera | June 5, 2026

Academy District 20 families who need bus service for the 2026–27 school year have a deadline of June 30, 2026. Here's what you need to know depending on your situation, plus an upcoming board meeting that public families are welcome to attend.

June 11: Public Budget Hearing

Academy District 20 will hold a public hearing on the proposed 2026–27 budget during the Board of Education meeting on Thursday, June 11, at 1110 Chapel Hills Drive, Colorado Springs. The schedule for that evening:

  • 3:30 PM: Board Study Session
  • 5:00 PM: Board of Education Meeting (includes public budget hearing)

Colorado school districts are required to adopt their budgets by June 30. This public hearing is the opportunity for community members to weigh in before the board votes. The full meeting agenda will be posted in advance on asd20.org.

District Context

Academy District 20 is currently operating under Interim Superintendent Dr. Susan Field, who has been with the district for 19 years and stepped into the role on May 19 after the board unanimously voted to part ways with Superintendent Jinger Haberer. Dr. Field previously served as assistant superintendent for learning services and as principal of Woodmen-Roberts Elementary. The district serves approximately 26,000 students across 40 schools.

D20's school leadership transition does not affect the transportation or budget timelines above — those processes are running on the district's standard calendar.

What's Next

New D20 students: mark June 8 as the date transportation requests open and June 30 as the hard deadline. Current students: if you haven't submitted yet, do so before the end of June to ensure your family is included in planning. Bus assignments will be available through the Extended Parent Portal starting August 1, 2026. Questions? Visit asd20.org/buses-and-transportation or contact the district transportation office.
