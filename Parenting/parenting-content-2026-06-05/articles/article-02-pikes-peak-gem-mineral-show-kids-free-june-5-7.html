Pikes Peak Gem, Mineral & Jewelry Show This Weekend: Kids Under 12 Free, Gold Panning and More

By Sarah Morales | June 5, 2026

If you're looking for a low-cost family outing this weekend in Colorado Springs, the Pikes Peak Gem, Mineral & Jewelry Show at Norris Penrose Event Center is running today through Sunday — and kids 12 and under get in free.

Hosted annually by the Colorado Springs Mineralogical Society, the show brings together more than 55 vendors specializing in gems, minerals, fossils, meteorites, crystals, and handcrafted jewelry. It's part shopping, part museum, part hands-on activity day for the whole family.

Kid-Friendly Activities

The show is designed with younger visitors in mind. Planned activities include:

  • Gold panning — always a hit for kids who want to walk away with something
  • Scavenger hunt through the vendor floor
  • Pebble Pups group — a dedicated space for young rock and mineral enthusiasts
  • Daily beading class at 11 AM — free to participate, all skill levels welcome
  • Wire-wrapping class, Saturday June 6 at 1 PM — a creative option for older kids and parents looking to make something together

Silent auctions run throughout the show, and door prizes are announced hourly throughout the weekend.

What to Expect

The show takes place inside the climate-controlled Norris Penrose Event Center, making it an easy indoor-outdoor activity if afternoon heat is a concern. There's genuine variety at the vendor tables — everything from rough gemstone specimens and polished crystals to fossils, meteorite slices, and artisan jewelry. Colorado and regional minerals are well-represented, which makes it a meaningful local experience beyond standard tourist shopping.

For families new to minerals and gems, vendors are generally happy to explain what they're selling and answer questions. That educational layer is part of what makes this show particularly good for curious kids.

What's Next

The Pikes Peak Gem, Mineral & Jewelry Show continues Saturday June 6 (10 AM–6 PM) and Sunday June 7 (10 AM–4 PM) at Norris Penrose Event Center, 1045 Lower Gold Camp Rd, Colorado Springs. Tickets are sold at the door only; cash is appreciated. Children 12 and under are free with a paying adult. Visit pikespeakgemshow.com for the full vendor list and schedule.
