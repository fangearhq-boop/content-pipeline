## Story Hooks and Angles

### STORY 1: CPSC Recall — VEVOR Baby Loungers (~237 Units)
- **Primary hook:** This is a mandatory standards compliance recall — the product never met the federal safety floor for infant sleep. Two distinct hazards (low sides + wide foot openings) compound the risk.
- **Angle:** Action guide — identify product, understand the specific hazard, follow the destruction + refund remedy.
- **Urgency:** HIGH — infant sleep products carry serious risk; parents need to act now.
- **Content required:** Article + 2 X posts + FB long-form + FB caption + 2 image concepts

### STORY 2: CPSC Recall — Target Gigglescape Under the Sea Popping Toy (~49,000 Units)

- **Urgency:** HIGH — choking hazard, large volume, common gift item.

### STORY 3: D11 Free Summer Meals — Free Breakfast and Lunch for Kids Ages 1-18 in COS

- **Angle:** Resource alert — how to find a site near you.
- **Urgency:** MEDIUM-HIGH — program is active now, families can use it this week.
- **Content required:** 1-2 X posts + FB long-form + FB caption + 1-2 image concepts (no article required for Tier 2, but may add short one)

### STORY 4: Pikes Peak or Bust Rodeo — July 14-18 at Norris Penrose Event Center (Plus Parade July 11)
