### STORY 1: VEVOR Baby Lounger Recall

#### Concept 1 — X Post / Facebook

**Prompt:**
A soft, neutral-toned baby nursery setting with a lounger-style infant product in the center of the frame. The lounger is generic with no brand markings — simple cream fabric with minimal stitching detail. A gentle, cautionary mood — slightly desaturated colors suggesting concern without alarm. The bottom third of the image is a clean open floor area with soft natural light. Warm but muted color palette: soft whites, warm grays, and muted sage. Flat editorial art style with gentle depth of field. No people, no text, no logos.
