**Pillar:** National Parenting (Safety)
**Tier:** 1

**Core Tension:** An infant sleep product sold on Amazon last fall is now the subject of a CPSC emergency warning — and the seller refused to recall it. That means these bassinets are still in homes, with no consumer notification from the company. Parents need to know NOW.

### STORY 4: Baby Bath Seat Recall Wave

**Pillar:** National Parenting (Safety)
**Tier:** 2

**Core Tension:** Multiple baby bath seat brands — all sold by third-party sellers on Amazon — have been recalled in early 2026 for the same dangerous failure: they tip over and trap babies in the water. Parents may have one in their bathroom right now without knowing it's been recalled.

**Story Angles:**
- Pattern alert: This isn't one product — it's a category problem. Multiple brands, same failure mode
- The "check tonight" hook: if you bought a baby bath seat on Amazon in the past year, verify the brand
- NFSVLB (model ZY2025) is specifically identified with photos on CPSC site
- Action steps: CPSC says stop using; mark "Recalled"; disassemble; cut suction cups; dispose

**Headlines:**
- A: "Check Your Baby's Bath Seat: Multiple Amazon Brands Recalled for Drowning Risk in 2026"
