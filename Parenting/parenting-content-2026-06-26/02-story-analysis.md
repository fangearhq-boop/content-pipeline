## Story 4: Hot Car Child Safety for Summer
**Tier:** 2
**Pillar:** National Parenting
**Byline:** Jamie Rivera

**Angle:** As summer heats up, the danger of leaving children in cars spikes. Six deaths already in 2026. COS families need this reminder.

**Hook:** A car parked in the Colorado sun turns dangerous in minutes. Here's what every parent needs to know — even if you'd never forget your child.

**Key Facts:**
- 6 deaths in U.S. so far in 2026; ~40/year nationally
- Interior temps can exceed 100°F in 5 minutes at 90°F outside
- Children's bodies heat 3–5x faster than adults (AAP)
- 2025+ model year vehicles required to have rear passenger alert
- Call 911 if you see a child in a hot car
