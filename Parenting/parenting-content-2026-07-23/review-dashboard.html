Safety Alert — Boon PIVOT Toddler Tower Recalled for Tip-Over Risk
NOT VERIFIEDFACEBOOK
### Long-Form Post Heads up for Colorado Springs families: if you have a Boon PIVOT Collapsible Toddler Tower in your kitchen, stop using it now. ⚠️ TOMY announced a recall on July 16 covering 116,908 of these kitchen step stools after 11 reports of the units becoming unstable, shifting, or leaning while in use. No injuries have been reported, but the tip-over and fall risk is real — and these are used by toddlers standing at counter height. The recalled towers are gray and white, roughly 22 inches wide, 23 inches deep, and 35 inches tall. They were sold at Target, Amazon, and Babylist from January 2023 through June 2026 for around $150. The good news: TOMY is handling the remedy through a free stabilizing repair part, not a return or a refund. Register your tower at recall.tomy.com/pivot and they'll send the part to you. In the meantime, stop using the tower and store it somewhere out of your kids' reach. Safety Alert — Target Cat & Jack Toddler Sandals Recalled for Choking Hazard
NOT VERIFIEDFACEBOOK
### Long-Form Post If your toddler has worn the tan Cat & Jack sandals with the little decorative pearls, it's time to check your shoe bin. 👟 Target is recalling about 211,000 pairs of Cat & Jack Toddler Girls' Sequerah Sandals after 23 reports of the plastic pearls detaching from the shoe. Detached plastic pearls are a choking hazard for young kids, and these sandals were popular — sold at Target stores and Target.com from January through May 2026 for about $20. The recall was announced July 16. The sandals are described as tan raffia with decorative plastic pearls. The remedy is straightforward: stop using the sandals, and return them to any Target store for a full refund. No need to call ahead or mail them in — just bring them back.
