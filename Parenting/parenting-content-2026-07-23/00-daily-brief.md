### STORY 2: Safety Alert — Boon PIVOT Toddler Tower Recalled for Tip-Over Risk
- **Tier:** 1
- **Pillar:** National Parenting
- **Angle:** CPSC RECALL — TOMY is recalling 116,908 Boon PIVOT Collapsible Toddler Tower Kitchen Step Stools (announced July 16, 2026) due to tip-over and fall hazards. The gray-and-white plastic and metal towers — about 22 inches wide, 23 inches deep, 35 inches tall — were sold at Target, Amazon, and Babylist from January 2023 through June 2026 for about $150. There have been 11 reports of instability, no injuries. Stop using immediately; store out of children's reach. TOMY will send a free stabilizing repair part. Register at recall.tomy.com/pivot.
- **Posting Window:** 10:30 AM MT

### STORY 3: Bronc Day Festival — Green Mountain Falls' Beloved Summer Tradition Is Saturday
- **Tier:** 2
- **Pillar:** Local Events
- **Angle:** LOCAL EVENT — The 88th annual Bronc Day Festival returns to Green Mountain Falls (20 minutes west of Colorado Springs on US-24) on Saturday July 25, 7:30 AM–3 PM. This year's theme is "Let Freedom Ring!" honoring the USA's 250th anniversary and Colorado's 150th. Free admission. Events include: pancake breakfast 7:30–10 AM at Church in the Wildwood (10585 Ute Pass Ave); parade at 10 AM on Ute Pass Ave; family activities, live music, and a pie contest at Gazebo Lake Park (100 Lake St). Grand Marshal: retired Colorado Springs Fire Chief Steve Murphy. Free and family-friendly for all ages.

### STORY 4: Safety Alert — Target Cat & Jack Toddler Sandals Recalled for Choking Hazard
- **Tier:** 2
- **Pillar:** National Parenting
- **Angle:** CPSC RECALL — Target is recalling about 211,000 pairs of Cat & Jack Toddler Girls' Sequerah Sandals (July 16, 2026) after decorative plastic pearls were found to detach from the tan raffia sandals, posing a choking hazard. Sold in Target stores and at Target.com from January through May 2026 for about $20. Target has received 23 reports of pearls coming loose. Stop using immediately; return to any Target store for a full refund.
- **Posting Window:** 3:00 PM MT
