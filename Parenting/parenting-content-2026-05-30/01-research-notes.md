## STORY 1: Walmart Mainstays Dresser Recall

### Verified Facts — HIGH confidence (2+ sources)
- Recall date: May 28, 2026 [CPSC.gov, FOX26, FOX10, FOX13, multiple Fox affiliates]
- Product: Walmart Mainstays 9-Drawer Fabric Dressers [CPSC.gov]
- Black metal frame with nine brown or black fabric drawers with handles [CPSC.gov]
- Dimensions: 40 inches long, 13.75 inches wide, 45 inches tall, weighing 32 pounds [CPSC.gov]
- Sold at Walmart stores nationwide and Walmart.com [CPSC.gov, Fox affiliates]
- Sales period: September 2023 through March 2026 [CPSC.gov]
- Price: approximately $80 [CPSC.gov]
- Violation: STURDY Act mandatory standard for clothing storage units [CPSC.gov]
- Hazard: tip-over and entrapment [CPSC.gov]
- No injuries or incidents reported [CPSC.gov]
- Remedy: full refund from Walmart [CPSC.gov, Fox affiliates]
- Action: return drawers to any Walmart store; dispose frame per local regulations [CPSC.gov]
- Units recalled: approximately 153,900 [CPSC.gov]

## STORY 3: Misco Sports Light-Up Racket Set Recall

### Verified Facts — HIGH confidence (2+ sources)
- Recall date: May 28, 2026 [CPSC.gov, multiple sources from CPSC search results]
- Seller: Missry Associates [CPSC.gov]
- Product: Misco Sports Light-Up Racket Sets [CPSC.gov]
- Hazard: screw on battery compartment does not remain attached when opened [CPSC.gov]
- Battery type: button cell batteries in shuttlecock [CPSC.gov]
- Risk: batteries easily accessible to young children; swallowing can cause chemical burns and death [CPSC.gov]
- Remedy: full refund from Missry Associates [CPSC.gov]
