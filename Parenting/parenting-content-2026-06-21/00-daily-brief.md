### STORY 1: YCXXKJ Baby Bath Seat Recall — Stop Use and Discard, Manufacturer Not Responding
**Tier:** 1
**Pillar:** National Parenting (Product Safety)
**Status:** NEW STORY
**Sources:** cpsc.gov, thehill.com, cpsc.gov (Do Not Use warning)
**Posting Window:** 8:00 AM MT
**Platforms:** X, Facebook, Article

The CPSC recalled approximately 9,000 YCXXKJ baby bath seats (Model YD-1958) in December 2025 due to a drowning hazard — the seats are unstable and can tip over with infant inside. Sold on Amazon by seller BenTalk from May 2024 through October 2025 for approximately $36. Available in blue, gray, pink, and yellow with two detachable arms, four suction cups, and three toys. CPSC has since escalated to a "Do Not Use" warning because BenTalk has stopped responding to refund requests. Parents should stop use immediately, write "recalled" on the seat, and discard it. Contact: cpsc.gov or 1-800-638-2772.

On June 18, 2026, CPSC recalled approximately 3,840 Joolz Aer2 Car Seat Adapters for Strollers due to a fall hazard — the adapters can fail to properly attach to the Joolz Aer2 stroller, allowing the car seat to fall. Return via prepaid shipping label.

### STORY 4: Cheyenne Mountain Zoo's 100th Birthday — Giraffe Center Opening This Summer
**Tier:** 2
**Pillar:** Local Events
**Status:** NEW STORY
**Sources:** cmzoo.org, gazette.com, fox21news.com, kktv.com
**Posting Window:** 3:00 PM MT
**Platforms:** X, Facebook, Article

Cheyenne Mountain Zoo is celebrating its centennial year in 2026, founded by Spencer Penrose in 1926. This summer the zoo opens the International Center for the Care and Conservation of Giraffe — a $40 million project that is the largest expansion in the zoo's history. The new facility includes a 12,000-square-foot indoor barn, an ETFE clear roof (largest of its kind over an animal exhibit in North America), 11 giraffe feeding zones (up from 3), and a 25% expansion of outdoor space. Six bronze giraffe sculptures by longtime zoo sculptor Antonia Chastain will flank the exhibit. The zoo plans a weeklong grand opening celebration. cmzoo.org.
