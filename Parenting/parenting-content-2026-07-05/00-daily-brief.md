### STORY 1: Aspen Acres Wildfire Smoke Safety — What COS Families Need to Do Now
- **Tier:** 1
- **Pillar:** Local News
- **Type:** NEW STORY
- **Byline:** Jamie Rivera
- **Posting Window:** 8:00 AM MT

Smoke from the Aspen Acres Fire — 85,580+ acres, 0% containment as of July 4 — has been reaching Colorado Springs, the I-25 corridor, and Canon City. Air quality has degraded in the region. The Colorado Department of Public Health and Environment has issued air quality warnings. ### STORY 2: Recall — Criblike Mattresses (Mengna, Amazon): ~118,000 Units, Entrapment and Suffocation Risk
- **Tier:** 1
- **Pillar:** National Parenting
- **Type:** NEW STORY
- **Byline:** Sarah Morales
- **Posting Window:** 10:00 AM MT

The CPSC has recalled approximately 118,000 Criblike brand aftermarket mattresses sold on Amazon by Mengna. The mattresses — Pack-and-Play models XCD-001 and XCD-002 (~38x26 inches) and mini-crib model MNCL-001 (~38x24 inches) — may not fit properly in certain play yards or non-full-sized cribs, leaving a deadly entrapment gap where an infant's face can press against the side and mattress, preventing breathing. The mini-crib models also fail mandatory mattress flammability standards. Sold May 2023 through September 2025 for $20–$60. Remedy: stop use immediately, cut mattress in half, photograph it, and email the photo to Criblike-service_2025@outlook.com for a full refund. Sources: cpsc.gov.

### STORY 3: Two Baby Lounger Recalls — Vevor and YGJT Products Pose Entrapment and Fall Hazards
- **Tier:** 1
- **Pillar:** National Parenting
- **Type:** NEW STORY
- **Byline:** Jamie Rivera
- **Posting Window:** 12:00 PM MT

Two separate CPSC recalls for baby loungers this week. (1) Vevor Baby Loungers: ~237 units sold at VEVOR.com, Amazon.com, and Wayfair.com from November 2024 through January 2026 for ~$30 (colors: snow deer, good night, forest friends, green). (2) YGJT Baby Loungers: ~2,500 units sold on SHEIN.com June–August 2025 for $18–$21 (white and light blue, rainbows and bears). Both loungers have sides too low to contain an infant, foot openings wider than allowed, and no stand — posing entrapment and fall hazards. Remedies: Vevor — contact 855-599-6320 or recalling@vevor.com for full refund. YGJT — destroy product (cut foam, pad, and cover in half) and email photo to YGJTrecall@outlook.com for full refund. Sources: cpsc.gov.
