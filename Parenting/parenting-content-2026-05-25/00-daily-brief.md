### STORY 3: CPSC Recall — Orb Funkee Squeeze Toys May Contain Asbestos
**Tier:** 1
**Content Pillar:** National Parenting
**Platforms:** X, Facebook, Article

The U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC) has recalled more than 121,000 Orb Funkee squeeze toys sold at Walmart and Ollie's Bargain Outlet nationwide due to possible asbestos contamination. The toys — including a large golden "monkee" (model 17451) and an assortment of small "monkees" in orange, purple, and green (model 41929) — may contain fibrous tremolite (a form of asbestos) in the sand filling. The recall was announced in May 2026. Asbestos exposure is especially dangerous when inhaled. If the toy has been damaged or the sand has leaked, the Orb Factory has specific safety disposal instructions (mask, gloves, double-bag, local disposal regulations). All families who purchased these at Walmart or Ollie's between February 2025 and April 2026 should stop use immediately and contact The Orb Factory for a refund.

**Key facts:**
- Recall: Orb Funkee squeeze toys; recalled by The Orb Factory; CPSC May 2026
- Risk: fibrous tremolite (asbestos) in sand filling; serious injury/death if inhaled
- Affected models: large golden "monkee" (model 17451); small "monkee" assortment orange/purple/green (model 41929)
- Date code: 3102491A (found on hand of large monkee or back of small monkees)
- Units recalled: more than 121,000
- Sold at: Walmart and Ollie's Bargain Outlet nationwide; February 2025–April 2026; $5–$40
- If sand has leaked: put on mask and gloves, use damp cloths, double-bag in heavy-duty plastic, dispose per local/state regulations
- Refund/contact: The Orb Factory at 800-741-0089 (Mon–Fri 8 AM–4 PM ET) or customerservice@orbtoys.com
- Full recall info: cpsc.gov/Recalls
- Sources: CPSC.gov official recall, The Hill, 9News, Fox Business, IndexBox, Newsweek
