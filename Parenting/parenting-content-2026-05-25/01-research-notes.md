## STORY 3: Orb Funkee Squeeze Toy Recall

**Source 1:** CPSC.gov official recall — "Orb Funkee Squeeze Toys Recalled Due to Risk of Serious Injury or Death from Asbestos Exposure; Imported by The Orb Factory"
**Source 2:** The Hill — "Squeeze toys sold at Walmart, Ollie's recalled over asbestos concern"
**Source 3:** 9news.com — "More than 120K toys recalled as they may contain asbestos"
**Source 4:** IndexBox — "Over 120,000 Orb Funkee Squeeze Toys Recalled Due to Asbestos in Sand Filling"
**Source 5:** Newsweek — "Children's Toys Recalled Over Death Risk, Asbestos Exposure"
**Source 6:** Fox Business — "Walmart, Ollie's sold recalled Orb squeeze toys tied to asbestos risk"

**Verified facts (HIGH confidence):**
- Recall: Orb Funkee squeeze toys; imported by The Orb Factory (CPSC.gov)
- Risk: fibrous tremolite (asbestos) in sand; serious injury/death if inhaled (CPSC.gov, multiple)
- Model 17451: large golden "monkee" (CPSC.gov, IndexBox)
- Model 41929: assortment of small "monkees" in orange, purple, green (CPSC.gov)
- Date code: 3102491A; found on hand of large monkee or back of small monkees (IndexBox, CPSC)
- Units: more than 121,000 (9news, IndexBox, multiple)
- Sold at: Walmart and Ollie's Bargain Outlet nationwide (CPSC, multiple)
- Sales period: February 2025–April 2026 (CPSC, multiple)
- Price: $5–$40 (CPSC, multiple)
- If sand leaked: mask + gloves + damp cloths + double-bag + local disposal (CPSC.gov)
- Contact: 800-741-0089, Mon–Fri 8 AM–4 PM ET; customerservice@orbtoys.com (CPSC.gov)
- Full info: cpsc.gov/Recalls (CPSC.gov)
