MeadowGrass Music Festival 2026: Last Day Today — Kids Under 12 Get In Free

By Jamie Rivera | COS Parenting | May 25, 2026

If you're looking for a Memorial Day Monday that goes a little beyond the usual cookout and parade, the 15th Annual MeadowGrass Music Festival wraps up today in Palmer Lake — about 25 miles north of Colorado Springs. It's a folk and Americana music festival with genuine family appeal, and one detail makes it particularly attractive for COS parents: kids 12 and under get in free with a paid adult.

Today is the last day of the three-day festival (May 23–25), which means whatever energy remains from the weekend is still available for families making a late decision to go.

What Makes It Worth the Drive

MeadowGrass is not a mega-festival. That's part of what makes it work for families. The Palmer Lake setting offers a smaller, more manageable crowd experience compared to a large urban music festival — kids have more room to move, lines are shorter, and the atmosphere is relaxed. The ropes course and archery range give older kids and tweens something genuinely engaging to do between music sets.

Workshops and yoga sessions run throughout the day alongside the live music, giving parents something to engage with while kids explore the activities. The late-night shows are more of an adults-only draw, but the afternoon programming is well-suited for families.

The military discount is especially relevant for Colorado Springs families, given the significant military presence at Fort Carson, Peterson Space Force Base, and Schriever Space Force Base. Check the festival's official site for discount verification requirements.

Practical Tips for Today

Palmer Lake is located north of Monument, off I-25. The drive from downtown Colorado Springs takes roughly 25 to 30 minutes. Parking at larger outdoor festivals can fill up — arriving by early afternoon is a safer bet for finding convenient parking. Check the festival's website for any updated schedule or access information before you leave.

Bring layers: Palmer Lake sits at a slightly higher elevation than Colorado Springs proper, and late May afternoons can feel warm but turn cool as the evening approaches. Even if today stays warm, having a light jacket for the kids on the return drive is a good idea.

What's Next

MeadowGrass Music Festival will return next Memorial Day weekend for its 16th annual run. For families who missed it this year or want to plan ahead for 2027, following their social channels after the festival wraps is the best way to get early ticket information. For today: check the festival's website for current hours and any last-day programming, pack the car, and make the most of the long weekend.
