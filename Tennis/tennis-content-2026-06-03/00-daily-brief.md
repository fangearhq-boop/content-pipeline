### STORY 1: Kostyuk def. Svitolina 6-3, 2-6, 6-2 — First Ukrainian Woman in Roland Garros SF
**Tier:** 1 | **Type:** Match Result (FOLLOW UP) | **Window:** 8:00 AM ET

Marta Kostyuk defeated compatriot Elina Svitolina 6-3, 2-6, 6-2 to become the first Ukrainian woman in a Roland Garros semifinal in the Open Era. The all-Ukrainian quarterfinal — the first between two Ukrainian women at any Grand Slam in the Open Era — produced a three-set battle on Day 10. Kostyuk, who stunned four-time champion Iga Swiatek 7-5, 6-1 in the fourth round, won the deciding set after five successive serve breaks. She faces No. 8 Mirra Andreeva in Thursday's women's semifinal.

### STORY 2: Mensik def. Fonseca 6-4, 6-3, 7-6(3) — Into First Major Semifinal
**Tier:** 1 | **Type:** Match Result (FOLLOW UP) | **Window:** 12:30 PM ET

Jakub Mensik converted his seventh match point to defeat Joao Fonseca 6-4, 6-3, 7-6(3) in the all-teen men's quarterfinal under the closed roof of Court Philippe-Chatrier. The 20-year-old Czech — youngest Czech in a Grand Slam QF since Ivan Lendl in 1980 — had seen six match points slip away in a dramatic return game at 5-6 in the third set before edging the tiebreak. Mensik becomes the first man born in 2004 or later to reach a Grand Slam semifinal. He faces No. 2 seed Alexander Zverev on Thursday.

### STORY 3: Chwalinska def. Kalinskaya 7-6(3), 6-3 — Qualifier Into Maiden Grand Slam SF
**Tier:** 1 | **Type:** Match Result | **Window:** 10:30 AM ET

Maja Chwalinska, a Polish qualifier, defeated No. 22 seed Anna Kalinskaya 7-6(3), 6-3 on Court Philippe-Chatrier to reach her first Grand Slam semifinal. The 24-year-old has won eight consecutive matches — three in qualifying, five in the main draw — dropping only one set in the entire tournament. Chwalinska becomes just the second women's qualifier to reach a Grand Slam semifinal in the Open Era, matching Nadia Podoroska's run at Roland Garros 2020. She faces the winner of Sabalenka vs Shnaider in Thursday's semifinal.
