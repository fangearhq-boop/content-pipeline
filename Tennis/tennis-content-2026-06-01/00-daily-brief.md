### STORY 1: Fonseca def. Ruud 7-5, 7-6(8), 5-7, 6-2 — First Brazilian RG QF since Kuerten
**Tier:** 1 | **Type:** Match Result | **Window:** 8:00 AM ET

João Fonseca (19, Brazil) reached his first Grand Slam quarterfinal, defeating two-time RG finalist Casper Ruud 7-5, 7-6(8), 5-7, 6-2 in 3h 58m on Day 8 (May 31). He is the first Brazilian man in the Roland Garros quarterfinals since Gustavo Kuerten in 2004. Fonseca, who stunned Novak Djokovic in R3, joins Andre Agassi and Carlos Alcaraz in the select company of Roland Garros QF berths at age 19. He faces Jakub Mensik (Czech, 20) in the quarterfinal on June 2 (night session, Philippe-Chatrier).

**Tier:** 1 | **Type:** Match Feature | **Window:** 3:00 PM ET
