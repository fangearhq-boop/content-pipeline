Marta Kostyuk (No. 15) shocked four-time Roland Garros champion Iga Swiatek (No. 3) 7-5, 6-1 in the fourth round on Sunday, May 31 — ending Swiatek's title defence on her 25th birthday. It was the first time Kostyuk had ever beaten Swiatek in four meetings, and the first set she had ever taken from the Pole across all their previous encounters. Swiatek's exit is her earliest at Roland Garros since 2019. Kostyuk extended her unbeaten clay record in 2026 to 16 matches — winning titles in Rouen and Madrid — and becomes only the second woman since Justine Henin in 2005 (since WTA rankings began) to reach 16 clay wins without a loss from the start of a season.
