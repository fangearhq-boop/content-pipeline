### STORY 1: CORRECTION + Tsitsipas Wins Gstaad — 13th Title, 16-Month Drought Over
- **Tier:** 1
- **Angle:** CORRECTION — Yesterday's pipeline (July 20) misidentified Raphael Collignon as the Gstaad champion in the "Clay Swing Week One Wrap" story. The actual result: Stefanos Tsitsipas defeated Collignon 6-4, 6-7(3), 6-3 in the final on Saturday July 19 to win the Swiss Open Gstaad (EFG Swiss Open). This was Tsitsipas's 13th career ATP title, ending a 16-month drought since his last title (Dubai, March 2025). He is the first Greek player to win the Gstaad title.
