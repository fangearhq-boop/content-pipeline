WTA Hamburg QF: Sherif Beats Badosa Again; Oliynykova and Bondar Also Advance

By Elena Voss | July 24, 2026 | Tennis Fanrecap

Five days after winning the Iasi title when Paula Badosa retired, Mayar Sherif met the Spanish player again on the clay in Hamburg — and this time produced a straight-sets win.

Sherif defeated Badosa 6-2, 6-3 in the MSC Hamburg Ladies Open quarterfinal on Friday, advancing to a Saturday semifinal against Anhelina Kalinina. The Egyptian has now beaten Badosa twice in five days on clay: first at Iasi on July 20 (Badosa retired trailing 6-4, 4-0), and now in a direct contest in Hamburg. Sherif beat Jacquemot 6-2, 6-3 in Thursday's second round to set up the rematch. Badosa had arrived in Hamburg after claiming a 6-4, 6-1 win over No. 3 seed Petra Martin Udvardy in her second-round match.

Oliynykova Through, Avanesyan Run Ends

No. 1 seed Oleksandra Oliynykova had the toughest quarterfinal on paper, facing Elina Avanesyan — an unseeded Armenian who had beaten No. 7 seed Anastasia Putintseva 6-3, 2-6, 6-2 in the second round. Avanesyan made the first set competitive before Oliynykova found her level and took the match 6-3, 2-6, 6-2. The top seed is into the semifinals against Anna Bondar.

Bondar of Hungary defeated Alina Charaeva 3-6, 7-5, 6-1 in a match that required recovery from a slow start. Bondar dropped the opening set, found her footing in a tight second set, and then closed out the third convincingly. She faces Oliynykova on Saturday.

The fourth quarterfinal saw Anhelina Kalinina of Ukraine defeat home-country favourite Tamara Korpatsch 6-1, 6-3. Korpatsch had German support throughout the week but could not convert against Kalinina's level in the quarterfinals.

Saturday Semifinals

The Hamburg semifinal pairings are: Sherif vs. Kalinina, and Bondar vs. Oliynykova. Sherif enters as arguably the player in form — two wins over Badosa in five days is a compelling resume — while Oliynykova is the top seed who has navigated her half of the draw without a default.

The final is scheduled for Sunday July 26 at the MSC Hamburg Ladies Open.

What's Next

The two semifinals take place Saturday in Hamburg. Tennis Fanrecap will follow the results as the WTA Hamburg Open heads toward its Sunday final. Sherif, Kalinina, Bondar, and Oliynykova are the final four.
