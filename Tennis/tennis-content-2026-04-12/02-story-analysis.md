### STORY 2: Sinner Joins Federer/Nadal/Djokovic — Third Consecutive M1000 Final

**Tier:** 1
**Status:** NEW STORY

**Why This Matters:**
Reaching the first three Masters 1000 finals of the same season is a feat only three players had accomplished before Sinner: Federer (2006), Nadal (2011), Djokovic (2015). All three are Grand Slam champions and all-time legends. Sinner joining that list is not a stat — it is a statement about what he is becoming. Additionally, his 21-match M1000 win streak and his methodical dismantling of Zverev (6-1, 6-4) show zero signs of regression from his recent form.

**Key Angles:**
1. The four-man club: Federer, Nadal, Djokovic, Sinner
2. SF performance: 80% (12/15) of Zverev first-serve points won in Set 1
3. 21 consecutive M1000 wins — "will this ever stop?"
4. First career Monte Carlo final

**Best Social Hook:** "Only Roger Federer, Rafael Nadal, and Novak Djokovic had reached the first three Masters 1000 finals in the same season. Now add Jannik Sinner."

**Target Audience:** Tennis history fans, analytics-minded readers, Sinner fans.

**Best Social Hook:** "Anastasia Potapova is an Austrian citizen playing the Linz final. Upper Austria has never had a home finalist before. That changes today."
