### STORY 2: Kyrgios Wins Stuttgart Return — Defeats Moutet 6-3, 6-4
- **Tier:** 1
- **Type:** FOLLOW-UP (Stuttgart Day 1 preview covered June 9; today is the match result)
- **Angle:** Nick Kyrgios defeated No. 8 seed Corentin Moutet 6-3, 6-4 in 69 minutes in the first round of the BOSS Open in Stuttgart. Kyrgios fired 8 aces. Fritz (defending champion) and Shelton (top seed) entered the second round.
