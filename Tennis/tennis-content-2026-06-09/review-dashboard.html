Queen's Club WTA — Zheng Upset by Cristian, Dart Stuns Samsonova
NOT VERIFIEDBRIEF
Queen's Club WTA — Zheng Upset by Cristian, Dart Stuns Samsonova - **Tier:** 1 - **Angle:** The HSBC Championships WTA 500 at Queen's Club opened with two big upsets on Day 1 (June 8). Jaqueline Cristian defeated No. 5 seed Qinwen Zheng 6-4, 7-6(4) in 94 minutes — only Cristian's second career tour-level win on grass. Harriet Dart, the British wildcard, beat world No. 35 Liudmila Samsonova 5-7, 6-4, 6-3 in 2h48m — her best win by ranking since defeating Katie Boulter at Wimbledon 2024. Katie Boulter's match against No. 8 seed Leylah Fernandez was halted at 3-6, 3-3 by rain and carries over to Day 2 (June 9). Emma Raducanu and wildcard Mika Stojsavljevic (17-year-old Brit) vs Marta Kostyuk are both on the Day 2 schedule.
