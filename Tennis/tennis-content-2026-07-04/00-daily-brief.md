## Today's Top Stories

### STORY 1: Djokovic Equals Federer's All-Time Wimbledon Win Record at 105
**Tier:** 1 (Breaking/Trending)
**Pillar:** Match Results / Milestone
Novak Djokovic defeated Arthur Rinderknech 7-5, 6-4, 1-6, 7-6(4) on Day 5 to record his 105th career match win at Wimbledon — equaling Roger Federer's all-time men's record. Only Martina Navratilova (120) has more wins at the All England Club. Djokovic faces Roman Safiullin (world No. 132, wild card) in the Round of 16 on Sunday; Djokovic leads their H2H 3-0.

### STORY 2: Struff Stuns No. 8 Medvedev in Three-Set Masterclass
**Tier:** 1 (Breaking/Trending)
**Pillar:** Match Results / Upset
Jan-Lennard Struff (world No. 74) eliminated eighth seed Daniil Medvedev 7-6(4), 7-6(5), 7-5 in one of the biggest upsets of Wimbledon 2026. Medvedev led by a break in all three sets and held a commanding 5-2 lead in the third, but Struff won all three tiebreaks to prevail. Medvedev converted just 4 of 12 break-point chances. Post-match, Medvedev said: "I should do better. I am really disappointed." It is Struff's first-ever Wimbledon Round of 16.

### STORY 3: Women's Day 5 Sweep — Sabalenka, Osaka and Gauff All Advance
**Tier:** 2 (Today's News)
**Pillar:** Match Results
All three top American/global women advanced on Day 5. No. 1 Aryna Sabalenka defeated Jelena Ostapenko 6-4, 6-4 (9 aces, 71% first-serve win rate) and will face Naomi Osaka in a marquee Round of 16 matchup on Sunday. Osaka eliminated Daria Kasatkina 6-1, 6-3 to reach the Wimbledon Round of 16 for the first time in her career. No. 7 Coco Gauff battled past Claire Liu 6-3, 6-7(5), 6-2.

### STORY 4: Day 6 Spotlight — Swiatek vs. Eala Headline Saturday's Action
**Tier:** 2 (Today's News / Upcoming)
**Pillar:** Match Preview / Match Results
Saturday at Wimbledon centres on Centre Court's marquee third-round clash: No. 3 Iga Swiatek vs. Alexandra Eala — the first grass-court meeting between the two players who split their H2H at 1-1 (Eala won at Miami 2025; Swiatek won at Madrid 2025). Also on the Day 6 card: No. 2 Elena Rybakina vs. Elise Mertens on Court 1; No. 2 Alexander Zverev vs. Marcos Giron; Frances Tiafoe vs. No. 10 Alexander Bublik; and Amanda Anisimova vs. No. 14 Madison Keys.

### STORY 5: Day 7 Preview — Round of 16 Begins with Djokovic, Sinner, Sabalenka
**Tier:** 2 (Today's News / Upcoming)
**Pillar:** Match Preview
Sunday brings the Round of 16 and three unmissable matches. Djokovic, going for his record-breaking 106th Wimbledon win, faces Roman Safiullin (who upset No. 24 seed Fonseca). Defending champion Sinner meets Japan's Shintaro Mochizuki. The women's marquee is Sabalenka vs. Osaka — world No. 1 against the player making her first-ever Wimbledon R4 appearance. Alcaraz, who would have been a leading contender, is absent due to a wrist injury.
