# Tennis Fanrecap — Research Playbook

How to research, tier, and fact-check tennis stories for daily content.

## Research Categories

Run **8-10 web searches** across these categories before writing content.

| # | Category | Example Search Queries |
|---|----------|----------------------|
| 1 | Match Results | "ATP results today", "WTA results today", "[tournament] scores", "[player] match score" |
| 2 | Rankings & Seedings | "ATP rankings today", "WTA rankings today", "ATP race to Turin standings", "WTA race standings" |
| 3 | Tournament Draws & Scheduling | "[tournament] draw", "[tournament] order of play tomorrow", "rain delay [tournament]", "[tournament] schedule" |
| 4 | Player News | "[player] injury update", "tennis withdrawal today", "[player] coaching change", "tennis comeback 2026" |
| 5 | Grand Slam Buildup | "[Grand Slam] seedings projection", "[player] [surface] form", "[player] vs [player] head to head", "[Grand Slam] qualifying results" |
| 6 | Upsets & Breakout Performances | "tennis upset today", "qualifier wins [tournament]", "teen tennis breakthrough", "biggest upset ATP WTA" |
| 7 | Records & Milestones | "[player] career titles", "Grand Slam titles record", "[player] ranking milestone", "tennis records 2026" |
| 8 | Preview & Lookahead | "[tournament] next round matchups", "tennis schedule this week", "surface transition preview", "ATP WTA upcoming tournaments" |

## Story Tiering

### Tier 1 — Lead Stories (top time slots)

Must-cover content that anchors the day. Gets the 8:00 AM or 10:30 AM slot.

| Story Type | Why Tier 1 |
|-----------|-----------|
| Grand Slam final or semifinal results | The biggest matches in tennis — always the lead story |
| Major upsets (top-10 player loses early) | Top seeds falling in early rounds are the most shareable tennis stories |
| Career milestones | Record-breaking Grand Slam titles, career #1 ranking, 1000th match win |
| Five-set / three-set thrillers | Epic matches with dramatic finishes define the sport |
| Major injury to top player | Star withdrawals reshape a tournament and the ranking race |
| Grand Slam title clinched | Championship moments are always Tier 1 |

### Tier 2 — Developing Stories (mid-day slots)

Strong content that fills the 12:30 PM or 3:00 PM slots.

| Story Type | Why Tier 2 |
|-----------|-----------|
| Early Grand Slam round results | Day 1-5 results with notable wins, upsets, or retirements |
| Masters 1000 / WTA 1000 results | The biggest non-Slam events — always relevant |
| Ranking changes | New #1, breaking into top 10, race to Finals implications |
| Injury news and withdrawals | Player pulls out of upcoming tournament, returning from injury |
| Coaching changes | New coach hire, partnership split |
| Seedings and draw analysis | Post-draw reaction, tough draws, potential blockbuster matchups |

### Tier 3 — Supporting Content (later slots)

Fills out the schedule. Gets the 6:00 PM or 9:00 PM slot.

| Story Type | Why Tier 3 |
|-----------|-----------|
| Previews for next round / next day | Matchup analysis, head-to-head stats, surface form |
| Historical comparisons | "Best seasons on clay" type features, era comparisons |
| Qualifying round results | Qualifier stories, lucky losers, next-gen spotlights |
| Surface transition previews | Looking ahead to the next surface swing |
| GOAT debate analysis | Evergreen content with fresh data points |
| Stat deep dives | Serve stats, return game analysis, surface-specific performance |

## Posting Priority

This order is non-negotiable:

1. **Match results (especially overnight)** — always first. Tennis is global; results from European, Asian, and Australian tournaments land overnight ET. A match result posted after the next round starts is useless.
2. **Today's order of play** — what's happening on court today, marquee matchups, time slots.
3. **Breaking news** — withdrawals, injuries, coaching changes, scheduling announcements.
4. **Everything else** — analysis, historical comparisons, previews for future rounds.

If multiple match results exist, order by tier: Grand Slam finals/semis first, then major upsets, then top-10 wins, then other notable results.

## Fact-Check Requirements

Every post must be verified before publishing. Priority order:

### Priority 1 — Scores and Match Results

| Check | How to Verify |
|-------|--------------|
| Final score (set-by-set) | Cross-reference official tournament site AND Flashscore |
| Match duration | Verify from official tournament site or Flashscore |
| Winner stated first | Score format: "Djokovic d. Alcaraz 6-4, 3-6, 7-5" — winner always first |
| Tiebreak scores | Verify exact tiebreak score — never omit or estimate |
| Retirement / walkover | Confirm the correct term: retirement (mid-match) vs walkover (pre-match) |

### Priority 2 — Rankings and Draw Results

| Check | How to Verify |
|-------|--------------|
| Current ATP/WTA ranking | Verify against atptour.com or wtatennis.com — rankings update weekly (Monday) |
| Live ranking projections | Use live-tennis.eu for mid-tournament projected rankings |
| Race to Finals standings | ATP/WTA official race standings — separate from the rankings |
| Seeding numbers | Confirm from official tournament draw — seedings can differ from rankings |
| Head-to-head records | TennisAbstract or ATP/WTA player profile pages |

### Priority 3 — Player Stats and Career Records

| Check | How to Verify |
|-------|--------------|
| Career titles count | ATP/WTA player profile — the gold standard |
| Grand Slam titles | Cross-reference ATP/WTA and Wikipedia (for historical players) |
| Season stats (ace%, 1st serve%) | ATP/WTA official stats or TennisAbstract |
| Player ages | Look up birth date from ATP/WTA player profile, then calculate — never trust a secondary source |
| Career records / milestones | TennisAbstract for deep historical data |
| Surface-specific records | TennisAbstract — specify the surface and time period |

### Priority 4 — Schedule and Logistics

| Check | How to Verify |
|-------|--------------|
| Match times | Official tournament order of play — convert to ET |
| Day of week | Cross-reference the calendar — "Saturday's semifinal" must actually be Saturday |
| Venue / court assignment | Official tournament order of play |
| Local time + ET | Always provide ET; add local time for non-US tournaments |
| Tournament dates | Official tournament site — dates shift year to year |

## Coverage Bias Check

Before finalizing any day's content, verify coverage balance:

- Cover both ATP and WTA with equal weight — never let a full day go by with only men's results
- Do not default to the Big 3/Big 4 era nostalgia — cover the current generation with equal depth
- Non-Grand Slam tournaments matter — a WTA 1000 final deserves coverage, not just Slams
- American players are not the only story — tennis is global, cover all nationalities
- If 4+ posts feature the same player, look harder for other stories
- Qualifying rounds and lower-ranked tournaments can produce great stories — don't ignore them

## Key Dates Reminder

- **Australian Open**: Mid-January to late January
- **Indian Wells + Miami**: March (Sunshine Double)
- **Clay Season**: April through early June (Monte Carlo, Madrid, Rome, Roland Garros)
- **Grass Season**: Mid-June through mid-July (Queen's/Halle, Wimbledon)
- **US Hard Court Swing**: Late July through mid-September (Canada, Cincinnati, US Open)
- **Asian Swing**: Late September through October
- **Indoor Season + Finals**: October through November (ATP Finals, WTA Finals)
- **Davis Cup / BJK Cup Finals**: November
- **Off-season**: December (Next Gen Finals, exhibitions)
