# Tennis Fanrecap — Brand Guide

## Colors

| Role | Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|------|-----|-------|
| Primary | Court Green | #2E7D32 | Headers, primary buttons, brand identity |
| Secondary | Championship Purple | #6A1B9A | Accents, secondary CTAs, highlight elements |
| Accent | Tennis Ball Yellow | #CDDC39 | Call-to-action highlights, stat callouts, emphasis |
| Dark | Midnight | #1A1A2E | Backgrounds, body text, dark mode base |
| Light | White | #FFFFFF | Background, card surfaces, text on dark |

## Usage Notes

- **Primary (Court Green)** anchors the brand — use for logos, headers, and primary UI elements
- **Secondary (Championship Purple)** adds depth — use for hover states, secondary buttons, and accent borders
- **Accent (Tennis Ball Yellow)** draws the eye — use sparingly for stat highlights, breaking news badges, and CTAs
- **Dark (Midnight)** for text and dark backgrounds — never use Court Green for body text (accessibility)
- Maintain sufficient contrast ratios (WCAG AA minimum) when pairing colors
- Tennis Ball Yellow on White fails contrast — always pair Yellow with Midnight or Court Green backgrounds
