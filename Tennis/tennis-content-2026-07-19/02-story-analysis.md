### STORY 1: Raphael Collignon Wins First ATP Title at Gstaad

**Freshness tags:** first-atp-title, Belgian-breakthrough, Gstaad-2026

**Summary:** Raphael Collignon of Belgium defeated No. 3 seed Stefanos Tsitsipas 7-6(2), 7-6(5) in the EFG Swiss Open Gstaad final to win his first ATP Tour title. Collignon had to survive a match point against Juan Manuel Cerundolo in the semifinals — coming back from a set and 3-5 down — to even reach Sunday's title match. Tsitsipas was playing in his first ATP final in 17 months. Two tiebreaks, no margin for error, and Collignon converted.

**Relevance:** First-title stories are the emotional core of tennis fandom — they define moments and careers.
