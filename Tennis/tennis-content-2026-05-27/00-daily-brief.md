### STORY 1: Medvedev Stunned by Australian Wildcard Walton — Biggest Upset of RG 2026
**Tier:** 1 | **Platforms:** X, FB, Article | **Byline:** Marcus Cole

Adam Walton (World No. 97, Australian wildcard) defeated No. 6 seed Daniil Medvedev 6-2, 1-6, 6-1, 1-6, 6-4 in 3 hours 22 minutes on Court Suzanne-Lenglen — the biggest upset of Roland Garros 2026. It was Walton's first career win over a top-10 player. The match also generated a viral moment when Medvedev argued with his wife Daria in his box, with her telling him "Everybody's suffering — you need to behave." After the loss, Medvedev said: "I know I'm in good shape and I know I can play well at Roland Garros." Walton said a win over Medvedev in Cincinnati last year gave him the confidence to repeat the feat.

### STORY 2: Sinner Extends Win Streak to 30, Career Grand Slam Quest Rolls On
**Tier:** 1 | **Platforms:** X, FB, Article | **Byline:** Ryan Calloway

Jannik Sinner defeated French wildcard Clément Tabur 6-1, 6-3, 6-4 in a night session on Court Philippe-Chatrier, extending his win streak to 30 consecutive matches — the fifth-longest streak in ATP Tour era (since 1990). Sinner hit 40 winners and did not face a single break point in 2 hours 8 minutes. The victory keeps his Career Grand Slam pursuit alive: Roland Garros is the only major he has yet to win. His next opponent is Francisco Cerundolo.

**Sources:** Roland Garros official, ATP Tour, Tennis Majors, Puntodebreak.com, Olympics.com

### STORY 5: Osaka's Eiffel Tower Moment — The Story Behind the Gold Dress
**Tier:** 2 | **Platforms:** X, FB, Article | **Byline:** Ryan Calloway
