     These are the "always true" heuristics for your domain.

     SPORTS EXAMPLES:
     - Ranked matchups always tier up — any game between top-25 teams is minimum Tier 2
     - Upsets are always Tier 1 — unranked beating top-15 is automatic lead
     - Milestones tier up — career records, perfect games always Tier 1-2
     - Regional bias awareness — cover all conferences, not just the big ones

     PARENTING EXAMPLES:
     - Product recalls are always Tier 1 — safety is non-negotiable
     - Back-to-school content tiers up July-August (even if the "news" isn't breaking)
     - Free events tier up — free is king for family content
     - Weather-dependent events need same-day coverage (cancellations, rescheduling)

     GARDENING EXAMPLES:
     - Frost date warnings are always Tier 1 during spring planting season
     - Pest/disease outbreaks tier up when they're actively spreading in your zone
