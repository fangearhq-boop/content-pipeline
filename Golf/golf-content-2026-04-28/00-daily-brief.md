### STORY 1: Korda Now 5 Points From LPGA Hall of Fame (TIER 1)
Following her Chevron Championship victory, the LPGA officially confirmed Nelly Korda is now five points away from induction into the LPGA Hall of Fame (22 of 27 required). The Chevron win was worth 2 HOF points (major victory). She needs 5 more — achievable via two more majors OR one major and three regular wins. She also became just the 4th American woman since 1990 to win three or more majors (joining Meg Mallon, Betsy King, and Patty Sheehan). Her next start: Riviera Maya Open at Mayakoba, April 30-May 3.
