Dow LPGA Championship: Somi Lee and Jin Hee Im Win at 20-Under With Dominant Final Round

By Jake Torres | Golf Fanrecap

June 14, 2026

Somi Lee and Jin Hee Im did not leave room for drama. They simply played the best golf of the week when it mattered most and walked off Midland Country Club as champions of the 2026 Dow LPGA Championship.

The pair posted a final round of 62 — completely bogey-free with eight birdies — to reach 20 under par (260 total) for the tournament. Their final-round performance was a statement round, the kind that closes out contenders and removes all doubt before the back nine conversation ever begins.

The Format That Rewards Partnership

The Dow Championship uses a best-ball format, pairing 72 two-person teams to compete over 72 teams. In best-ball play, the premium is on consistent ball-striking, aggressive birdie-hunting, and the ability of partners to carry each other through difficult stretches. When both players are on — as Lee and Im clearly were on Sunday — the format can produce scores that would be impossible for an individual to sustain.

Their 62 was a product of that partnership firing on all cylinders. Eight birdies, no bogeys, no wasted holes. Over 18 holes at Midland Country Club, that is elite performance by any measure.

The Tournament

The 2026 Dow LPGA Championship ran June 8-14 at Midland Country Club in Midland, Michigan, with a purse of $3.3 million. The event is a longstanding fixture on the LPGA Tour calendar, supported by the Dow Chemical Company and consistently one of the best-attended team events in women's professional golf.

The field going into the final round had been tight, with multiple teams within range of the leaders. Lee and Im's bogey-free 62 removed any possibility of a final-round drama by making the mathematics impossible for the field to overcome.

What's Next

The LPGA Tour continues with events through the summer. For Lee and Im, the 2026 Dow title represents a significant result that will strengthen their competitive positioning for the remainder of the season. Congratulations to both champions on a commanding week.
