### STORY 1: Rory McIlroy Wins 2026 Masters — Back-to-Back, Joins Tiger in History Books
**Tier:** 1 | **Priority:** Breaking/Trending
**Status:** NEW STORY

Rory McIlroy won his second consecutive Masters Tournament at Augusta National, finishing 12-under (276) — one shot ahead of Scottie Scheffler (-11). Cameron Young, Russell Henley, Justin Rose, and Tyrrell Hatton tied at -10. McIlroy became only the fourth player in Masters history to defend the green jacket, and the first since Tiger Woods won back-to-back in 2001 and 2002. The final round was dramatic: McIlroy, who entered with a commanding six-shot lead after 36 holes, saw Cameron Young wrestle the lead away during the back nine, and Rose briefly led before McIlroy recovered on the second nine. He posted a final-round 71 despite a bogey on 18, winning by one.

**Key Facts:**
- McIlroy: -12 (276), 1 shot clear of Scheffler
- McIlroy bogeys 18th, still wins by one
- First to defend Masters since Tiger Woods 2001-02
- Only fourth player ever to win back-to-back Masters (joins Nicklaus, Faldo, Woods)
- McIlroy now has two green jackets and six major championships total
- Had six-shot lead after 36 holes — nearly lost it before recovering
- Cameron Young led briefly; Justin Rose also led briefly on final day
- Scheffler (-11), Young/Henley/Rose/Hatton tied at -10

### STORY 2: Fitzpatrick Stuns Scheffler in Playoff to Win RBC Heritage — Second Win in Three Starts
**Tier:** 1 | **Priority:** Breaking/Trending
**Status:** NEW STORY

Matt Fitzpatrick defeated world No. 1 Scottie Scheffler in a sudden-death playoff to win the 2026 RBC Heritage at Harbour Town Golf Links on April 19. Fitzpatrick hit a 4-iron from 204 yards to 13 feet, then drained the birdie putt to claim his second RBC Heritage title and his second win in his last three PGA Tour starts. Scheffler had erased a 3-shot deficit in the final four holes of regulation to force the playoff. Fitzpatrick won the $3.6 million winner's share from the $20 million signature event purse and climbed to No. 3 in the world rankings. Fitzpatrick's other 2026 win: the Valspar Championship in March, plus a runner-up at The Players Championship.
