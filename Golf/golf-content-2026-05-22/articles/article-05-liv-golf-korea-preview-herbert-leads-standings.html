LIV Golf Heads to Korea: Herbert Leads Standings After Wire-to-Wire Virginia Victory

By Jake Torres | Golf Fanrecap | May 22, 2026

Lucas Herbert won the LIV Golf Virginia event at Trump National Golf Club on May 10 in as complete a fashion as the 2026 season has produced. Wire-to-wire. Four shots clear. A final-round 69 to hold off Sergio Garcia and finish at 24-under par.

It was the first LIV Golf individual trophy of his career. Herbert leads the 2026 individual standings with 99.18 points. And now the tour moves to Asia for the first time this season.

LIV Golf Korea

LIV Golf Korea runs May 28-31 at Asiad Country Club in Busan, South Korea. The full 57-player field competes over 72 holes with no cut — every player in the draw will complete all four rounds. Ripper GC leads the team standings coming in; 4Aces GC, which won the team title at Virginia in a playoff, sits second.

Busan is a port city with deep cultural identity and strong commercial golf infrastructure. The Korean market has long been one of the most engaged in the sport globally, and LIV Golf's return to Asia generates attention throughout the Asia-Pacific region. Korea itself has produced some of the most decorated professionals in the history of both the LPGA and PGA Tours, making this stop significant beyond its competitive implications.

The Competitive Picture

Herbert's Virginia performance was remarkable in the context of a 72-hole event. Leading wire-to-wire in a field of 57 elite professionals, including multiple major champions and former world No. 1 players, requires consistent execution over four full days. He did it without a wobble.

The individual standings with Herbert at the front will be closely contested in Korea. Multiple players are within range to close the gap with a strong performance at Asiad Country Club. The team competition — with Ripper GC and 4Aces GC separated by a narrow margin — adds another layer of strategy to the event's framework.

What's Next

LIV Golf Korea begins Thursday, May 28 at Asiad Country Club in Busan. It is the fifth event of the 2026 LIV Golf season. LIV Golf Korea: May 28-31, 2026, Asiad Country Club, Busan, South Korea. Individual leader: Lucas Herbert (99.18 pts). Team leader: Ripper GC.
