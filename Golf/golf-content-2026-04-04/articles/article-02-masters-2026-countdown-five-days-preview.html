Five Days to Augusta: Your Complete 2026 Masters Preview

By Jake Torres | Golf Fanrecap | April 4, 2026

In five days, golf's most anticipated week of the year begins at Augusta National Golf Club. The 90th Masters Tournament — the first major of the 2026 season — opens with Thursday's first round on April 9, and the field, the stakes, and the storylines are in place. Here's everything you need to know before the azaleas bloom and the competition begins.

The Favorites

Scottie Scheffler enters as the +550 favorite, and the case for him is straightforward. The world No. 1 won the Masters in 2022. He won it again in 2024. A third Green Jacket in five years would place him in genuinely rare historical company at Augusta National — only a handful of champions have won more than twice in modern professional golf's era. Scheffler withdrew from the Houston Open to be present for his wife's second child's birth, choosing family over tournament preparation in a decision that reflects exactly the grounded perspective that makes him difficult to rattle under major championship pressure.

Rory McIlroy returns as defending champion at +1100. His 2025 Masters victory completed the career Grand Slam — making him only the sixth player to win all four major championships. Seeking a back-to-back at Augusta would make him the first since Tiger Woods accomplished the feat in 2001-02. McIlroy's ball-striking heading into the tournament ranks among the best in the field; his Augusta track record prior to 2025 was the source of years of conversation, and now he arrives without the weight of the monkey he finally knocked off last spring.

The Intriguing Threats

Bryson DeChambeau carries +1000 odds and something more valuable: back-to-back LIV Golf victories and the confidence that comes with winning in pressure situations. He was the Masters runner-up in 2021 and knows exactly what Augusta demands. His power game creates genuine two-shot opportunities on the par-5s that most of the field can't replicate.

Tommy Fleetwood, the world No. 1 in rankings entering the week, is an intriguing contender at +1400. Other threats include the field of Grand Slam chasers — players who have won every other major and need only Augusta to complete the set.

The Tournament Details

The 90th Masters carries a $21 million purse — up from $20 million in 2025 — with the winner's share set at a record level. Amazon Prime debuts as a broadcast partner for the tournament, a significant shift in the Masters' traditionally conservative approach to distribution rights. CBS and ESPN continue in their traditional coverage roles, with Amazon adding streaming-exclusive content.

The Par 3 Contest takes place Wednesday April 8 — the one day at Augusta where the atmosphere lightens, families join players on the course, and the nine-hole par-3 course produces the competition's most memorable non-competitive moments. Twenty-two players are making their Masters debuts this year, bringing fresh faces into one of sport's most storied settings.

Tiger Woods is not in the field. Phil Mickelson is not competing. The two most iconic figures of the past quarter-century won't be present — but the tournament's competitive depth remains extraordinary. Eleven LIV Golf players have earned invitations through various exemption categories and world rankings.

What's Next

Sunday's Valero Texas Open final round will determine who receives the last Masters invitation. The Par 3 Contest is Wednesday April 8. Round 1 tees off Thursday April 9, with featured group coverage across multiple platforms. The 2026 Masters champion will be crowned Sunday April 12 with a green jacket ceremony at Augusta National.
