# Golf Fanrecap — Seasonal Calendar (2026)

Golf is a nearly year-round sport. Content volume peaks around the four majors and dips only slightly in December.

## Monthly Breakdown

### January
- Tournament of Champions (Kapalua)
- PGA Tour West Coast Swing begins
- LIV Golf season starts
- Content focus: New season storylines, offseason moves recap, early form watch

### February
- WM Phoenix Open (TPC Scottsdale — biggest crowds in golf)
- Genesis Invitational (Riviera CC, LA)
- Content focus: Signature events ramp up, early FedEx Cup race, LIV early results

### March
- **The Players Championship** (TPC Sawgrass — "the fifth major")
- WGC events
- Content focus: Major season buildup begins, Players Championship deep coverage
- **Currently March 2026 = Players Championship / early PGA Tour signature events season**

### April
- **THE MASTERS** (Augusta National) — Tier 1 everything, all week
- Content focus: Masters preview (course guide, past champions, Par 3 contest), daily round coverage, Sunday drama, green jacket ceremony
- This is the biggest content week of the golf year

### May
- **PGA Championship** (2nd major)
- Content focus: Major championship week coverage, course preview, leaderboard tracking

### June
- **US Open** (3rd major)
- LPGA majors (US Women's Open, KPMG Women's PGA Championship)
- Content focus: USGA course setup debate, major coverage, LPGA major spotlight

### July
- **The Open Championship** (4th major, UK links course)
- LPGA events continue
- Content focus: Links golf, weather as a factor, international coverage, The Open history

### August
- FedEx Cup Playoffs begin (3 events)
- Olympics golf (every 4 years — next: 2028 LA)
- Content focus: Playoff race, FedEx Cup standings, elimination drama

### September
- FedEx Cup Final (Tour Championship, East Lake)
- **Ryder Cup / Presidents Cup** (alternating years)
- Content focus: FedEx Cup champion, Ryder Cup/Presidents Cup team drama and match play

### October
- LIV Golf season finale
- DP World Tour fall events
- PGA Tour fall series (wrap-around season)
- Content focus: LIV team championship, DP World Tour race, early next-season positioning

### November
- RSM Classic (PGA Tour fall)
- DP World Tour Championship (Dubai — Race to Dubai finale)
- Content focus: Season-ending events, award season, year-in-review content

### December
- Limited PGA Tour schedule
- Q-School finals
- Content focus: Offseason features, year-end lists, Q-School drama, equipment previews for next season

## Tier 1 Events (Always Maximum Coverage)

| Event | Typical Month | Notes |
|-------|--------------|-------|
| The Masters | April | Green jacket, Augusta National, Par 3 contest, Amen Corner |
| PGA Championship | May | Often the "forgotten major" — give it full energy |
| US Open | June | USGA setup, hardest test in golf |
| The Open Championship | July | Links golf, Claret Jug, weather drama |
| The Players Championship | March | Unofficial 5th major, island green 17th |
| Ryder Cup | September (odd years) | Team event, USA vs Europe, electric atmosphere |
| Presidents Cup | September (even years) | USA vs International (non-Europe) |
| FedEx Cup Final | September | Season champion crowned |

## Current Context (March 2026)

- PGA-LIV merger situation still evolving — cover both tours fairly
- PGA Tour Signature Events format in place (elevated fields, no cuts in some events)
- LIV Golf running 54-hole team format with shotgun starts
- LPGA Tour gaining mainstream momentum — Nelly Korda's dominance a major storyline
