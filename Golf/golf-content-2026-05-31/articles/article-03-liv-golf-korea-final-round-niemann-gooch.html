LIV Golf Korea Final Round: Niemann and Gooch Co-Lead in Busan's Inaugural LIV Event

By Ryan Calloway | Golf Fanrecap | May 31, 2026

South Korea's debut on the LIV Golf calendar reaches its conclusion on Sunday at Asiad Country Club in Busan, and the final round couldn't offer a more compelling headline match: Joaquin Niemann and Talor Gooch are tied at nine under par, two proven LIV Golf champions locked in a winner-take-all chase at a venue that has welcomed professional golf for the first time on this international stage.

Two Champions, One Prize

Niemann brings the fan following and ballstriking brilliance that have made him one of LIV Golf's signature attractions. The Chilean talent has won on the LIV circuit before and arrived in Busan among the tournament favorites. He has played measured, disciplined golf throughout the week, holding his position through the shifts and momentum swings that LIV Golf's no-cut, 54-hole format creates.

Gooch, the former FedEx Cup champion who has built a second chapter of his career in LIV Golf, is the co-leader alongside him. Gooch held the Round 2 lead and demonstrated his capacity to manage the pressure of leading. His precision and course management throughout the week have been textbook for a player with his experience. Niemann's Crushers GC lead the team standings entering Sunday, adding a second dimension of competition to the day's drama.

DeChambeau in the Mix

Bryson DeChambeau shared the first-round lead alongside Charles Howell III and Harold Varner III, and while he has given ground at the top of the individual standings, he remains the kind of finisher that leaderboards should fear. His combination of distance and improving course management makes him a viable contender for any final-round run.

What's Next

The LIV Golf Korea final round is underway Sunday at Asiad Country Club in Busan. Whoever wins today becomes the inaugural champion at this new LIV destination — a title that carries its own distinctive prestige as the tour continues to build its global footprint across Asia and beyond. Full results are available at livgolf.com as the tournament concludes. The next LIV Golf event will continue the second half of the 2026 season schedule.
