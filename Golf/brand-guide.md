# Golf Fanrecap — Brand Guide

## Brand Colors

| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|
| Primary | Masters Green | #006747 | Headers, primary buttons, brand identity |
| Secondary | Fairway Blue | #1565C0 | Links, secondary elements, accent backgrounds |
| Accent | Pin Flag Red | #D32F2F | Alerts, CTAs, highlights, breaking news badges |
| Dark | Midnight | #1A1A2E | Body text, dark backgrounds, footer |
| White | White | #FFFFFF | Backgrounds, text on dark, clean space |

## Color Pairing Rules

- **Primary + White**: Default combination for headers and branded elements
- **Dark + White**: Body text and long-form content
- **Accent (Red)**: Use sparingly — breaking news, major championship alerts, critical CTAs only
- **Secondary (Blue)**: Links, hover states, secondary information
- **Never**: Red text on green background (accessibility), blue on dark without sufficient contrast

## Logo Usage

- Brand name: "Golf Fanrecap" (two words, capital G and F)
- Short form: "GFR" (for file naming, internal references, dashboard subfolder)
- Always pair with FanGear HQ parent branding where applicable
