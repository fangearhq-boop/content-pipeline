### STORY 3: LIV Golf Financial Crisis — Four Events Hang in the Balance
- **Tier:** 1
- **Status:** NEW
- **Summary:** LIV Golf's financial situation has reached a critical point. Saudi Arabia's Public Investment Fund (PIF) has delivered only approximately $200 million of the $600 million LIV Golf needs to complete its 2026 season. The tour is operating on PIF loans — a June debenture agreement listed PIF as LIV's lender. Multiple insiders believe PIF could cut monthly payments before LIV completes its four remaining events, starting July 23 with LIV Golf UK at JCB Golf and Country Club in Rocester. LIV has approached hundreds of investors and hired US investment banking firm Ducera Partners to search for alternatives.
- **Posting window:** 1:00 PM, 3:30 PM
