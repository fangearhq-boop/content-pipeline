## Editorial Note

Monday's playoff drama is settled: Viktor Hovland defeated Scottie Scheffler in sudden death at TPC River Highlands to win the 2026 Travelers Championship — his first PGA Tour victory in 15 months and first as a Norwegian at this event. The win snaps a remarkable Scheffler near-miss and hands Portland's best-in-class challenger a signature summer result. Meanwhile, the 154th Open Championship at Royal Birkdale is taking shape today with Final Qualifying happening at venues across the UK, including Notts (Hollinwell) and St Annes Old Links. The John Deere Classic tees off Thursday July 2 at TPC Deere Run in Silvis, Illinois — a quality field that includes Jordan Spieth, Rickie Fowler, and world No. 13 Chris Gotterup. Haeran Ryu's KPMG Women's PGA title from Sunday is still being celebrated across the LPGA.
