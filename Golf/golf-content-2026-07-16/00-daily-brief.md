- **Tier:** 1
- **Status:** FOLLOW UP
- **Summary:** Tommy Fleetwood is one of the most compelling storylines at Royal Birkdale. ### STORY 4: LIV Golf Running on Loans to Complete 2026 Season
- **Tier:** 2
- **Status:** FOLLOW UP
- **Summary:** LIV Golf is using loans — not capital investment — to fund the completion of its 2026 season. The Public Investment Fund filed a debenture on June 4, securing a loan against LIV Golf's assets. PIF has provided approximately $200 million of the roughly $600 million it committed to fund the remainder of the campaign. LIV Golf spends about $100 million per month. Four events remain: UK (July 23-26), New York, Indianapolis, and the Team Championship Michigan. All four events are reportedly uncertain without new funding.
- **Posting window:** 6:00 PM
