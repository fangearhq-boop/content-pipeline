# Golf Story Analysis — 2026-04-16

### STORY 1: RBC Heritage Round 1 in Play

**Primary Angle:** The first post-Masters PGA Tour event gives us an immediate look at who can bounce back — especially Scheffler, who came one shot short of a third Masters title.

**Headline Options:**
- "Post-Masters Golf Returns: Scheffler Headline Act at RBC Heritage Round 1"
- "Back to Work: Harbour Town Opens as PGA Tour's Post-Augusta Chapter Begins"

**Hook:** Augusta National just wrapped. Harbour Town just opened. The best players in the world have 48 hours to shake off the emotional weight of the Masters and refocus on a lighthouse and a par-71 that rewards precision over power.

**What's Next:** Round 2 Friday, Round 3 Saturday, Final Round Sunday. Scheffler won here in 2024 and is built for this course.

### STORY 3: McIlroy Masters Legacy

**Primary Angle:** Back-to-back Masters victories are the rarest achievement in golf. They require peak performance sustained across two consecutive Aprils. McIlroy has done it, and the meaning of that runs deep.

**Headline Options:**
- "The Company He Keeps: McIlroy's Back-to-Back Masters Win Puts Him With Nicklaus, Faldo, and Tiger"
- "More Than a Win: What Rory McIlroy's Second Straight Green Jacket Really Means"

**Hook:** The first time Rory McIlroy won the Masters, in 2025, it ended the longest, most painful major drought in modern golf history. The second time, in 2026, it proved something different: that he belongs in a category occupied by only three men in the history of Augusta National.

**What's Next:** McIlroy is not at this week's RBC Heritage — resting before what will be a busy summer of majors.

### STORY 5: The Case for Russell Henley

**Primary Angle:** Henley has everything you want in a contender at Harbour Town: elite approach play, a course record that borders on absurd (67.42 average over 12 rounds), a recent win at this very event, and post-Masters momentum that comes from playing some of the best golf of his career.

**Headline Options:**
- "The Smart Money at Harbour Town: Why Russell Henley Is the RBC Heritage's Strongest Story"
- "Henley's Moment: T3 at the Masters, Elite Course History, and a Win Just Waiting to Happen"

**Hook:** The number that defines Russell Henley at Harbour Town is 67.42. That is his stroke average over 12 trips to this golf course. For context, that means he arrives each year and the course gives him a nearly automatic score in the high 60s. He just came off the best major finish of his career. And Scheffler has a reputation for being human in the week after Augusta.

**What's Next:** Henley's trajectory this week and whether he holds up in the final round.
