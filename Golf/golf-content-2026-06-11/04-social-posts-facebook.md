### STORY 2: Dow LPGA Championship

#### Long-Form Post — 10:30 AM ET

Golf's most unique team event is back.

The 2026 Dow Championship teed off today at Midland Country Club in Midland, Michigan, bringing together 72 two-player teams for one of the LPGA Tour's most celebrated events. The tournament runs through Sunday with a total purse of $3.3 million.

The format is what sets the Dow Championship apart from every other event on the women's professional golf calendar: best-ball, where each team takes the lower of the two players' scores on every hole. It rewards aggressive play, rewards partnership, and creates moments of celebration that a standard stroke-play event simply cannot generate. When your partner makes an eagle and you're already at birdie, the whole dynamic of the round changes.

With 144 players competing as 72 teams, the field includes some of the most recognizable names in women's golf. The event has produced memorable winners throughout its history and consistently delivers the highest crowd engagement of any tournament in Midland's summer season.
