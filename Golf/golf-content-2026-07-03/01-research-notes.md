## Viktor Hovland — Travelers Championship Win

### Result
- Won Travelers Championship on June 29 (Monday finish — playoff after Sunday rain/darkness)
- Defeated Scottie Scheffler in one-hole playoff
- Final score: -21 (65-61-64-69 at TPC River Highlands, Cromwell, CT)
- 8th career PGA Tour victory
- First win since 2025 Valspar Championship
- Prize: $3.6 million + 700 FedExCup points

### Playoff Drama
- Sunday night, near-darkness: Scheffler made an 8-foot par putt to force the playoff
- Playoff hole 1: Scheffler missed a 4-foot birdie putt — Hovland won

### Personal Milestone
- Hovland's mother Galina was watching from behind the 18th green
- This was the first PGA Tour win she witnessed in person
- Hovland is now in the John Deere Classic field this week
- No back-to-back Deere/Travelers winner documented in recent years
