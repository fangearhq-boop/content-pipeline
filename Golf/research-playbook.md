# Golf Fanrecap — Research Playbook

How to research, tier, and fact-check golf stories for daily content.

## Research Categories

Run **8-10 web searches** across these categories before writing content.

| # | Category | Example Search Queries |
|---|----------|----------------------|
| 1 | Tournament Results & Leaderboard | "PGA Tour leaderboard today", "LIV Golf results", "[tournament name] scores round 2", "[player] scorecard today" |
| 2 | World Rankings | "OWGR rankings this week", "FedEx Cup standings", "Race to Dubai standings", "LPGA Race to CME Globe" |
| 3 | Player Performances | "lowest round PGA Tour today", "eagle hole-in-one PGA Tour", "[player] course record", "strokes gained leaders this week" |
| 4 | PGA/LIV/LPGA/DP World News | "PGA LIV merger update", "PGA Tour schedule change", "LIV Golf news today", "LPGA Tour news", "DP World Tour news" |
| 5 | Equipment & Course Setup | "PGA Tour equipment change", "[tournament] course setup", "USGA course conditions", "[course name] yardage changes" |
| 6 | Injury & Withdrawals | "[player] withdrawal reason", "PGA Tour injury report", "[player] return timeline", "WD [tournament name]" |
| 7 | Majors & Big Events | "[major] field exemptions", "[major] course preview", "Masters past champions", "Ryder Cup picks", "[major] weather forecast" |
| 8 | Preview & Lookahead | "[tournament] tee times", "[tournament] featured groups", "[course] history winners", "[tournament] weather forecast", "PGA Tour next week" |

## Story Tiering

### Tier 1 — Lead Stories (top time slots)

Must-cover content that anchors the day. Gets the 8:00 AM or 10:30 AM slot.

| Story Type | Why Tier 1 |
|-----------|-----------|
| Major championship results | The four majors are the biggest events in golf — every round is Tier 1 during major weeks |
| Playoff / sudden-death finishes | Dramatic finishes are the most shareable content in golf |
| Course records | Historic rounds at iconic courses define careers |
| Major tour announcements | PGA-LIV merger news, schedule overhauls, purse changes that reshape the sport |
| First-time major winners | Career-defining moments with great human-interest angles |
| Ryder Cup / Presidents Cup results | Team golf generates the most passionate fan engagement |

### Tier 2 — Developing Stories (mid-day slots)

Strong content that fills the 1:00 PM or 3:30 PM slots.

| Story Type | Why Tier 2 |
|-----------|-----------|
| Weekly tournament results (non-major) | PGA Tour, LIV, LPGA, and DP World Tour event winners and final leaderboards |
| Notable rounds (62 or lower) | Exceptional individual performances worth spotlighting |
| World ranking shifts | Moves into or out of top 10, major championship exemption implications |
| Injury news (star players) | Tiger, Scheffler, McIlroy, Korda — health updates on the biggest names matter |
| FedEx Cup / Race to Dubai movement | Standings shifts with playoff or season-ending implications |
| Ryder Cup / Presidents Cup qualifying | Team selection drama and captain's picks |

### Tier 3 — Supporting Content (later slots)

Fills out the schedule. Gets the 6:00 PM or 8:30 PM slot.

| Story Type | Why Tier 3 |
|-----------|-----------|
| Tournament previews | Course history, past winners, tee times, featured groups, weather |
| Course guides and history | Signature holes, course architecture, tournament traditions |
| Historical comparisons | "Best Masters performances ever", "youngest major winners" |
| Equipment talk | Club changes, ball regulations, gear controversies |
| Rules of Golf changes | USGA/R&A updates, pace-of-play initiatives |
| Evergreen features | Player profiles, caddie stories, course beauty |

## Posting Priority

This order is non-negotiable:

1. **Round results and leaderboard updates** — always first. Especially Sunday final round results. A round recap after the next round starts is useless.
2. **Today's tee times and featured groups** — what to watch today, key pairings, the leaders' tee times.
3. **Breaking news** — PGA-LIV merger updates, major withdrawals, tour announcements.
4. **Everything else** — analysis, previews, historical content, equipment talk.

If multiple tournament results exist (PGA Tour + LIV + LPGA same weekend), order by tier: major > PGA Tour signature event > regular PGA Tour > LIV > LPGA > DP World Tour. But always cover all tours — don't skip LPGA or DP World Tour results.

## Fact-Check Requirements

Every post must be verified before publishing. Priority order:

### Priority 1 — Tee Times, Dates, Courses

| Check | How to Verify |
|-------|--------------|
| Tee times | Verify against pgatour.com or livgolf.com official tee times — always convert to ET |
| Tournament dates | Cross-reference official tour schedule — dates shift year to year |
| Course name | Verify exact name (TPC Sawgrass, not "Sawgrass"; Augusta National Golf Club, not "Augusta") |
| Day of week | Cross-reference the calendar — "Saturday's third round" must actually be Saturday |
| Tournament name | Use official name — "The Players Championship" not "The Players" on first mention |

### Priority 2 — Scores, Leaderboard Positions, Rankings

| Check | How to Verify |
|-------|--------------|
| Round scores | Verify against official leaderboard at pgatour.com, livgolf.com, or lpga.com |
| Leaderboard position | Confirm current position including ties (T3, not 3rd if tied) |
| Score relative to par | Calculate: course par minus strokes = relative to par. Verify, don't assume |
| World rankings | OWGR.com — updated Monday after each tournament week |
| FedEx Cup standings | pgatour.com/fedexcup — changes weekly |
| Cut line | Verify from official results — cut line varies by tournament |

### Priority 3 — Player Stats, Career Records, Prize Money

| Check | How to Verify |
|-------|--------------|
| Career wins | pgatour.com player profile or Golf Channel career stats |
| Major championship wins | Cross-reference multiple sources — major wins are career-defining claims |
| Strokes gained stats | DataGolf.com or PGA Tour ShotLink — specify the source |
| Prize money | Verify against official purse breakdown on tour site — never estimate |
| Player ages | Look up birth date from tour profile, then calculate — never trust a secondary source's stated age |
| Course records | Verify against tournament history on tour site — records can be course-specific or tournament-specific |

## Coverage Bias Check

Before finalizing any day's content, verify coverage breadth:

- Do not default to PGA Tour coverage only — LIV, LPGA, and DP World Tour deserve real coverage
- Women's golf produces Tier 1 stories — Nelly Korda's dominance, LPGA major championships
- International players are a huge part of golf — cover non-American storylines proportionally
- If 4+ posts are about the same player or tour, look harder at other storylines
- During major weeks, it's OK to go all-in on the major — but still mention what else happened

## Key Dates Reminder

- **The Masters**: First full week of April (practice rounds Mon-Wed, competition Thu-Sun)
- **PGA Championship**: Third week of May
- **US Open**: Third week of June
- **The Open Championship**: Third week of July
- **The Players Championship**: Second week of March
- **FedEx Cup Playoffs**: August (3 events)
- **Tour Championship**: Labor Day weekend (September)
- **Ryder Cup**: Late September (odd years)
- **Presidents Cup**: Late September (even years)
