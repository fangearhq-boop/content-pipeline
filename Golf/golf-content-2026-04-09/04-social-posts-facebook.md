Jon Rahm, the 2023 Masters champion, is also in the field and tees off at 1:08 PM ET.

The rest of the LIV roster is in mixed form at best. The shrinking presence is a data point that Augusta's traditional gatekeeping still matters — and that the pathway from LIV to a major start is getting narrower.
